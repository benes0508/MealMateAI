Ingredients:
For the base:

1 frozen banana

1 cup frozen mixed berries (strawberries, blueberries, raspberries, etc.)

½ cup Greek yogurt (or plant-based alternative)

¼ cup unsweetened almond milk (adjust for consistency)

1 tsp honey or maple syrup (optional)

Toppings (customize as you like):

Fresh berries

Sliced banana

Granola

Chia seeds

Coconut flakes

Nut butter drizzle (e.g., peanut or almond butter)

A few mint leaves (optional)

Instructions:
Add all base ingredients to a blender.

Blend until smooth and thick. If needed, add a splash more almond milk to help blend—but keep it thick enough to eat with a spoon.

Pour into a bowl.

Add your favorite toppings.

Enjoy immediately!
