Classic Bagel with Cream Cheese
Ingredients:
1 fresh bagel (plain, everything, sesame, or your favorite flavor)

2–3 tbsp cream cheese (plain or flavored)

Optional toppings:

Sliced tomato

Red onion (thinly sliced)

Capers

Smoked salmon (lox)

Cucumber slices

Fresh herbs (like dill or chives)

Avocado

Instructions:
Slice the bagel in half horizontally.

Toast both halves to your desired level of crispiness.

Spread an even layer of cream cheese on the cut sides.

Optional: Add any extra toppings like smoked salmon, sliced tomato, or cucumber for a more deluxe version.

Serve immediately — open-faced or as a sandwich.
