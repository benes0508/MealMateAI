This recipe for "Not-Quite-Bonnie's Rugelach" outlines how to make a classic cream cheese pastry filled with a combination of roasted walnuts, brown sugar, and cinnamon, along with a layer of softened quince paste with lemon juice. The dough is prepared with butter and cream cheese, then rolled out, filled, cut into triangles, and rolled into crescent shapes. After chilling, they are brushed with an egg wash and demerara sugar, then baked until golden brown. The recipe also includes tips for making components ahead of time and proper storage to maintain texture.
