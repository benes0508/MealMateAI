This recipe details how to make a rustic **Rosemary Olive Oil Bread**, featuring a dough made with flour, yeast, salt, olive oil, and fresh rosemary. The process involves mixing ingredients in a food processor, kneading in the rosemary by hand, two stages of rising, shaping into a boule, and baking in a hot oven with steam to achieve a crisp crust. The recipe also provides versatile variations, allowing for additions like olives, dried tomatoes, onions and mint, or pancetta and black pepper.
