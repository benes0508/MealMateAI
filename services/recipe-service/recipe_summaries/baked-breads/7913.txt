**Scrambled Eggs with Leeks, Fava Beans, Crispy Breadcrumbs, and Parmesan**

A sophisticated scrambled egg dish featuring creamy eggs (whisked with crème fraîche or sour cream) cooked with tender sautéed leeks and fava beans. The dish is topped with homemade crispy French breadcrumbs and grated Parmesan for added texture and savory flavor.
