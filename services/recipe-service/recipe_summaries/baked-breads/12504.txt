This recipe details how to make Caesar Salad from scratch, including homemade croutons and a balsamic-based dressing. It involves baking seasoned bread cubes for croutons and blending Parmesan, mayonnaise, anchovies, balsamic vinegar, lemon juice, Dijon, Worcestershire, and garlic for the dressing. The salad is assembled by tossing torn romaine and croutons with the dressing.
