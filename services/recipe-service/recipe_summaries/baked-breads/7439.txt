This recipe for Lemon Soufflé Tartlets with Sugared Almonds and Blackberry Sauce is a sophisticated, multi-step dessert. It features tangy lemon curd lightened with egg whites to create a puffed soufflé filling baked in individual buttery tartlet crusts. The tartlets are topped with crisp sugared almonds and served with a vibrant homemade blackberry sauce, offering a delightful blend of sweet, tart, and varied textures.
