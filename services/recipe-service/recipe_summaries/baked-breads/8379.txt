These Adobo-Glazed Mini Turkey Loaves are baked in muffin tins, yielding 7 individual portions. Made with a blend of ground dark and breast turkey, they incorporate mixed vegetables like broccoli, red bell pepper, and peas. The loaves are finished with a savory-sweet adobo, honey, and lime glaze. This recipe is suitable for meal prepping, with instructions for freezing and reheating.
