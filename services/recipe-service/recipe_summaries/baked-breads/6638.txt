This recipe details how to make **Chicken Liver Mousse with Riesling-Thyme Gelée**. It involves mellowing chicken livers in milk, then sautéing them with shallots and thyme, deglazing with Calvados, and blending with butter into a silky-smooth mousse. A sweet Riesling and gelatin gelée is prepared and poured over the chilled mousse. The dish is served with crisp, oven-baked toast triangles.
