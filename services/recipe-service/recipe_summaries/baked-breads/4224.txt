This recipe describes a Spiced Custard Pie with a distinctive Sesame Crust. It features a rich, creamy custard infused with aromatic ginger, cinnamon, and cloves, poured into a unique butter-based crust with raw sesame seeds. The instructions include detailed steps for blind-baking the crust, tempering egg yolks for a smooth custard, and an optional finish of torching sugar on top for a caramelized crust.
