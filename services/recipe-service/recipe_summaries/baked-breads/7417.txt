This recipe is for **Gluten-Free Buttermilk Biscuits**, made with sorghum and tapioca flours, cornstarch, guar gum, and leavened with baking powder. Cold butter and vegetable shortening are cut into the dry ingredients, then buttermilk is added to form a dough. The dough is shaped into 1-inch thick disks, cut with a biscuit cutter, and baked at 425Â°F for 12-15 minutes until golden. Serve hot with butter, agave syrup, or preserves.
