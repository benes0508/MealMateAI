This recipe is for an **Apple and Calvados Tart (Galette de Pommes au Calvados)**. It's a rustic, open-faced tart featuring sliced apples tossed with lemon juice and sugar, spread over a layer of calvados applesauce on an all-butter pastry. The edges of the dough are folded over the filling. It's baked until golden, then brushed with melted apple jelly and served with a **Calvados-infused whipped cream**. Key ingredients include apples, calvados, apple jelly, and a homemade pastry.
