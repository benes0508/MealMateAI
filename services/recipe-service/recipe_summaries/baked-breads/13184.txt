This recipe for Dried Tomato and Fennel Stuffing features toasted crusty bread combined with sautéed fennel, simmered dried tomatoes in chicken broth, balsamic vinegar, and herbs. The mixture is then baked. The recipe includes crucial food safety instructions for stuffing poultry, advising against stuffing the night before and emphasizing internal temperature checks for both the bird (180°F) and the stuffing (160-165°F).
