This recipe details how to make "Pumpkin Bread Pudding with Spicy Caramel Apple Sauce." It involves toasting cubed pumpkin bread, then soaking it in a custard made from cream, milk, vanilla, egg yolks, sugar, maple syrup, pumpkin puree, and bourbon. The pudding is baked in a water bath until set and served warm with whipped cream and a spicy caramel apple sauce.
