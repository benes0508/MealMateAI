This recipe is for "Boneless Buffalo Chicken 'Wings'" which are deep-fried chicken breast strips. The chicken is first marinated in buttermilk and hot sauce, then double-dredged in a seasoned flour mixture before being deep-fried until crispy. The fried chicken is then tossed in a warm buffalo sauce made with butter, hot sauce, and maple syrup. It's served with a homemade blue cheese dip and celery sticks.
