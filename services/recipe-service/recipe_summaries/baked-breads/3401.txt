Here's a concise summary of each recipe:

**Butternut Squash Vegducken**
This elaborate vegetarian main features hollowed butternut squash, eggplant, and zucchini layers, each stuffed with a finely chopped mixture of their own pulp, mushrooms, shallots, garlic, Parmesan, and breadcrumbs, all brushed with maple syrup butter. Tied and baked, it's served sliced with a fresh mint-lemon herb butter.

**Crispy Chicken Sandwich with Buttermilk Slaw and Herbed Mayo**
Chicken cutlets are brined in buttermilk and hot sauce, then double-dredged and deep-fried until crispy. These are served on buttered, toasted potato rolls with a tangy buttermilk-mayonnaise slaw and a flavorful herbed mayonnaise.

**Sole Meuniere with Rice Pilaf**
Sole fillets are lightly floured, pan-fried in clarified butter, and basted with brown butter. It's served with a rich brown butter, lemon, and cream sauce alongside an oven-baked rice pilaf.

**Barley-Stuffed Peppers**
Bell pepper halves are filled with a savory mixture of cooked barley, sautéed cremini mushrooms, Parmesan, mozzarella, and parsley. They are then baked until tender and hot, finished with a golden-brown breadcrumb topping.

**Rib Eye With Bone Marrow and Radish Salad**
A thick bone-in rib eye steak is pan-seared and roasted, served with rich roasted bone marrow (infused with anchovy and capers). The dish is complemented by a crisp, fresh salad of radishes, celery leaves, and parsley with a lemon-anchovy dressing.

**D.I.Y. Apple Mille-Feuille**
This dessert features individual layers of crispy baked puff pastry. Guests assemble their own mille-feuille with tender, bourbon-maple baked pink lady apples and a light, bourbon-spiked whipped cream.

**Sour Cream Biscuits With Sausage Gravy**
Fluffy, tender biscuits made with sour cream are baked golden brown. They are served alongside a classic, hearty white gravy made from browned breakfast sausage, a roux, and milk.

**Chicken Thigh Potpie**
Pan-fried chicken thighs and roasted root vegetables (rutabaga, sweet potato, pearl onions, mushrooms, kale) are combined in a rich gravy. This hearty filling is topped with a partially pre-baked cornmeal crust and baked until bubbly.

**Vegan Pumpkin Pie**
A vegan pie crust is blind-baked and filled with a creamy, spiced pumpkin and silken tofu blend. The pie is baked until set and then chilled for a satisfying plant-based dessert.

**Onion Tart**
A simple pastry crust is blind-baked and then filled with a rich mixture of slowly sautéed brown onions, egg yolks, and cream, seasoned with nutmeg. The tart is baked until the filling is set.

**BBQ Chicken French Bread Pizzas with Smoked Mozzarella**
Quick pizzas made on split French bread, topped with a base of BBQ sauce, tender caramelized onions, shredded rotisserie chicken mixed with more BBQ sauce, and smoked mozzarella, then baked until bubbly.

**French Bread Pizzas With Ricotta, Roasted Tomatoes, and Pesto**
French bread halves are lightly baked and rubbed with garlic, then topped with creamy ricotta, blistered roasted cherry tomatoes, and Parmesan cheese. After baking, they are drizzled with basil pesto.

**French Bread Pizzas with Mozzarella and Pepperoni**
Easy French bread pizzas start with lightly baked, garlic-rubbed bread, topped with tomato sauce, mozzarella, and pepperoni. They are baked until the cheese is melted and golden, then garnished with fresh basil.

**Fancy Grilled Cheese With A Fried Egg**
A gourmet grilled cheese sandwich featuring cranberry walnut or country bread and Raclette or Gruyere cheese. It's griddled until golden and melted, then topped with a sunny-side-up fried egg and a drizzle of truffle honey.

**Sheet-Pan Crispy Fish Tacos with Chili-Roasted Corn**
Mild fish fillets are breaded in a pumpkin seed-panko crust and baked on a sheet pan alongside corn cobs seasoned with chili-mayo. Served in warm tortillas with various fresh toppings for build-your-own tacos.

**Wild Rice-Crusted Halibut**
Halibut fillets are coated in a unique crust made from popped and ground wild rice. The fish is pan-fried until golden, then finished in the oven, resulting in a tender, flaky interior with a crispy exterior.

**Apple Crumble with Calvados and Créme Fraîche Ice Cream**
This dessert features a boozy twist: baked Granny Smith apples infused with Calvados are folded into a creamy crème fraîche ice cream, along with pieces of a sweet, spiced crumble.

**Tomato-Cheddar Pie**
A savory pie with a homemade crust, layered with drained heirloom tomato slices and a rich filling of sharp cheddar, mayonnaise, herbs, and sautéed Vidalia onion. It's baked until golden and set.

**Tomato Soup with Arugula, Croutons, and Pecorino**
A comforting, smooth tomato soup made with tomatoes, onion, garlic, and thyme, finished with hot sauce. It's served garnished with crunchy homemade croutons, fresh arugula, and shaved Pecorino cheese.

**Chicken-Fried Steak**
Tenderized top round steaks are double-dredged in a seasoned flour and buttermilk mixture, creating a crispy, flavorful crust. The breaded steaks are then deep-fried until golden brown.

**Texas Toast with Roasted Tomatoes and Parsley**
Thick slices of white bread are transformed into Texas toast by brushing them with warm garlic-parsley butter and topping them with tender, gently smashed broiled cherry tomatoes.

**Ham, Gruyère, and Tomato Toast with Dijon Mustard**
Toasted sourdough bread is spread with Dijon mustard, then topped with slices of smoked ham, fresh tomato, and grated Gruyère cheese, broiled until bubbly and golden.

**Lime Cornmeal Bars with Blueberry Glaze**
A sweet-tart dessert featuring a buttery cornmeal crust, topped with a vibrant lime custard filling. Once cooled, it's finished with a smooth, sweet blueberry glaze and cut into bars.

**Blueberry-Pecan Galette**
A rustic, free-form pie featuring a buttery crust enriched with ground pecans. It's generously filled with a simple mixture of fresh blueberries, lemon juice, and sugar, then baked until the fruit is bubbling and the crust is golden.

**The Easiest Peach-Raspberry Pie with Press-In Crust**
This simple pie uses an easy press-in butter crust, with some reserved for a crumb topping. It's filled generously with sweet peaches and tart raspberries, baked until bubbly, and then cooled to set.
