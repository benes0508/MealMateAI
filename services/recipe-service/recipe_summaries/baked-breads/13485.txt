This recipe details how to prepare Chestnut, Onion, and Currant Stuffing. It involves toasting seven-grain bread, sautéing onions in butter, simmering chestnuts in chicken broth with currants, then combining these with herbs, more broth, and seasoning. The mixture is baked at 325°F for 45 minutes, with an option to bake covered for moistness or uncovered for a crisp top. The recipe also includes important food safety guidelines for stuffing poultry.
