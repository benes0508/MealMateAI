This recipe details a One-Hour Apricot and Almond Galette. It features a puff pastry base spread with a sweet almond cream made from ground almonds, sugar, egg, butter, and extracts, then topped with halved apricots. The galette is baked in two stages: initially at 425°F for 15-20 minutes, then at 325°F for another 15-20 minutes until golden and cooked through.
