This recipe for Pork-Belly Buns features tender, brined and roasted pork belly served in fluffy, homemade steamed buns. The pork is first brined, then slow-roasted and finished at a higher temperature until golden. The yeast-leavened buns are prepared from scratch, proofed, and steamed. The dish is assembled by filling the warm buns with sliced pork, hoisin sauce, fresh cucumber, and scallions.
