This recipe details a **Creole shrimp casserole** topped with a vibrant **"Confetti Corn Bread" crust**. The filling consists of shrimp, onion, celery, green bell peppers, garlic, diced tomatoes, tomato sauce, and chicken broth, all seasoned with Creole spice. The topping is a rich corn bread batter incorporating butter, eggs, green chiles, corn kernels, creamed corn, and red and green bell peppers. The dish is assembled by spooning the corn bread batter over the prepared shrimp filling and then baked until golden and bubbling.
