This recipe is for **Hoisin-Glazed Meatloaf**, featuring two loaves made from a mixture of ground beef, Italian sausage, panko breadcrumbs, finely chopped carrots, onion, and an egg, seasoned with various herbs and spices. The meatloaves are generously coated with a savory-sweet glaze of hoisin sauce, ketchup, and hot sauce, then baked until cooked through.
