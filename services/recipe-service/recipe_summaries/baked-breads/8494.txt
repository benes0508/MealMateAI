This recipe details how to make a Potato-Crusted Goat Cheese Tart served with an Heirloom Tomato Salad. It involves preparing a béchamel sauce and caramelized onions for the filling, par-frying potato slices to create a crispy crust in individual tart pans, then layering the onions, béchamel, and goat cheese. The tarts are baked until golden and served alongside a fresh Heirloom Tomato Salad.
