This recipe details a Rustic Fig, Serrano Ham & Hazelnut Tartine, featuring a gluten-free crust made from brown rice, millet, and hazelnut flours. The crust is baked until golden, then topped with creamy goat cheese, fresh figs, serrano ham, watercress, thinly sliced red onion, and toasted hazelnuts, finished with a drizzle of olive oil and balsamic vinegar.
