This recipe provides instructions for making Golden Yellow Corn Bread or Corn Muffins. It involves combining yellow cornmeal, flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt with a mixture of oil, egg, and milk. The batter can be baked in a 9"x9" pan at 425°F for 20-25 minutes, or as 12 muffins for 15-20 minutes. A variation for a sweeter, moister version is also included, adjusting ingredient quantities and baking at 400°F.
