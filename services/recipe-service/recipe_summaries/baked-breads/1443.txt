This recipe is for a savory Parmesan Bread Pudding featuring broccoli rabe and pancetta. It involves sautéing broccoli rabe with garlic and red pepper flakes, then combining it with a custard made from eggs, milk, bread, and Parmesan cheese. The mixture is baked with additional pancetta and Parmesan on top until puffed and golden, taking 45-55 minutes at 350°F.
