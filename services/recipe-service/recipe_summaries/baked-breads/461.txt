This recipe details a Pecan-Rye Pumpkin Pie, featuring a unique crust made with rye flour for a distinctive flavor, pre-baked and egg-washed to prevent sogginess. The creamy pumpkin filling is flavored with rye whiskey and an array of warming spices like cinnamon, ginger, and nutmeg. It's topped with a crunchy pecan mixture coated in brown sugar, maple syrup, and egg white, baked until crisp.
