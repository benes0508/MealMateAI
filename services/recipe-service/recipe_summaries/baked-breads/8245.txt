This recipe details how to make a moist Lemon or Orange Loaf Cake. It combines sifted cake flour, sugar infused with citrus zest, eggs, crème fraîche, and melted butter, with a choice of rum or Grand Marnier for flavor. The batter is baked in a loaf pan at 350°F (180°C) for approximately 1.5 hours until cooked through and golden.
