This recipe is for **Dill-Cured Pork Crostini with Sweet Mustard Sauce**. It involves curing pork tenderloins with a salt, sugar, garlic, and dill rub, then roasting and chilling them. A sweet mustard sauce with dill is prepared. Finally, the thinly sliced cured pork is served on sourdough baguette rounds, topped with the sweet mustard sauce.
