This recipe for "The Loftiest Soufflé" guides you through making a savory, cheesy soufflé. It involves preparing ramekins, creating a béchamel sauce enriched with Parmesan and Gruyère, beating egg whites, then gently folding egg yolks into the béchamel before incorporating the egg whites. The soufflés are baked until risen and golden brown, and served immediately.
