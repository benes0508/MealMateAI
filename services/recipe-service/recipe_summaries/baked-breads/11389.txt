This recipe describes how to make a **Pear-Cranberry Mincemeat Lattice Pie**. The filling, a traditional mincemeat, is made with finely chopped Anjou pears, dried sour cherries, currants, and cranberries, sweetened with brown sugar and flavored with brandy, citrus zests, cinnamon, allspice, nutmeg, and chopped walnuts. This mixture is prepared and chilled for at least 3 days to meld flavors. The pie is assembled with a double pastry crust, featuring a decorative lattice top, and baked at 425°F then 375°F until golden brown and bubbly.
