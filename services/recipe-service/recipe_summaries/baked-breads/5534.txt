This recipe details "Cabbage Rolls with Meat Stuffing and Wild Mushroom Sauce." It involves parboiling savoy cabbage leaves, filling them with a savory mixture of finely chopped pork or chicken, cooked white rice, and onion, then baking them in chicken broth. A separate, rich wild mushroom sauce is prepared using a combination of rehydrated dried mushrooms and fresh mushrooms, thickened with flour, and flavored with white wine, heavy cream, and lemon juice. The sauce is served poured over the baked cabbage rolls.
