This recipe details **Cherry-Chocolate Shortcakes with Kirsch Whipped Cream**. It involves baking **semisweet chocolate shortcakes**. Fresh **Bing cherries are marinated in sugar and kirsch** for several hours, with their juices reduced into a thick syrup that's mixed back with the fruit. **Kirsch-flavored whipped cream** is also prepared. The warm shortcakes are then split and layered with the cherry mixture and whipped cream.
