This recipe is for **Garlic Herb Bread Twists**, a savory snack or side dish. It involves rolling out pizza dough, filling it with a mixture of rosemary, thyme, and Parmesan cheese, then twisting the strips and baking them until golden. After baking, the twists are coated with a garlic-infused olive oil, parsley, and more Parmesan. They can be served warm or at room temperature.
