This recipe for "Piquitos De Enrique Dacosta—Enrique's Bread Bites" involves kneading pizza dough with salt, olive oil, and flour, then rolling it thin and cutting into shapes. The bites are brushed with olive oil, sprinkled with salt, baked at 375°F for 15-16 minutes until golden, and then left in the cooling oven for an additional 10 minutes to crisp.
