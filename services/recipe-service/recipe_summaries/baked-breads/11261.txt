This recipe details the preparation of Potato Latkes served with smoked salmon, caviar, and a flavorful tarragon crème fraîche. The latkes are made by shredding Yukon Gold potatoes and onion, mixing with eggs, flour, salt, and pepper, then frying until golden brown. A simple tarragon crème fraîche is prepared by combining crème fraîche and fresh tarragon. The dish is assembled by topping warm latkes with smoked salmon, a dollop of the tarragon crème fraîche, and a finishing touch of caviar.
