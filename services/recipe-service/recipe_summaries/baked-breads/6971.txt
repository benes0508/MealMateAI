This recipe details how to make an Indian Eggplant and Onion Dip served with homemade Pita Chips. First, pita wedges are brushed with olive oil and baked until crisp. Simultaneously, eggplant, onions, and garlic are roasted until tender. The roasted vegetables are then pureed with fresh mint, cilantro, and garam masala to create the dip, which is seasoned and served with the crispy pita chips.
