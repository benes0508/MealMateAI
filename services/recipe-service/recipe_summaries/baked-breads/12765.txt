This recipe details how to prepare Chicken Drumsticks with Muhammara Sauce. Chicken drumsticks are seasoned and baked until golden brown. Concurrently, a Muhammara sauce is made by blending roasted red peppers, walnuts, bread crumbs, garlic, pomegranate molasses, cumin, and cayenne in a food processor, then gradually incorporating olive oil. The sauce is seasoned with salt and drizzled over the baked chicken.
