This recipe creates a Nacho Macaroni and Cheese casserole. It involves cooking elbow macaroni, preparing a creamy cheese sauce with cheddar, milk, cream, and a hint of cloves, and blending salsa verde with cilantro. The dish is then layered in a baking dish with macaroni and cheese mixture, salsa verde, and cheese, topped with corn chips and more cheese, and baked until hot and bubbly.
