This recipe is for **chewy coconut and chocolate chip cookies**, featuring whole wheat flour, oats, shredded coconut, and semisweet chocolate chips. Key to their texture is chilling the dough and a unique mid-bake "cookie drop" technique to achieve a chewy center and crisp edges. The recipe also includes instructions for freezing dough for future baking. This recipe fits well within the **desserts-sweets** collection.
