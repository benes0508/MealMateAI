This recipe details a "Strawberry Pazzo Cake with Herbed Crème Fraîche," a unique dessert featuring a butter cake topped with fresh strawberries, which can be baked in an oven or on a grill. It includes a savory herbed crème fraîche (with rosemary or basil) prepared in advance, and a balsamic glaze with black pepper that's drizzled over the warm cake. The cooled cake is served with the herbed crème fraîche and additional balsamic glaze, optionally with roasted strawberries.
