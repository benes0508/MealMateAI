These **flourless almond cookies** are flavored with **cardamom**, **orange zest**, and **pistachios**. Made with **almond flour** and **virgin coconut oil**, the dough is **chilled**, **rolled into logs**, and **sliced** before baking. They can be garnished with extra pistachios or an optional **dark chocolate drizzle**. A great **gluten-free** dessert option that can be **made ahead**.
