This recipe for Cauliflower-Carrot Cheesy Tots details how to make homemade tater tots from finely processed carrots and cauliflower. The vegetables are combined with Parmesan and sharp cheddar cheeses, garlic, chives, and panko breadcrumbs, then shaped into tots. After chilling and breading, they are baked (or optionally pan-fried) until golden brown and crispy, and served with ketchup.
