This recipe details how to make a Roasted Portobello and Prosciutto Lasagna. It involves roasting portobello mushrooms, sautéing prosciutto with shallots and herbs, and preparing a rich béchamel sauce with Gruyère and Parmesan cheeses. Layers of lasagna noodles, the mushroom-prosciutto mixture, and sauce are assembled, then baked until golden and bubbling. The dish can be prepared ahead of time.
