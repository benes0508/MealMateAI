This recipe is for a large **Chocolate-Pecan Sheet Pie with Molasses**. It features a homemade **pecan-infused crust**, a rich filling made with **browned butter, molasses, and corn syrup**, and is topped with a smooth **chocolate ganache** and **candied pecans**. The process involves preparing and pre-baking the crust, making and baking the filling, then topping with a homemade ganache and toasted, sugar-coated pecans.
