This recipe prepares **Baked Falafel with Orange-Tahini Sauce**.

It involves:
1.  Quick-pickling red onions.
2.  Pulsing chickpeas with herbs and spices to form falafel balls, which are then baked until golden and crispy.
3.  Whisking together a creamy orange-tahini sauce.
4.  Tossing shredded cabbage, quartered cherry tomatoes, and the pickled onions with a simple dressing.
The dish is served with warm pita bread, hummus, the cabbage salad, and the orange-tahini sauce. Falafel can be made ahead and stored or frozen.
