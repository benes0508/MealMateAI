Parmesan Thumbprint Cookies with Tomato-Tart Cherry Jam are a unique savory-sweet treat. The recipe features a rich, cheesy cookie dough made with Parmesan, butter, and cream cheese, complemented by a homemade jam of tomatoes, tart cherries, and fresh thyme. The process involves cooking the jam, preparing the dough, forming and filling the thumbprint cookies, then baking them until firm and browned.
