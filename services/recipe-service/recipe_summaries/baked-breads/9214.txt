This recipe provides a step-by-step guide to making homemade Fresh Egg Fettuccine from scratch. It uses unbleached all-purpose flour, semolina, and large eggs, detailing the process of combining ingredients, kneading the dough until smooth, and resting it. A **pasta machine** is essential for rolling the dough progressively thin and then cutting it into 1/4-inch fettuccine strips. The fresh pasta is briefly dried until pliable before being cooked in boiling salted water for approximately 2 minutes.
