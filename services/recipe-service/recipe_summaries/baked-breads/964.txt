This recipe details how to make **Roasted Cauliflower with Parmesan-Panko Crumble**. Large cauliflower heads are cut into steaks, tossed with shallots, garlic, thyme, butter, salt, and pepper, then roasted until tender and browned. Concurrently, a savory crumble is prepared by baking panko breadcrumbs with Parmesan, sharp cheddar, and butter until golden, then crumbling it. The roasted cauliflower is served topped with this cheesy crumble, fresh parsley, and lemon wedges.
