This recipe details how to make a **Gluten-Free Multi-Purpose Sweet Pastry Dough**. It's prepared by pulsing gluten-free flours, sugar, and leavening with chilled butter and cream cheese in a food processor, then adding egg and vanilla until just combined. The dough requires at least 2 hours of chilling and is highly versatile, suitable for:
*   **Cookies:** Rolled, cut, and baked at 350Â°F for 10-12 minutes.
*   **Pie/Tart Crusts:** Rolled and fitted into a 9-inch pie plate, then baked according to specific pie recipes.
*   **Bar Cookies:** Prepared and baked as per individual bar cookie recipes.
The dough can also be frozen for up to three months.
