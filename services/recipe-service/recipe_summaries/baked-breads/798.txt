This recipe is for **Cheesy Hand Pies**, savory individual pastries featuring a flaky butter dough filled with a mixture of feta, mozzarella, and fresh dill. The dough is prepared by grating frozen butter into flour, chilled, then rolled out, filled, sealed, brushed with an egg wash, and baked until golden brown and crisp. The recipe yields 16 hand pies and the dough can be made up to 3 days ahead.
