This recipe details how to make **Spaghetti with Turkey Meatballs**. It involves preparing a rich, roasted tomato, onion, and garlic sauce from scratch. The meatballs are made from lean ground turkey mixed with milk-soaked bread and an egg, then gently browned before simmering in the homemade sauce. The dish is served over whole wheat spaghetti, optionally topped with parmesan or romano cheese.
