This recipe details how to make **Cream Puffs (Profiteroles)**, filling them with **vanilla ice cream** and topping them with **chocolate sauce**. It involves preparing a choux pastry dough from water, butter, sugar, salt, flour, and eggs, then piping and baking the puffs until hollow. Finally, the cooled puffs are split, filled with ice cream, and drizzled with warm chocolate sauce.
