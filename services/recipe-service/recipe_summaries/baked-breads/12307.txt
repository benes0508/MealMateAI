This recipe is for a **Spinach and Leek Gratin with Roquefort Crumb Topping**. It features a creamy base of wilted spinach and sautéed leeks, enriched with heavy cream and Dijon mustard. This mixture is topped with a savory crumb topping made from toasted breadcrumbs, butter, mustard, and generous amounts of crumbled Roquefort cheese. The dish is baked at 400°F until bubbling.
