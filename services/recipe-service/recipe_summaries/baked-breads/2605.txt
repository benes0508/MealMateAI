This recipe details making crispy, homemade potato gnocchi served with littleneck clams. It involves roasting potatoes for the gnocchi dough, boiling the gnocchi, then pan-frying them in butter until golden and crisp. Littleneck clams are cooked and their liquid is used to create a savory garlic-lemon sauce, which is then tossed with the crispy gnocchi and tender clams before serving.
