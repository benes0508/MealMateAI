This recipe for Southwest Pita Crisps creates crispy, spiced pita triangles. Pita rounds are brushed with a vegetable oil mixture seasoned with chili powder, cumin, and cayenne, then topped with shredded extra sharp yellow cheddar. Cut into triangles, they are baked until golden and crisp. These savory crisps offer a bold Southwest flavor and can be stored airtight for up to one month.
