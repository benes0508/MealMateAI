This recipe features a Rotisserie Chicken Salad, highlighted by a unique Charred Scallion Dressing. It combines shredded rotisserie chicken, crispy homemade croutons (made from baked country bread), and sliced radishes, served over Bibb lettuce with fresh avocado. The dressing is a blend of charred scallion greens, lemon juice, rice vinegar, Dijon mustard, and mayonnaise, whisked with olive oil.
