This recipe details how to make savory Hazelnut and Olive Rugelach. A cream cheese and butter dough, seasoned with thyme, pepper, and salt, is prepared and chilled. Toasted hazelnuts and finely chopped green olives are then sprinkled over the rolled-out dough rounds, which are cut into wedges, rolled up, and baked until golden. They can be served warm or at room temperature.
