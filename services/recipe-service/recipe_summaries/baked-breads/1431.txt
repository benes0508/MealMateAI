This recipe details how to make **Mango Toast with Hazelnut-Pepita Butter**. It involves toasting hazelnuts and pumpkin seeds to create a homemade, smooth nut and seed butter, which is then spread over toasted sourdough bread. Sliced fresh mango is layered on top, drizzled with honey and olive oil, and finished with a unique sprinkle of ground freeze-dried blueberries, red pepper flakes, and flaky sea salt.
