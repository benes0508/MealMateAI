This recipe details a **Cauliflower and Brussels Sprout Gratin with Pine Nut-Breadcrumb Topping**. It involves blanching Brussels sprouts and cauliflower, preparing a reduced cream sauce with shallots and sage, and a toasted breadcrumb-pine nut topping. The vegetables are layered with Parmesan and the cream sauce, then baked until tender and bubbly, with the crunchy topping added towards the end. It can be prepared a day ahead.
