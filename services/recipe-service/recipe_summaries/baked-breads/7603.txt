This recipe details a Summer Vegetable Ragout featuring roasted eggplant and assorted summer squash, blanched green beans and corn, and chickpeas. These are combined with a unique, aromatic Carrot-Ginger Sauce made with fresh carrot juice, ginger, lemongrass, onion, apple, and curry powder. The dish is finished with fresh arugula and basil, offering a vibrant, flavorful, and seasonally-inspired meal.
