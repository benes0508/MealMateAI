This recipe details how to make Tomato Soup with Arugula, Croutons, and Pecorino. It involves preparing homemade crispy croutons from country bread, simmering a base of beefsteak tomatoes with onion, garlic, and thyme, then blending the mixture until smooth. The soup is seasoned and served garnished with fresh baby arugula, shaved Pecorino cheese, and the reserved croutons.
