This recipe details how to make a **Smoked Salmon Quiche with Kale, Basil, and a Sesame Seed Crust**. It involves preparing a savory sesame and spelt flour pastry crust, par-baking it, then filling it with a mixture of sautéed onion, kale, smoked salmon, crème fraîche, yogurt, and eggs. The quiche is then baked until set and golden, and garnished with fresh basil.
