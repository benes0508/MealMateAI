This recipe details **Pickled Egg Salad Crostini with Serrano Ham**. It features hard-boiled eggs pickled in a vinegar-sugar solution, then mixed with mayonnaise, chives, and parsley to form an egg salad. This salad is served atop toasted country bread or ciabatta rubbed with garlic, garnished with chervil, and topped with a slice of serrano ham or prosciutto. Eggs can be pickled up to a week in advance.
