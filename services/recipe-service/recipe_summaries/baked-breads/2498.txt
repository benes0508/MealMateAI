This recipe details how to make **Shredded Brussels Sprout and Ricotta Toast**. It involves toasting bread, then spreading it with whole milk ricotta. The topping consists of shredded Brussels sprouts tossed in a vinaigrette (shallot, lemon, honey, mustard, olive oil) with rehydrated golden raisins and toasted pine nuts. The toasts are finished with fresh lemon zest, black pepper, and flaky sea salt.
