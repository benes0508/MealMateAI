This recipe details how to prepare **Buffalo Meatloaf with Spinach and Roasted Baby Potatoes**. The process involves first roasting seasoned baby potatoes, then preparing the meatloaf mixture with ground buffalo, sautéed mushrooms, onion, herbs, egg, and panko. The meatloaf is shaped and roasted alongside the potatoes, then glazed with tomato sauce and red pepper for the final roasting phase. While the meatloaf rests, fresh spinach is quickly sautéed with garlic. The dish is served by slicing the meatloaf and plating it with the roasted potatoes and wilted spinach.
