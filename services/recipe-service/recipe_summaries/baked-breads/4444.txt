This recipe provides comprehensive instructions for making homemade squid ink pasta from scratch. It details the precise method for mixing a very dry dough using 00 flour, eggs, and squid ink, emphasizing extensive kneading, proper hydration with water spritzing, and crucial resting times. The process then covers rolling the dough thinly with a pasta machine and offers two methods for cutting the noodles: using a chitarra or hand-cutting. A key tip is to avoid adding extra flour during the rolling process to maintain optimal texture.
