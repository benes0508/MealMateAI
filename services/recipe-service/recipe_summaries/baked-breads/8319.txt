This recipe for Sweet Potato Stuffing with Bacon and Thyme creates a savory and slightly sweet side dish. It involves toasting sourdough bread cubes, cooking thick-cut bacon, then saut√©ing sweet potatoes, onions, celery, and fresh thyme in bacon drippings with butter and orange juice until tender. The cooked vegetables and bacon are combined with the toasted bread, then mixed with a whisked egg and chicken broth mixture. The stuffing is then baked until golden brown and crisp. This dish can be partially prepared ahead of time.
