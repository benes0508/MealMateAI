This recipe details how to make Rustic Bread Stuffing with Bell Pepper and Fresh Thyme. It involves toasting bread cubes with olive oil, thyme, and garlic until golden. Separately, onion, celery, and bell pepper are sautéed in butter with parsley. The toasted bread and sautéed vegetables are then combined with hot chicken or turkey broth, seasoned, and baked in a buttered dish, first covered then uncovered, until heated through and browned.
