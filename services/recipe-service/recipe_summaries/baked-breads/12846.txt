This recipe describes how to make **Roasted Cherry Tomato and Fresh Herb Bread**, an appetizer or side dish. Halved cherry tomatoes, shallots, olive oil, balsamic vinegar, and crushed red pepper are roasted until tender. This flavorful mixture is then spread over ciabatta bread halves and baked until the bread is crusty. It's finished with fresh marjoram and black pepper, then cut into squares for serving.
