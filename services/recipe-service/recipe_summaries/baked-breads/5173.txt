This recipe details how to make Eggplant Parmesan with fresh mozzarella. It involves preparing a savory tomato sauce with onions and garlic, then roasting eggplant halves until tender. The dish is assembled by layering the roasted eggplant with the tomato sauce, fresh mozzarella, breadcrumbs, and Parmesan, then baked until bubbly and golden. Key ingredients include eggplant, olive oil, onion, garlic, canned tomatoes, fresh mozzarella, and Parmesan. The sauce and roasted eggplant can be prepped ahead of time.
