**Veal Chops with Sherry Gastrique and Roasted Peperonata**

This recipe features pan-seared and oven-roasted veal rib chops seasoned with allspice and thyme. They are served with a sweet and tangy sherry gastrique (a reduction of sherry, broth, and brown sugar) and roasted peperonata. A red wine with good acidity is recommended for pairing.
