**Lemon-Pepper Popovers:**
Lemon-Pepper Popovers are a light, airy baked good made from a simple batter of milk, water, eggs, flour, lemon zest, and seasonings. The batter is whisked, poured into a buttered popover pan, and baked until puffed, golden, and airy.

**Georgian Cheese Bread:**
Georgian Cheese Bread is a savory bread featuring a yeast-leavened dough stuffed with a blend of Havarti and mozzarella cheeses. The dough is kneaded, risen, filled with cheese, flattened into a disk, scored, and then baked until golden, typically brushed with butter.

**Lettuce Soup with Croutons:**
Lettuce Soup with Croutons is a blended vegetable soup made by sautéing shallots, simmering them with broth and potato, then adding lettuce and herbs before pureeing. It's served hot, topped with homemade crispy rustic bread croutons.

**Classic Cheese Soufflé:**
Classic Cheese Soufflé is a light and airy baked dish prepared by creating a thick, seasoned béchamel sauce base with egg yolks, then folding in stiffly beaten egg whites and Gruyère cheese. The mixture is baked until dramatically puffed and golden brown.

**White Lasagne with Parmigiano Besciamella (Lasagne in Bianco):**
White Lasagne with Parmigiano Besciamella is a savory baked pasta dish featuring layers of no-boil lasagne sheets and a rich, creamy Parmigiano-Reggiano béchamel sauce. The sauce is made with shallots, butter, flour, milk, chicken stock, eggs, and Marsala, then baked until browned.

**Flatbread Stuffed with Cheese and Prosciutto (Torta al Testo):**
Flatbread Stuffed with Cheese and Prosciutto (Torta al Testo) is a savory dish featuring a yeast-leavened flatbread. The dough is kneaded, risen, skillet-cooked, then split and filled with Italian cheese, baby arugula, and prosciutto before being baked again until the cheese is melted and the bread is crisp.

**Truffled Taleggio and Mushroom Pizza:**
Truffled Taleggio and Mushroom Pizza is a gourmet pizza made with a pre-baked pizza dough, topped with sliced mushrooms and creamy Taleggio (or Fontina) cheese. It's baked until golden and bubbling, then finished with a drizzle of aromatic white truffle oil.

**Strawberry Cheesecake Cookie Stacks:**
Strawberry Cheesecake Cookie Stacks are a dessert featuring layers of creamy strawberry cheesecake filling sandwiched between crispy, baked phyllo dough cookies sprinkled with almond-sugar mixture. Fresh strawberry slices are added between the layers.

**Gluten-Free Pizza:**
Gluten-Free Pizza features a custom dough made from a blend of tapioca, white rice, chickpea, and sorghum flours with xanthan gum, activated with yeast and milk. The crusts are pre-baked, then topped with a reduced tomato sauce, mozzarella, and Parmigiano-Reggiano, broiled until bubbly, and finished with fresh basil.

**Easter Bread Dolls (Pupi or Titola):**
Easter Bread Dolls (Pupi or Titola) are decorative sweet breads made from pinza dough. The dough is shaped into "dolls" with braided bodies, featuring a dyed Easter egg pressed into the center and thin dough ropes forming a cross over it. After rising, the dolls are baked until golden and then brushed with a sugary egg glaze.

**Potato, Greens, and Goat Cheese Quesadillas:**
Potato, Greens, and Goat Cheese Quesadillas are savory tortillas filled with steamed, seasoned Yukon gold potatoes, hot pepper Monterey Jack cheese, and crumbled goat cheese. A unique salsa verde blended with mustard greens is used within the filling and as a side, and the quesadillas are cooked until golden brown.

**Braised Chicken with Tomatoes and Olives (Poulet Provencal):**
Braised Chicken with Tomatoes and Olives (Poulet Provencal) is a flavorful roasted dish featuring a whole chicken seasoned with garlic, herbs de Provence, and olive oil. It's roasted alongside a medley of tomatoes, onions, black olives, and sliced garlic until cooked through, then served with the pan juices and vegetables.

**Pear and Almond Tart:**
Pear and Almond Tart is a delicate dessert featuring a butter pastry crust, filled with brandy-tossed pear slices and a rich custard made with eggs, sugar, vanilla, and crème fraîche. The tart is baked, then topped with a crispy almond-sugar mixture and baked again until golden and set.

**Cream Biscuits:**
Cream Biscuits are a tender, flaky baked good made from a simple dough of flour, baking powder, salt, chilled butter, heavy cream, and half-and-half. The dough is gently kneaded, rolled out, pricked, cut into rounds, and then baked until light golden brown.

**Baked Tomatoes with Crusty Bread:**
Baked Tomatoes with Crusty Bread is a savory dish featuring a rich, spiced tomato mixture simmered with onions, garlic, allspice, cloves, thyme, and brown sugar. This thick tomato base is transferred to a baking dish, topped with buttered slices of white sandwich bread, and baked until bubbling and the bread is crisp and golden.

**Crusty Buttermilk Biscuits:**
Crusty Buttermilk Biscuits are a traditional baked good made from flour, baking powder, salt, cold lard, and buttermilk. The dough is briefly kneaded, rolled out, pricked, and cut into rounds, then baked until crusty and golden, and finished with a brush of melted butter.

**Texas Buttermilk Cornbread:**
Texas Buttermilk Cornbread is a classic, crusty baked good prepared by whisking yellow cornmeal, flour, baking powder, salt, and baking soda, then stirring in buttermilk and melted butter. The batter is baked in a preheated, buttered dish until golden and set.

**Caramel-Banana Bread Puddings:**
Caramel-Banana Bread Puddings are individual desserts featuring lightly toasted white sandwich bread squares layered with banana slices and a rich caramel sauce, all soaked in a custard made with eggs, half-and-half, milk, sugar, and vanilla. Baked in a water bath, they're finished with a butter and sugar topping for a golden crust.

**Wild-Mushroom Bread Pudding:**
Wild-Mushroom Bread Pudding is a savory baked dish combining toasted bread cubes and sautéed mixed wild mushrooms with shallots, garlic, and parsley. This mixture is then soaked in a custard of half-and-half, eggs, and Parmigiano-Reggiano, and baked in individual ramekins until firm.

**Cinnamon Palmiers:**
Cinnamon Palmiers are flaky, caramelized puff pastry cookies made from a butter-laminated dough. The dough is repeatedly rolled and folded, then generously sprinkled with cinnamon sugar before being shaped into a log. Sliced and baked, they puff up into golden, crispy heart-shaped treats.

**Salted Praline Langues de Chat:**
Salted Praline Langues de Chat are delicate, crispy butter cookies featuring a unique salted praline topping. The cookies are made from a batter of butter, sugar, egg whites, and extracts, piped into long strips, baked until pale, then sprinkled with a homemade praline (caramelized sugar and toasted almonds) and flaky sea salt before final baking until golden.

**Trios:**
Trios are unique shortbread-style cookies, where small balls of dough are arranged in a trio, creating a central space. Each of the three segments is then deeply indented and filled with a different fruit jam or preserve (raspberry, apricot, strawberry), then baked until golden.

**Galician Pie:**
Galician Pie is a savory, traditional Spanish pie encased in a yeast-leavened dough, featuring a hearty filling. The filling, known as "rustido," is made by sautéing onions, garlic, parsley, and chorizo, combined with strips of pork tenderloin or chicken and bell peppers. The pie is baked until the crust is golden.

**Farmhouse Herbed Stuffing:**
Farmhouse Herbed Stuffing is a classic, savory bread-based side dish made with sautéed onions and celery, seasoned bread cubes, and a blend of fresh parsley and dried herbs like sage, rosemary, and thyme. Moistened with hot turkey stock, it's baked until heated through and golden-brown.

**Savory Parmesan Shortbread Rounds:**
Savory Parmesan Shortbread Rounds are a crisp, flavorful appetizer made by processing flour, Parmesan cheese, salt, minced garlic, and cayenne pepper with chilled butter to form a dough. The dough is shaped into rounds, topped with more Parmesan, and baked until dry on top and golden brown on the bottom.
