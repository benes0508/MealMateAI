This recipe is for a savory Dried Cod Fish Pie (bacalao), featuring rehydrated and shredded salt cod combined with long-grain rice, tomatoes, onion, garlic, fresh herbs (parsley, mint, marjoram), and aromatic spices (cinnamon, allspice, orange zest) for the filling. The pie is encased in a homemade, simple dough and baked until golden brown.
