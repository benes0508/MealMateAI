This recipe details how to make Gnocchi Gratin with Gorgonzola Dolce. It involves crafting homemade potato gnocchi, par-boiling them, then baking them in a rich cream infused with rosemary and fresh ginger. The dish is layered with gorgonzola dolce and Parmesan cheeses, topped with a buttery breadcrumb mixture, and baked until bubbling and golden.
