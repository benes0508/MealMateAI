This recipe details how to make **Artichoke, Spinach, and Prosciutto Flatbreads with Spicy Honey**. It involves preparing a savory filling of cooked spinach, artichoke hearts, garlic, and parsley, seasoned with lemon zest. Pizza dough is rolled out, topped with Parmesan cheese, the vegetable mixture, more Parmesan, and baked until golden. Finally, it's finished with thinly sliced prosciutto and a drizzle of spicy honey (honey infused with red pepper flakes).
