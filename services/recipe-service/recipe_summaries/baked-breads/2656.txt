This recipe details how to make a large **Blueberry Peach Slab Pie**, perfect for a crowd. It features a **homemade butter pie crust**, prepared in two batches and baked on a **13x18 inch rimmed baking sheet**. The filling consists of **fresh peaches (peeled and sliced) and blueberries**, combined with sugar, cornstarch, vanilla, and lemon juice. The assembled pie is brushed with an egg wash and sanding sugar, then baked in two stages (initial high heat, then lower) until golden brown and bubbling, and requires cooling to set.
