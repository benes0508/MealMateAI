This recipe details how to make Crispy Whole Wheat Graham Crackers. It involves creaming together sugar, butter, and flavorings (cinnamon, vanilla, golden syrup) before adding whole wheat flour to form a soft dough. The dough is then rolled very thin, scored, and baked until tawny brown and firm. The recipe also includes variations for Chocolate, Cinnamon Sugar, Coconut (vegan), Gingerbread, and Gluten-Free graham crackers, as well as instructions for making a Graham Cracker Crumb Crust. The crackers can be stored for up to 3 weeks.
