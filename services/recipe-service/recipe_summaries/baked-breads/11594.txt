This recipe details how to make an **Apple Phyllo Strudel** featuring a sweet filling of cooked apples, dried cranberries, brown sugar, and cinnamon. The filling is rolled within multiple layers of phyllo dough, sprayed with cooking spray and sprinkled with cookie crumbs, then baked until golden. It's recommended to serve warm, garnished with vanilla yogurt (or frozen), fresh mint, and raspberries, or cinnamon cream.
