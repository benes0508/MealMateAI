This recipe for "Tuscan Beans in Summery Tomato Ragù" features dried cannellini or great northern beans simmered until tender, then combined with a rich ragù made from fresh grape tomatoes (both puréed and halved), sautéed onion, celery, and garlic, seasoned with thyme and a touch of sugar. The dish is finished by baking it covered with Parmesan cheese until saucy, and is best served with crusty bread. It's a hearty, flavorful, and savory Tuscan-inspired dish.
