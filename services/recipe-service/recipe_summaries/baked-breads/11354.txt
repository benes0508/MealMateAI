This recipe details how to make Bread Stuffing with Crawfish, Bacon, and Collard Greens. It involves first baking seasoned cubed crusty white bread until golden. Separately, you sauté a mixture of onion, celery, bell pepper, crawfish, bacon, and collard greens (simmered with broth). Finally, combine the baked bread with the sautéed ingredients and additional broth, then bake in a dish until heated through and the top is browned.
