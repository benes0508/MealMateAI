This recipe for **Blueberry Crumble Pie** details how to make a classic dessert featuring a buttery, flaky pie crust, a sweet and tangy blueberry filling, and a crispy crumble topping. The process involves preparing and blind-baking the pie crust, making a blueberry filling with sugar, cornstarch, and lemon, creating a buttery brown sugar crumble, then assembling and baking the pie until golden and bubbling.
