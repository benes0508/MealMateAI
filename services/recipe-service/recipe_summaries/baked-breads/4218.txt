This recipe details how to make a **Spiced Kabocha Squash Pie with a Pumpkin-Seed Crumble**. It involves preparing a flaky pie crust, roasting kabocha squash for a creamy, spiced filling (sweetened with brown sugar and molasses), and making a crunchy topping with flour, brown sugar, butter, and toasted pumpkin seeds. The pie is assembled and baked in two stages until golden and set, then cooled completely before serving.
