This recipe details how to make a **Blueberry Pie with a unique Cornmeal Crust**, served alongside **Lemon Cream**. The crust is prepared from flour, cornmeal, butter, and shortening, then chilled. The filling consists of fresh blueberries, sugar, cornstarch, and lemon juice. The pie is assembled with the filling encased in the cornmeal crust, topped with a second crust, and baked until golden brown and bubbling. It is served cooled, with the accompanying lemon cream.
