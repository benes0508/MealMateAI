This recipe details how to make **Wildflower-Honey Semifreddo with Honey Sesame Wafers**. The semifreddo is a creamy, no-churn frozen dessert created from a honey-egg yolk base, gelatin, and whipped cream, then frozen in individual ramekins. It's served alongside delicate, crisp honey sesame wafers, which are thin cookies baked with a homemade honey sesame brittle. Fresh diced navel oranges are used as a garnish.
