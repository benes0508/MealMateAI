This recipe details a layered **Peppermint Meringue Cake with Chocolate Buttercream**. It involves preparing three distinct components: crisp peppermint meringue layers, moist chocolate cake layers, and a rich chocolate buttercream. The cake is assembled by alternating meringue and cake layers, brushed with a peppermint syrup, and generously frosted with the buttercream. It's then chilled and garnished with chocolate mints, raspberries, and fresh mint before serving.
