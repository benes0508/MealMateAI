This recipe details how to make Rosemary and Cranberry Soda Bread. It combines flour, bread soda, salt, dried cranberries, and fresh rosemary, then mixed with buttermilk to form a soft dough. After light kneading and shaping into a round with a cross cut, it's baked at 220°C for 15 minutes, then at 200°C for another 20-25 minutes until golden and hollow when tapped. Best served warm with butter.
