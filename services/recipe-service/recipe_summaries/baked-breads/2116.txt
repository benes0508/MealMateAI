This recipe details how to make **Granola Bark**, a crispy, baked snack. It combines rolled oats, various nuts and seeds (almonds, flax/chia, sesame, coconut), and almond flour, bound together with a warm syrup of maple syrup/honey, coconut sugar, water, vanilla, and salt, along with olive oil and whisked egg white. The mixture is pressed firmly onto a baking sheet, baked at 325°F/165°C until dark golden brown and crisp, then broken into pieces for storage.
