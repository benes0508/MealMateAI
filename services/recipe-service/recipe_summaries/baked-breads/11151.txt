This recipe is for a **Chai-Spiced Cheesecake with a Ginger Crust**. It features a flavorful **ginger cookie crust**, a rich **chai-spiced cream cheese and mascarpone filling** (infused with ground ginger, cardamom, allspice, vanilla, and black pepper), and is finished with a **tangy sour cream topping**. The dessert requires extensive baking time and an overnight chill for best results.
