This recipe is for "Salty Buckwheat Chocolate Chunk Cookies," featuring melted butter, a mix of all-purpose and buckwheat flours, and bittersweet chocolate chunks. Key steps include chilling the dough for at least 2 hours, tapping the baking sheets after baking to deflate the cookies, and sprinkling with kosher salt for a salty finish. The recipe yields 10-16 cookies and can be prepared 3 days ahead.
