This recipe details how to make a **Blueberry-Pecan Galette**, a rustic, free-form tart. It features a homemade crust enriched with toasted and ground pecans, butter, flour, and cinnamon. The filling consists of fresh blueberries tossed with sugar, cornstarch, and lemon juice. The galette is assembled by mounding the berry mixture on rolled dough, folding the edges, and baking until golden brown and the filling is bubbling.
