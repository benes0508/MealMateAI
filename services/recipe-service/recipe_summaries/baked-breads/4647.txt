This recipe details how to prepare a Chicken Salad with Crème Fraîche and Rye. It involves roasting a chicken breast, shredding it, and combining it with fava beans, thinly sliced fennel, scallions, tarragon, and a sherry vinaigrette. The dish is served with whipped crème fraîche, a cucumber and lemon zest salad, and toasted Danish rye bread.
