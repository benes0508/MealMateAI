This recipe details how to make Roasted Garlic Crostini with Assorted Toppings. It involves roasting whole garlic heads until tender and puréeing them, then baking olive oil-brushed bread slices until golden to create the crostini. The crostini are spread with the roasted garlic purée and topped with a diverse array of options, including various cheeses (goat, Parmesan, mozzarella), roasted or fresh vegetables (bell peppers, sun-dried tomatoes, Roma tomatoes, basil), cured meats like prosciutto, anchovies, and savory pastes such as tapenade.
