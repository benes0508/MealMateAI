This recipe is for **Arugula and Ricotta Calzones**. It involves cooking garlic and wilting arugula, then mixing it with ricotta, mozzarella, Parmigiano-Reggiano, and an egg yolk to create a flavorful filling. Thawed pizza dough is divided, filled, folded into semicircles, and sealed with a crimped edge, then baked until golden and puffed.
