This recipe for Baked Breaded Cauliflower features a whole cooked cauliflower head topped with a rich, savory sauce made from sautéed scallions, flour, half-and-half, and Parmesan cheese, seasoned with salt, pepper, and nutmeg. The dish is baked at 375°F for about 20 minutes until lightly browned. It's intended as a versatile accompaniment for various roasted meats like beef, lamb, pork, chicken, turkey, or baked ham.
