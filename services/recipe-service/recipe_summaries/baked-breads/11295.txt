This recipe for **Parmesan Black-Pepper Biscotti** creates a savory, crispy biscuit. It involves making a dough from flour, baking powder, salt, Parmesan cheese, coarsely ground black pepper, cold butter, eggs, and milk. The dough is formed into logs, brushed with egg wash, and sprinkled with more cheese and pepper, then baked until pale golden. After cooling slightly and slicing diagonally, the biscotti are baked a second time at a lower temperature until golden and crisp.
