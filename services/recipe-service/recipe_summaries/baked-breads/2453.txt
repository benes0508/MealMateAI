This recipe details how to make Shakshuka, featuring eggs baked in a spicy, pureed tomato sauce. Key ingredients include diced tomatoes, garlic, Maras pepper, hawayej/curry powder, and eggs, served with pita bread and zhoug. The process involves slow-simmering and pureeing the tomato sauce, then gently baking eggs directly in the sauce until the whites are set and yolks are runny. It can be made in a single dish or individual portions.
