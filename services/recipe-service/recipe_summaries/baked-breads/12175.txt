This recipe details how to make a **Peppermint Cream-Puff Ring with Chocolate Glaze**. It involves baking a large choux pastry ring, preparing a light and airy peppermint cream filling infused with fresh mint leaves and crème fraîche, and crafting a smooth bittersweet chocolate glaze. The pastry ring is filled with the mint cream and then topped with the chocolate glaze and toasted almonds.
