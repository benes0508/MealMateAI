This recipe details how to make **Cornbread Dressing with Roasted Fall Vegetables**. It involves roasting a variety of fall vegetables (pearl onions, carrots, parsnips, rutabagas, crimini mushrooms, and garlic) with herbs, then combining them with dried cornbread cubes, beaten eggs, melted butter, and chicken broth. The mixture is then baked until lightly browned and crisp.
