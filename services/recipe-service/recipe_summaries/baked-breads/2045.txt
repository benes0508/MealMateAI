This recipe details "Spinach and Artichoke Omelet Wheels," a savory dish prepared by baking an egg mixture with flour, milk, and cream, flavored with mustard and soy sauce. It's filled with spinach, chopped artichoke hearts, and Monterey Jack cheese. After baking until set, the omelet is rolled into a cylinder, sprinkled with more cheese and chives, then sliced into "wheels" for serving.
