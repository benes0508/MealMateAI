This recipe features a **roasted half chicken** served with a creamy **cashew tarator** and a vibrant **celery salad**. The salad is enhanced with **spicy, candied walnuts** and mixed seeds, providing a textural contrast. The preparation involves making the walnuts and tarator ahead, searing and then roasting the chicken, and finally assembling the dish with the celery salad for a flavorful and multi-textured meal.
