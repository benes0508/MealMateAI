This recipe provides instructions for a **gluten-free zucchini pizza crust** paired with a vibrant **lemony pea pesto**. The crust is made by **de-watering grated zucchini** before combining it with almond and brown rice flours, eggs, and spices, then **baked at high heat until crisp**. The **fresh pea pesto** is blended with pine nuts, garlic, lemon, and hemp seeds. Once the crust is baked, it's topped with the pesto and briefly warmed, resulting in a **fresh, savory, and plant-forward** meal.
