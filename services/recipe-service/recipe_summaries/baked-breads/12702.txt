This recipe features a meatloaf made with ground beef, mozzarella, panko, and various herbs, bound with eggs, red wine, and ketchup. It incorporates pre-roasted diced zucchini, red bell peppers, and red onions into the meatloaf mix, and a portion of these roasted vegetables are also used as a topping when serving. The meatloaf is baked until cooked through, then rested and sliced. It's served topped with the re-warmed roasted vegetables and accompanied by mustard mashed potatoes.
