This recipe focuses on **roasted cabbage steaks** as the main component, complemented by **crispy, cumin-coriander seasoned chickpeas** and **herby sourdough croutons** (garlic, Dijon, parsley). The dish is unified by a **creamy, tangy lemon-garlic mayonnaise sauce**. It emphasizes varied textures and savory flavors, easily adaptable to be vegan.
