This recipe is for **Breaded Chicken Sandwiches (Cemita Poblana de Milanesa)**. It features boneless, skinless chicken breasts pounded thin, seasoned, breaded, and pan-fried until crispy. The sandwiches are assembled on toasted rolls (cemitas or soft sandwich rolls) with a generous layering of Salsa de Morita, white onion, sliced avocado, romaine, cheese, mayonnaise, and refried pinto beans.
