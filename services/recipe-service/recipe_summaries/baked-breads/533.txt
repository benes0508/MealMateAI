This recipe features **long-roasted eggplant halves**, seasoned and cooked until very tender and golden brown. It's served over a **creamy base of labne** (or yogurt/sour cream) seasoned with preserved lemon and fresh lemon juice. The dish is topped with **crispy croutons** made from bread, garlic, and chile, and garnished with fresh herbs. The components can be prepared ahead for convenience.
