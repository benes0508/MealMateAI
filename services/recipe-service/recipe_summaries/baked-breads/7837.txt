This recipe for Roasted Striped Bass with Fennel, Tomatoes, and Oil-Cured Olives involves searing whole striped bass for a golden crust, then roasting it with sliced fennel and red onion. The dish is cooked on a baking sheet, with reduced dry white wine poured over the vegetables. The fish cavity is stuffed with garlic and parsley, and cherry tomatoes and oil-cured olives are added later during the roasting. The meal is finished with an olive oil drizzle and fresh fennel fronds.
