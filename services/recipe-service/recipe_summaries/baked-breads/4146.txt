This All-in-One Sugar Cookie Dough recipe provides a versatile foundational dough adaptable for both classic roll-and-cut and convenient slice-and-bake cookies. It features five unique flavor variations: Pistachio-Cranberry, Lemon-Thyme, Chai-Spiced with Crystallized Ginger, Carrot Cake, and Mocha. Additionally, it includes instructions for creating Carrot Cake Sandwich Cookies with cream cheese filling and Mocha-Caramel Sandwich Cookies.
