This recipe for **Spicy Sweet-and-Sour Salmon With Dates (Qaliyeh-e Khorma)** features salmon fillets seared and then simmered in a rich, sweet-and-sour sauce made with date molasses, vinegar, garlic, cayenne, and fenugreek leaves, finished with Medjool dates. It's served with an accompanying **quick pickle** of seasoned red onion and Persian cucumbers, and traditionally with rice and fresh herbs.
