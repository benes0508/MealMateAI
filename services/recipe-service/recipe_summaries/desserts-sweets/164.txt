This recipe details a **"Very Red Velvet Cake"** featuring a **vibrant red hue** (from two bottles of food coloring), a tender crumb achieved with **buttermilk and distilled white vinegar**, baked in **three 8-inch layers**. It's generously frosted with a classic **cream cheese icing** made from cream cheese, butter, and confectioners sugar, and finished with **chopped pecans** sprinkled on top. The cake is designed for easy assembly after refrigeration and keeps well for up to two weeks.
