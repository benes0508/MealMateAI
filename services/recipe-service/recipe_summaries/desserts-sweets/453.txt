This recipe details how to make Sourdough Rye Brownies, utilizing a two-stage fermentation process with a rye (or wheat) sourdough starter and rye flour for a distinctive flavor. Key ingredients include dark chocolate (at least 66% cocoa), butter, eggs, and a combination of white and dark brown sugars. The fermented base is combined with melted chocolate and butter, then baked until just set. The brownies are served cooled and cut into squares.
