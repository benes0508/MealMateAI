A recipe for Blueberry Lemon Corn Muffins, featuring a unique blend of yellow cornmeal, sweet blueberries, and tangy lemon zest. The batter is easily prepared by mixing wet and dry ingredients, with half the blueberries folded in and the rest pressed into the tops. Baked until golden, these muffins are perfect for breakfast or a snack and keep for 2 days at room temperature.
