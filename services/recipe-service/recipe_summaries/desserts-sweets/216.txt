This recipe for "Mochi Cake, Any Way You Want It" creates a chewy, customizable mochi cake or cupcakes. It primarily uses glutinous sweet rice flour, coconut milk (or half-and-half), butter, and eggs. The recipe emphasizes versatility by allowing the cook to choose powdered flavorings (such as cocoa, pandan, or matcha) and ground spices (like cinnamon or cardamom) to tailor the taste. Baked until golden, the cake can be prepared up to 5 days in advance.
