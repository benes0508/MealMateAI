This recipe is for a flavorful Ramen Noodles With Spring Onions and Garlic Crisp. It features fresh ramen noodles tossed in a savory dressing made from soy sauce, rice vinegar, sesame seeds, and herbs, infused with a fragrant oil cooked with spring onions, ginger, and garlic. The dish is topped with a crispy garlic and scallion mixture, adding texture and aroma.
