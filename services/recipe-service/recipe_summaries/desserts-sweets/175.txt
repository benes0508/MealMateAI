This recipe details a multi-layered **Roasted Strawberry Layer Cake**, featuring three 6-inch sponge cake layers flavored with **elderflower cordial** and **lemon zest**. It's filled and frosted with a rich **Swiss meringue buttercream** infused with elderflower cordial and some **roasted strawberry purée**. An additional layer of roasted strawberry purée is spread between cake layers for enhanced fruit flavor. The cake is garnished with fresh strawberries and elderflower, making for a complex and elegant dessert.
