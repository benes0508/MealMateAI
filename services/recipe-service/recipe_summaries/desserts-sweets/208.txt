This recipe is for **Scallion Pancakes with Chili-Ginger Dipping Sauce**. It involves making a savory pancake batter with flour, cornstarch, and sliced scallions, cooked until golden and crisp in a skillet. The accompanying dipping sauce is a quick whisk of fresh ginger, soy sauce, rice vinegar, chili oil, and sugar. The pancakes are served in wedges with the sauce on the side.
