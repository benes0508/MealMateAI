This recipe details how to prepare **Pomegranate and Fennel Chicken (Pollastre amb Magrana)**. A butterflied chicken is seasoned and roasted with sliced red onion, garlic, and fennel. A separate sweet and tangy sauce is made from pomegranate arils and molasses, sherry, and sugar, which is used to baste the chicken during roasting. The dish is finished with fresh pomegranate arils, dressed walnuts, fennel fronds, and salad leaves.
