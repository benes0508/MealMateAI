"Pop It Like It's Hot Popcorn" is a versatile stovetop popcorn recipe. It's popped with coconut oil, then tossed with sea salt. A wide array of optional flavor additions are suggested, ranging from sweet (cinnamon, coconut sugar, honey, chocolate chips, dried fruit) to savory (chives, nutritional yeast, cayenne), and unique combinations (lemon zest, almond butter, pistachios). The recipe details a simple stovetop popping method.
