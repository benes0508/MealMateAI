This recipe details how to make Strawberry Honey Balsamic With Black Pepper Ice Cream. It involves roasting strawberries with honey to create a puree, preparing a creamy ice cream base from scratch, then combining the chilled puree with honey balsamic vinegar and finely ground black pepper. The mixture is churned in an ice cream maker and layered with strawberry jam before being frozen until firm. The resulting dessert offers a unique balance of sweet strawberry, tangy balsamic, and a subtle peppery kick.
