This recipe is for **Rice Noodles and Tofu in Peanut Sauce**. It features pan-fried tofu and bell peppers combined with cooked rice noodles, all tossed in a rich, savory-sweet peanut sauce made with peanut butter, soy sauce, rice vinegar, brown sugar, fish sauce, and lime juice. Fresh spinach is added, and the dish is garnished with cilantro and chopped peanuts, served with lime wedges.
