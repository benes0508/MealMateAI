This recipe for Triple-Threat Onion Galette describes how to create a rustic, savory tart featuring a buttery, flaky homemade crust. The filling consists of caramelized onions and garlic spread over Dijon mustard, topped with Parmesan cheese, and finished with fresh, butter-tossed scallions. It's baked until the crust is deep golden brown and the scallions are lightly charred, resulting in a rich and aromatic dish.
