This recipe details how to make "Tortillas with Eggs and Spicy Bean Chili." It involves first pureeing tomatoes, stock, onion, cumin, garlic, and chipotle in adobo to create a sauce. This sauce is then reduced, black beans are added, and finally, eggs are poached directly in the mixture until the whites are set and yolks are runny. The dish is served over corn tortillas, garnished with cotija cheese, avocado, cilantro, and lime wedges.
