This recipe details how to prepare **Grilled Salmon Wrapped in Lemon and Bay Leaves**. A center-cut salmon fillet is seasoned and then encased in layers of fresh bay leaves and thinly sliced lemons, secured with kitchen string. The salmon packet is then grilled in a fish basket until firm and cooked through, about 20 minutes per side. It's served drizzled with olive oil after removing the aromatic wrapping.
