This recipe details how to make **Fried Rice**, a versatile dish utilizing **leftover ingredients**. It involves cooking scrambled eggs and scallions, then stir-frying them with diced leftover meat (pork, chicken, or beef), thawed peas and carrots (plus any other vegetables), and cold cooked rice until crispy. A sauce made from soy sauce and water is added and absorbed, with the dish seasoned with salt and pepper to finish.
