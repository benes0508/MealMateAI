This recipe features **Grilled Jerk Chicken** marinated in a spicy scotch bonnet and herb paste. It's served with a sweet and tangy **Mango Chutney** (made with red onion, mango, cider vinegar, and brown sugar) and a fiery, aromatic **Scotch Bonnet Hot Sauce** (with mustard, orange/lime juice, brown sugar, curry, cumin, and coriander) on the side.
