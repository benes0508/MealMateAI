This recipe features **grilled jumbo shrimp** served with a **tangy, sweet, and spicy tamarind sauce**. The sauce is made from tamarind pulp, cilantro, garlic, Sriracha, palm sugar, and fish sauce, simmered to a light syrup. Shrimp are soaked in salt water, butterflied in their shells, then quickly grilled until pink and charred before being drizzled with the flavorful sauce. It's an **Asian-inspired seafood dish**, typically garnished with fresh chiles and cilantro.
