This recipe details how to prepare **Oven-Roasted Chicken Thighs with Carrots and Yukon Gold Potatoes**. Chicken thighs are seasoned with salt, thyme, pepper, and nutmeg, then roasted at 450Â°F for 30 minutes. Potatoes and carrots are tossed with oil and remaining spices, then roasted on the same pan for another 30 minutes. Finally, the chicken is returned to the pan with the vegetables and roasted for an additional 15 minutes, until fully cooked and vegetables are browned, then garnished with chives.
