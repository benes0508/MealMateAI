This recipe details how to prepare a **Mixed Seafood Grill** featuring firm white fish (like halibut), large shrimp, and squid. The seafood is lightly coated with olive oil, salt, and pepper, then **grilled** quickly until just opaque. It's served with a vibrant **Paprika-Lemon Dressing** made with olive oil, fresh lemon juice, parsley, garlic, and paprika, which can be prepared ahead of time.
