This recipe for Spicy Chicken Burgers features lean chicken patties made with a blend of chicken, portobello, edamame, garlic, and onion, seasoned with soy sauce and cayenne for a spicy kick. The patties are seared on the stovetop and finished in the oven. They are served on whole wheat English muffins, topped with a fresh, creamy relish of Greek yogurt, avocado, cucumber, and red onion.
