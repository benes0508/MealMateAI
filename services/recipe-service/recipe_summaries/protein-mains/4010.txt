This recipe is for a hearty **Kale Minestrone** soup, optionally made with pancetta, featuring cannellini beans, Yukon gold potatoes, tomatoes, and a mix of vegetables like onion, leek, carrots, and celery, simmered with an herb bundle (oregano, rosemary, bay) and a Parmesan rind. It's served topped with a fresh **pistou** made from basil, garlic, olive oil, Parmesan, and lemon zest.
