This recipe details **grilled chicken drumsticks** coated in a **savory caramel sauce**. The sauce features **garlic, lemongrass, sugar, rice vinegar, fish sauce**, and **spices** (red pepper flakes, cumin, black pepper). The **chicken is first grilled over indirect heat** until crisp and nearly cooked, then **finished over direct heat**, **basted with the sauce** until **lacquered and lightly charred**. Served with reserved sauce.
