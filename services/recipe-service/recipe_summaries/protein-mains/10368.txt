This recipe details **Roast Beef Tenderloin with Port Sauce**. The beef tenderloin is dry-brined for 24-36 hours before being roasted to a medium-rare finish. A rich, savory sauce is prepared with shallots, Cognac, rosemary, Port, and beef stock, reduced to a flavorful consistency. The sliced tenderloin is served with the accompanying elegant sauce, making it a sophisticated main course.
