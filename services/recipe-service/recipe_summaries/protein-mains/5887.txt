This recipe is for **Candied Nuts with Smoked Almonds**, featuring pecans and roasted almonds coated in a caramelized sugar glaze. The nuts are flavored with fresh rosemary, butter, ground coriander, and cayenne pepper, then combined with smoked almonds and seasoned with salt. The process involves candying nuts, re-glazing them, and finally tossing them with additional smoked almonds and spices.
