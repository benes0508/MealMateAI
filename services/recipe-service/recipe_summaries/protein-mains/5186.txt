This recipe details how to make **Duck Confit with Spicy Pickled Raisins**. It involves slow-cooking duck legs at a low temperature until tender and the fat renders, then crisping the skin at a higher temperature. Simultaneously, golden raisins are pickled in a sweet and spicy brine with vinegar, sugar, mustard seeds, herbs, and chiles. The crispy duck confit is served with the tangy, spicy pickled raisins.
