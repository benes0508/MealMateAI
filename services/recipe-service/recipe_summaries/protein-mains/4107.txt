This recipe features **roasted hot-honey shrimp and bok choy** served over **kimchi rice**. Shrimp is coated in a sweet and spicy honey-soy mixture, while bok choy is lightly oiled and salted. Both are roasted until tender and caramelized. Jasmine rice is cooked, then mixed with kimchi for a flavorful base. The dish is finished with a drizzle of sesame oil, sesame seeds, scallions, and lime wedges.
