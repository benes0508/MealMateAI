This recipe details how to make **Grilled Chicken and Shrimp Kebabs with Lemon and Garlic**. Large shrimp and chicken tenders are threaded onto skewers, then marinated in a mixture of lemon juice, lemon peel, garlic, and olive oil. The kebabs are grilled until cooked through (chicken about 5 mins/side, shrimp about 4 mins/side), then served over baby lettuces. Suggested accompaniments include Tomato-Avocado Salsa and Chipotle Aioli.
