This recipe details **Grilled Shrimp and Sausage Skewers** featuring **shrimp**, **smoked sausage** (such as andouille), **cherry tomatoes**, and **red onion**. The skewers are coated in a **smoky paprika glaze** made with olive oil, garlic, fresh thyme, and sherry wine vinegar, with half reserved for serving. They are **grilled quickly** (6-8 minutes) until the shrimp are opaque. This dish can be **prepared up to 6 hours ahead**.
