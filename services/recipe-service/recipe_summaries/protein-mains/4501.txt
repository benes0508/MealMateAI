Shellfish Mixed Grill is a seafood platter featuring grilled lobster, jumbo shrimp, and littleneck clams. Lobsters are butter-brushed and grilled on a grill with high and moderate heat zones, shrimp are skewered and grilled until marked, and clams are grilled until they open. The dish is served with tarragon cilantro salsa verde, sambal orange vinaigrette, and chorizo lemon butter.
