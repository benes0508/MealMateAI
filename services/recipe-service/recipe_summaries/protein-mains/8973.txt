This recipe for "Jasmin's Pad Thai" is a quick, authentic Thai stir-fry featuring rice noodles. It offers versatile protein options like shrimp, calamari, chicken, or pork, combined with egg, bean sprouts, and scallions. The flavorful sauce is made with fish sauce, palm sugar, chili flakes, and a homemade tamarind sauce (with instructions provided). The dish is cooked rapidly in a wok, combining ingredients in stages, and garnished with cashews, lime, and cucumber.
