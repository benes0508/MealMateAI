This recipe is for **Turkey Meatloaf with Mushrooms and Herbs**. It features a blend of ground turkey (including some 15% fat for flavor), mushrooms, shallots, Italian parsley, and thyme, bound with bread soaked in chicken broth and eggs. The meatloaf is baked at 350°F for about 1 hour 25 minutes until it reaches 170°F internal temperature, then rested before serving.
