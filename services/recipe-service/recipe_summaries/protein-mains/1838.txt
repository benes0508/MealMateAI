This recipe for **Smoked Chicken Wings** details how to prepare chicken wings seasoned with a savory and spicy dry rub (paprika, chili, garlic, onion, chipotle, mustard powder). The wings are slow-smoked on a charcoal or gas grill at 275°F using wood chunks/chips for 1.5-2 hours until deeply browned and cooked to 165°F. They are then tossed in a flavorful sauce made from melted butter, barbecue sauce, hot sauce, and molasses.
