This recipe for **Southwestern Barbecued Brisket with Ancho Chile Sauce** involves rubbing a beef brisket with a flavorful spice blend (ancho chile, paprika, cumin, etc.), then refrigerating it. The brisket is then slow-barbecued (or smoked) on a charcoal or gas grill with wood chips at 250Â°F for approximately 5 hours, until very tender.
