This recipe for "Torn Zucchini with Mint and Calabrian Chiles" features grilled zucchini torn into pieces and tossed with a flavorful dressing of thinly sliced red onion marinated in red wine vinegar, sugar, salt, and coarsely chopped oil-packed Calabrian chiles. Finished with olive oil and fresh mint, it's a sweet, spicy, and tangy side dish, served on a platter topped with more mint.
