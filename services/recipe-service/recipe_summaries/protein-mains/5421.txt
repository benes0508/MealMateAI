This recipe for **Grilled Beets with Burrata and Poppy Seed Vinaigrette** features grilled beets and scallions, paired with creamy burrata cheese. The dish is finished with a bright poppy seed vinaigrette made with shallots, sherry vinegar, Dijon, and orange zest, and garnished with thinly sliced raw beets. It's a fresh, flavorful, and visually appealing appetizer or side dish.
