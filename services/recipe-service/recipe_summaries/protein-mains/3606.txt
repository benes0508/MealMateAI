This recipe details how to make Enchiladas Verdes (Green Chicken Enchiladas). It involves simmering and shredding chicken, then preparing a vibrant green sauce from cooked tomatillos, serrano chiles, and chicken stock. Corn tortillas are lightly fried, then layered with the shredded chicken, green sauce, cheese, diced onion, cilantro, and Mexican crema or sour cream for a classic assembly.
