This recipe details how to prepare a **Garlic-Aioli Roasted Turkey with Lemon-Parsley Pan Sauce**. A whole turkey is roasted after being basted with a roasted garlic-mayonnaise aioli both under and over the skin, and stuffed with aromatics. The pan drippings are then transformed into a savory sauce, enriched with roasted garlic, shallots, herbs, and brightened with lemon juice.
