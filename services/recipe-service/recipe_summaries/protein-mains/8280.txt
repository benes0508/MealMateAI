This recipe details how to make **Peppered Lamb Burgers with "Hot Tomato" Jam**. It involves preparing a sweet and spicy tomato jam by simmering tomatoes, sugar, ginger, vinegar, and hot pepper sauce on the grill, then stirring in basil. Lamb patties are made with ground lamb, a five-peppercorn blend, garlic, sesame oil, and Merlot. Both the patties and buns are grilled, then assembled with the "Hot Tomato" Jam and lettuce.
