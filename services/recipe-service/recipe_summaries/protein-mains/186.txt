This recipe details how to make **Crispy Turmeric-and-Pepper-Spiced Chicken Wings**. Chicken wings are coated in a spice rub of finely ground peppercorns, turmeric, sugar, and salt, then drizzled with a ginger-garlic-oil mixture before marinating.
