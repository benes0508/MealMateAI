This recipe for **Chicken Thighs With Tomatoes and Feta** features bone-in, skin-on chicken thighs seared to a crisp golden brown in a cast-iron skillet. The chicken is then simmered with a flavorful sauce made from burst cherry tomatoes, harissa paste, and red wine vinegar. The dish is finished with crumbled feta and fresh oregano, served with crusty bread to soak up the rich tomato sauce.
