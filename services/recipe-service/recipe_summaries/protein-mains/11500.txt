This recipe details how to prepare **Pork Satay with Fresh Vegetable Pickles**. Thinly sliced pork tenderloin is marinated in a savory sauce made with shallots, garlic, lemongrass, oyster sauce, fish sauce, soy sauce, and sesame seeds. The marinated pork is then threaded onto skewers, broiled until cooked, brushed with a reserved portion of the sauce, and served with fresh vegetable pickles.
