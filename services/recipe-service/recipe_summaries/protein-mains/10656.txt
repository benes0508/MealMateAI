This recipe details a creamy **Risotto with Leeks, Shiitake Mushrooms, and Truffles**. It involves simmering leeks in cream, separately roasting seasoned shiitake mushrooms, and then preparing a classic risotto with Arborio rice, white wine, and vegetable broth. All components are combined at the end with Parmesan cheese, truffle oil, and an optional truffle garnish, finished with fresh parsley.
