This recipe for **Vietnamese Pork Chops** features bone-in pork chops marinated in a flavorful mixture of shallot, brown sugar, fish sauce, rice vinegar, and pepper. The chops are pan-fried until browned and cooked through, then served with a reduction of the reserved marinade and lime halves. The recipe also offers a "do ahead" option for marinating the chops up to one day in advance.
