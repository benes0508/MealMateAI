This recipe for Wilted Autumn Greens with Honey Mustard Vinaigrette features roasted butternut squash, Asian pears, chestnuts, and shallots combined with crispy bacon and a mix of fresh autumn greens (komatsuna, tatsoi, mustard greens). A warm vinaigrette is prepared using Dijon mustard, local honey, champagne vinegar, fresh thyme, and rendered bacon fat. All components are tossed together with the warm dressing and served immediately.
