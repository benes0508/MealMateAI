This recipe is for a **Thai Chicken and Shrimp Noodle Salad**, a refreshing dish featuring bean thread noodles, cooked chicken, and shrimp. It's tossed with fresh vegetables and herbs like tomatoes, cucumber, bean sprouts, mint, basil, cilantro, and shallots, all coated in a tangy dressing made with lime juice, fish sauce, and sweet chili sauce. The salad is garnished with toasted peanuts and lime wedges.
