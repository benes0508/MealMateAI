This recipe is for a Shrimp Poke with pickled radishes. It features warm sushi rice topped with shrimp tossed in a savory gochujang-based dressing with rehydrated hijiki seaweed. The dish is brightened by quick-pickled radishes and served with baby mizuna/arugula and toasted sesame seeds. Prep includes pickling radishes, cooking rice, soaking hijiki, and quickly cooking and chilling shrimp before assembling.
