This recipe features grilled Moroccan-Spiced Lamb Burgers, seasoned with paprika, cumin, shallot, cilantro, jalapeño, and garlic. They are served with a vibrant Beet, Red Onion, and Orange Salsa, which also includes green olives, cilantro, and jalapeño, dressed in a lemon, honey, and olive oil vinaigrette. Both the burgers and salsa can be prepared ahead of time, and the dish is typically assembled on cracked wheat hamburger buns with bibb lettuce and mayonnaise.
