This recipe details how to prepare "Grilled Lemon-Garlic Chicken with Leeks and Potatoes." Boneless, skinless chicken thighs, leeks, red bell pepper, and baby new potatoes are marinated in a mixture of lemon zest, lemon juice, grated garlic, kosher salt, smoked paprika, and olive oil. The ingredients, including lemon slices, are then cooked in a grill basket over medium heat for 20-25 minutes until the chicken is cooked through and vegetables are lightly charred. Fresh parsley is tossed in at the end.
