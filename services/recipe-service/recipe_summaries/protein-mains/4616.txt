This recipe details how to make a **Grilled Bloody Mary**, a smoky twist on the classic cocktail. It involves grilling tomatoes, lemons, and some celery stalks to impart a charred flavor. These grilled ingredients are then blended with Worcestershire sauce, fresh horseradish, sugar, salt, and pepper to create a flavorful Bloody Mary mix. The mix is strained, seasoned, and then combined with vodka over ice, garnished with a singed celery stalk, and served with hot sauce.
