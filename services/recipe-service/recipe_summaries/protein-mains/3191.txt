This recipe for "Back-Burner Stock" is a savory broth made from charcuterie trimmings, mixed vegetables (onion, cilantro stems, garlic, carrots, celery, fennel), and optional shrimp shells, simmered with spices like bay leaves, coriander, and red pepper flakes. It's quick to prepare in about 25 minutes (5 min cook, 20 min simmer) and can be refrigerated for 3 days or frozen for up to 3 months.
