This recipe details a tangy and sweet Charred Corn and Pepperoncini Relish, featuring grilled corn kernels, finely chopped pepperoncini, and sweet onion. The relish is built on a vibrant base of cider vinegar and sugar, offering a delightful sweet-sour balance. Fresh basil is incorporated just before serving, adding a fragrant finish. Ideal as a versatile side or condiment.
