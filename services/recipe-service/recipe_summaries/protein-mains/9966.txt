This recipe is for **Grilled Citrus Chicken Under a Brick**, featuring a whole butterflied chicken marinated in a zesty blend of orange, lime, and lemon juices with fresh oregano, rosemary, and garlic. The chicken is seasoned with paprika, salt, and pepper, then grilled skin-side down under foil-wrapped bricks or a cast-iron skillet for crispy skin, and finally flipped to cook through. It's served with grilled orange wedges for squeezing.
