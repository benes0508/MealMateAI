This recipe for **Grilled Chicken and Ratatouille** features grilled chicken breast served alongside a charred vegetable medley. The ratatouille consists of grilled zucchini, Japanese eggplant, red bell pepper, red onion, and tomatoes, which are then coarsely chopped and tossed with basil and red wine vinegar. Both the chicken and vegetables are seasoned with salt, pepper, and olive oil before grilling.
