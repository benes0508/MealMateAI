This recipe details how to make "Beef Picadillo Puffy Tacos." It involves preparing a savory ground beef picadillo with onion, potato, carrot, poblano chile, and garlic, simmered with salsa. Separately, corn tortillas are deep-fried using a specific technique to "puff" them into taco shells. The dish is assembled by filling the crispy, puffy taco shells with the beef picadillo and topping with lettuce, tomatoes, sharp cheddar cheese, and cilantro, served with lime wedges. The picadillo can be prepared a day in advance.
