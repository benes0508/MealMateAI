This recipe details how to make a **Fresh Corn Soup topped with Roasted Corn Guacamole**. The soup base is made by sautéing onions, garlic, and jalapeños, blending them with fresh corn, and then simmering the puree with chicken broth. The topping features oven-roasted corn kernels combined with fresh red onion, cilantro, lime, jalapeño, and avocado, creating a savory guacamole. The soup is served warm, garnished with a spoonful of the roasted corn guacamole and a cilantro sprig.
