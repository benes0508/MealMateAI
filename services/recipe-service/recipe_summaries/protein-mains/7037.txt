This recipe details how to prepare **Grilled Corn, Crema Mexicana & Cilantro Crostini**. Ciabatta slices are grilled and rubbed with garlic. They are then topped with a creamy mixture of crema mexicana (or sour cream) and crumbled feta, followed by grilled, slightly charred corn. Each crostini is finished with a squeeze of lime, fresh cilantro, and a sprinkle of chili powder.
