This recipe is for **Char-Grilled Squid in Sherry Marinade**. Squid is marinated for 3-4 hours in a mixture of olive oil, dry sherry, garlic, oregano, red pepper flakes, paprika, and salt. It's then quickly grilled for about 4 minutes until opaque and slightly crispy. Serve drizzled with olive oil and garnished with parsley.
