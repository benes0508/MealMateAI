This recipe for Summer Squash Sloppy Joes features ground beef or turkey cooked with finely chopped onion, carrot, and diced summer squash. A flavorful sauce is made by stirring in tomato paste, water, garlic, chili powder, paprika, and oregano, then simmered until thickened. The mixture is served on toasted hamburger buns topped with melted cheddar cheese.
