This recipe for **Citrus-Brined Pork Loin with Peach Mustard** features a bone-in pork loin first brined for 12-24 hours in a fragrant solution of citrus, herbs, and spices. After brining, it's coated with a dry rub of toasted coriander, fennel, and paprika, then grilled using two-zone heat until cooked through. The pork is rested before slicing and served with peach mustard.
