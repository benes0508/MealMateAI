This recipe details how to prepare **Brussels Sprouts with Pistachios and Lime**. Brussels sprouts are roasted until deeply browned and tender. Separately, pistachios are toasted in browned butter, then chopped. A quick glaze is made from date molasses, honey, and lime juice. The roasted sprouts are tossed in the glaze, then topped with the crunchy pistachios, lime zest, and red pepper flakes. It's a flavorful side dish with a balance of sweet, tangy, and savory notes, offering a nice textural contrast.
