This recipe for "Herby Ricotta-Poblano Tacos" features a creamy, savory filling made from roasted garlic, ricotta cheese, fresh herbs (like cilantro, epazote, thyme, marjoram), and roasted poblano chiles. The tacos are assembled with warm corn tortillas and garnished with thinly sliced radishes, chopped white onion, and cilantro sprigs for a light and zesty flavor.
