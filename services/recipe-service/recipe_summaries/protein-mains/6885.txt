This recipe details how to prepare "Roast Chicken with Herb-and-Garlic Pan Drippings." It involves brining quartered chickens, then pan-searing them with garlic, thyme, and rosemary until golden. The chicken is then roasted in the oven until cooked through. Finally, a flavorful pan sauce is made from the drippings, remaining herbs, garlic, butter, and lemon juice, which is poured over the rested chicken.
