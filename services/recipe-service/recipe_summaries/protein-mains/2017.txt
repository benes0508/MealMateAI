This recipe for "The Barbecue Burger" features grilled ground beef patties seasoned with barbecue rub, complemented by grilled bacon, lightly grilled romaine lettuce, fresh tomato slices, and barbecue sauce, all served on toasted onion rolls or hamburger buns. The patties are formed with a slight depression, seasoned, and then direct-grilled to desired doneness, along with the bacon, lettuce, and buns.
