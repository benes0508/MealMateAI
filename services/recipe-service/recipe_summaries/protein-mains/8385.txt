This recipe is for **Thai Rice Curry with Herbed Chicken (Khao Mok Gai)**, served with a sweet and sour cucumber relish. The main dish features chicken browned with aromatics (shallot, garlic, ginger), then simmered with brown jasmine rice, coconut milk, curry powder, turmeric, soy sauce, miso, and spices (cardamom, bay leaf, cinnamon stick) until the rice is tender. The accompanying relish is made by boiling vinegar and honey, then adding chopped cucumber, red chiles, and coriander after cooling.
