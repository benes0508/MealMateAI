This recipe is for **Grilled Portabellas with Soppressata Stuffing**. Large portabella mushrooms are brushed with oil and grilled. A savory stuffing is prepared by cooking finely chopped soppressata (or salami) and garlic, then mixing with breadcrumbs, oregano, Parmesan cheese, and mayonnaise. The grilled mushrooms are filled with this stuffing, topped with more cheese, and returned to the grill until tender.
