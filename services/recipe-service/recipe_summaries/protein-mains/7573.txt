This recipe details **Layered Chicken Enchiladas** featuring a vibrant homemade **tomatillo-cilantro sauce**. Roasted chicken and mozzarella are layered between corn tortillas in a baking dish, generously topped with the blended tomatillo, garlic, green onion, cilantro, and serrano chile sauce, plus a touch of whipping cream, then baked until bubbling. The dish is served with the remaining sauce.
