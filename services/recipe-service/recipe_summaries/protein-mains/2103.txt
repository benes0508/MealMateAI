This recipe for "Charred Sugar Snap Peas with Buttermilk Aioli" features grilled and raw sugar snap peas tossed with olive oil, salt, and pepper. It's served over a creamy, tangy aioli made with egg yolk, vegetable oil, buttermilk, crème fraîche, shallots, lemon juice, garlic, and cayenne, then topped with Calabrian chiles and flaky sea salt.
