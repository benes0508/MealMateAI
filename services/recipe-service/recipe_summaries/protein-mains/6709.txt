This recipe details **Oyster-Cornbread Stuffing**, a savory baked dish. It combines cooked breakfast sausage, sautéed onions and celery, fresh herbs (sage, parsley, thyme, rosemary-infused broth), shucked oysters with their liquor, and whisked eggs, all mixed with savory cornbread cubes. The mixture is then baked in a 3-quart dish at 350°F for 1 hour to 1 hour 15 minutes until browned and liquid is absorbed.
