A light and refreshing vegetable side dish featuring crisp-tender sugar snap peas and green beans. These are tossed with a vibrant, homemade pesto made from toasted almonds, fresh arugula, mint, garlic, and Parmesan, brightened with a squeeze of lemon juice. The dish is garnished with extra mint, toasted almonds, and Parmesan, making it a flavorful and make-ahead friendly accompaniment.
