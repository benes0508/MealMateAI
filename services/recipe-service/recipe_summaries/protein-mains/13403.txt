This recipe details how to make **Cauliflower Fritters**. The fritters are made from a mix of mashed and whole cauliflower florets, seasoned with various herbs (parsley, mint, chives, optional chervil), salt, pepper, and nutmeg, bound with eggs and flour. They are pan-fried until golden and traditionally served warm, topped with **smoked salmon** and **crème fraîche**.
