This recipe for **Roasted Pork Loin with Poached Plums** involves pan-searing and then oven-roasting pork tenderloins. Concurrently, plums are poached in a spiced wine (Pinot Gris/Viognier, red wine, star anise, cinnamon) and sugar mixture. The poaching liquid is then reduced with chicken broth, thyme, and shallots to create a flavorful sauce. The sliced pork is served with the poached plums and sauce, garnished with fresh chives.
