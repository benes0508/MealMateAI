This recipe describes making sweet and savory Apple, Goat Cheese, and Honey Tartlets. It involves preparing puff pastry rounds and rings, filling them with a blend of goat cheese, lemon, and salt, then topping with thinly sliced Gala apples. The tartlets are brushed with a butter-honey mixture, sprinkled with allspice, baked until golden, and finished with a final drizzle of honey. They can be served warm or at room temperature.
