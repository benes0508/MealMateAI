This recipe features **Barbecue Brisket with Pickled Fennel**. The brisket is marinated overnight with a dry rub of garlic, coriander, paprika, brown sugar, pepper, and sea salt, then slow-cooked on a barbecue until tender. The pickled fennel is made by simmering sliced fennel in a brine of apple cider vinegar, sugar, cloves, and fennel seeds, then vacuum-sealed.
