This recipe is for **Fettuccine Carbonara with Fried Eggs and Broccoli Rabe**. It features egg fettuccine tossed with a creamy sauce made from eggs, Parmesan, Pecorino Romano, garlic, and black pepper, along with crispy pancetta and tender broccoli rabe. The dish is uniquely topped with individually fried eggs, creating a rich and savory meal.
