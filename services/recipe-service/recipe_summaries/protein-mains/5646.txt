This recipe for **Chopped Liver** involves hard-boiling eggs, gently cooking onions, then adding chicken livers. The mixture is then processed or ground to a coarse or smooth paste, optionally with gribenes. Grated hard-boiled eggs and chopped parsley are folded in, and the mixture is moistened with wine, brandy, or chicken stock, then seasoned. It's chilled and served in scoops, garnished with grated egg and paprika, ideally with challah.
