This recipe details how to make Baked Polenta with Tomato Sauce and Ricotta. It involves roasting tomatoes, onion, and garlic to create a smooth, seasoned tomato sauce. Separately, polenta is cooked, then mixed with olive oil, basil, and ricotta cheese before being baked until firm. The dish is served by topping warm tomato sauce with slices of baked polenta and a sprinkle of fresh basil.
