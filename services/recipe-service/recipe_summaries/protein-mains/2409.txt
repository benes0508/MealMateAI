This recipe for **Green Beans With Mushroom XO Sauce** features green beans prepared two ways (partially blanched, partially blistered) and then tossed with a rich, savory mushroom XO sauce. The sauce is built by browning king trumpet and shiitake mushrooms, deglazing with brandy, then simmering with shallots, chile, garlic, dried shrimp (or fish sauce), soy sauce, vinegars, and sugar, finished with chile oil. The dish is served topped with fresh scallions.
