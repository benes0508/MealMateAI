**Tacos de Gobernador (Shrimp, Poblano, and Cheese Tacos)**

This recipe features shrimp, poblano chiles, and cheese served in warm corn tortillas. It involves saut√©ing onion, garlic, and poblano peppers, then adding diced bacon and shrimp, seasoned with salt, until cooked through. Lime juice is used to deglaze the pan and add flavor. Finally, cheese is melted into the shrimp and vegetable mixture, which is then distributed into warmed tortillas. Serve with lime wedges and red salsa.
