A recipe for Carbonara, emphasizing a silky sauce. It features guanciale (or pancetta), egg yolks, and Pecorino or Parmesan cheese, combined with rigatoni. The method involves rendering guanciale fat, then creating a creamy sauce by tossing hot pasta with an egg mixture, reserved drippings, and gradually adding cheese and pasta water for emulsification. It's seasoned with various freshly ground peppers.
