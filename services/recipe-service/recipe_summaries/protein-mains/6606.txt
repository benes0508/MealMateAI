This recipe for "Stuffed Sliders Your Way" describes customizable mini burgers made from your choice of ground meat (beef, turkey, lamb, or pork), seasoned with Parmesan, herbs, and garlic. Each slider is uniquely stuffed with a cube of cheese (cheddar, feta, etc.) before being flattened and cooked quickly by broiling or grilling. They are served on soft rolls with a wide array of suggested toppings and condiments for a personalized meal.
