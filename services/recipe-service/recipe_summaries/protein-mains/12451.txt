**Cedar-Planked Monkfish with Fire-Roasted and Puttanesca Relish:** Monkfish fillets wrapped in prosciutto are cooked on a pre-soaked cedar plank on the grill. They are served with a vibrant relish made from fire-roasted tomatoes, onion, and bell pepper, combined with Kalamata olives, anchovies, garlic, fresh basil, and capers.
