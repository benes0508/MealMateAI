This recipe details how to prepare a creamy Spring Vegetable Risotto with Poached Eggs. It involves simmering Arborio rice with warm chicken broth, dry white wine, leeks, fennel, and garlic, then stirring in spinach, crème fraîche, Pecorino, chives, fava beans (or peas), and sautéed mushrooms. The dish is topped with perfectly poached eggs, extra Pecorino, and fresh chives.
