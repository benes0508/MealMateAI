Grilled Short Ribs with Sesame-Chipotle Mole features boneless beef short ribs grilled to medium-rare. The ribs are seasoned with a flavorful mole made from pulsed almonds, sesame seeds, roasted red peppers, chipotle chiles in adobo, and sherry vinegar. They are marinated briefly with a portion of the mole before grilling and served sliced with the remaining mole.
