This recipe for Pan-Grilled Steak features 1-inch thick beef steaks, marinated in olive oil, then seasoned and pan-grilled in a hot cast-iron skillet. Steaks are seared on high heat, then cooked for approximately 3 minutes per side on medium heat for medium rare, with additional time for well-done. It also provides alternative cooking instructions for oven broiling and charcoal barbecues.
