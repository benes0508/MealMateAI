This recipe describes how to make **Pancetta-Wrapped Dates Stuffed with Manchego Cheese and Mint**. Medjool dates are pitted, then stuffed with a strip of Manchego cheese and a fresh mint leaf. Each stuffed date is then wrapped in three slices of pancetta. The wrapped dates are baked at 375Â°F for about 30 minutes until the pancetta is crisp and the dates caramelize. Serve warm.
