This recipe for Somali Beef Stew with Spiced Rice (Bariis Maraq) features tender beef chuck and vegetables like potatoes, carrots, and bell peppers, simmered in a rich tomato-based sauce. The dish is heavily flavored with a homemade *xawaash* spice blend (cinnamon, coriander, cumin, peppercorns, cardamom, cloves, turmeric). It's served with aromatic basmati rice, separately cooked with onions, garlic, tomato, and similar spices. Traditionally garnished with hot sauce, cilantro, lime wedges, and sliced banana.
