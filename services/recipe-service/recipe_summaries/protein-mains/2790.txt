This recipe features **dorade fillets** served with **roasted potatoes** and a **burst tomato sauce**. The sauce is made from roasted cherry tomatoes combined with rehydrated golden raisins, toasted pine nuts, capers, mint, garlic, and red pepper flakes. The potatoes are boiled, sliced, and then roasted with lemon, mint, garlic, bay leaves, and white wine. The seasoned dorade is roasted atop the potatoes until cooked through, then served with the flavorful potatoes and vibrant tomato sauce.
