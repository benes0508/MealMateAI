This recipe is for **Tuna Kebabs with Ginger-Chile Marinade**. Ahi tuna cubes are marinated in a flavorful blend of rice vinegar, ginger, oils, soy sauce, honey, cilantro, and serrano chile. The marinated tuna is then skewered with red bell pepper and sweet onion squares, grilled, and finally drizzled with reserved marinade and fresh cilantro.
