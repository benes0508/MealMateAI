A recipe for Grilled Shish Taouk (chicken skewers) featuring a homemade Toum (creamy garlic sauce). Chicken thighs are marinated in a flavorful blend of lemon juice, tomato paste, coriander, and a touch of Toum, then threaded onto skewers and grilled until browned and slightly charred. Served on warm pitas with additional Toum and pickles.
