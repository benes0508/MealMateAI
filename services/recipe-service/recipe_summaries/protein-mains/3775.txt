This recipe details making **Grilled Corn, Zucchini, and Black Bean Quesadillas**. It involves grilling corn, zucchini, and scallions, then assembling them with black beans and Monterey Jack cheese inside flour tortillas. These quesadillas are cooked in a skillet until golden and the cheese is melted, and served with a fresh avocado-tomato salsa and lime wedges.
