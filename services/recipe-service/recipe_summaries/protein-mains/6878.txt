This recipe for **Shrimp & Grits** features creamy, cheesy grits enriched with jalapeño, served with sautéed shrimp and tasso/andouille sausage (or bacon) in a beer and chicken stock sauce. It's topped with a fried egg and fresh tarragon. Key steps involve slowly simmering grits, sautéing the sausage/shrimp mixture, frying eggs, and then assembling the dish with grits forming a base, topped with the shrimp mixture, an egg, and tarragon.
