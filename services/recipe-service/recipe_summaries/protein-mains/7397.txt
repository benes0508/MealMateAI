This recipe is for **Roast Pork Tenderloin with Apricot-Miso Glaze**. Pork tenderloins are seasoned, brushed with a glaze made from apricot preserves, red miso, champagne vinegar, orange peel, and garlic, then roasted until tender. While the pork rests, the remaining glaze is simmered with chicken broth to create a sauce. The pork is sliced and served with the sauce.
