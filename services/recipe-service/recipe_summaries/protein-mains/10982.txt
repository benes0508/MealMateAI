This recipe details how to prepare Black Sea Bass with Moroccan-inspired Vegetables. Black sea bass fillets are baked over a medley of carrots, cauliflower, broccoli, spinach, and zucchini, all infused with a flavorful sauce made from leeks, garlic, oregano, curry, allspice, tomato, bell pepper, celery, soy sauce, and vegetable stock. The dish is served with couscous and garnished with pine nuts, fresh dill, and crumbled feta.
