A sophisticated dish featuring creamy, pureed cauliflower soup, prepared by simmering cauliflower with onion, garlic, broth, and cream. It's elegantly topped with perfectly seared sea scallops and garnished with luxurious American sturgeon caviar, blanched leek, fresh chives, and a bright drizzle of lemon-infused oil. The soup component can be made ahead, making it suitable for special occasions or entertaining.
