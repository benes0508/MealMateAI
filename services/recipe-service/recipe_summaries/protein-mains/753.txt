Liu Shaokun's Spicy Buckwheat Noodles with Chicken is a refreshing dish featuring poached and shredded chicken combined with tender buckwheat noodles (soba). The noodles and chicken are tossed in a savory and spicy dressing made from Chinese black vinegar, various soy sauces, chile oil, garlic, sugar, and salt, then finished with scallions and roasted soy nuts.
