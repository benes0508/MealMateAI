This recipe for "The BA Patty Melt" details how to make a classic sandwich featuring ground beef patties, caramelized onions, and two types of cheese (cheddar and Swiss) on rye bread, grilled until golden and the cheese is melted. Key steps include slow-cooking onions, forming and searing beef patties, then assembling and grilling the melts with mayonnaise-spread bread for a crispy finish.
