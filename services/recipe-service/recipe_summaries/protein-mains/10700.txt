This recipe for Crispy Pancetta, Burrata, and Tomato Sandwiches involves cooking thinly sliced pancetta until crisp, marinating tomato slices with fresh basil, olive oil, and seasonings, then assembling them on lightly toasted egg bread or brioche. Each sandwich is spread with creamy burrata, topped with the marinated tomato, crispy pancetta, and arugula, then covered and served.
