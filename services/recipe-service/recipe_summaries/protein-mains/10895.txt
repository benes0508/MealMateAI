This recipe details how to prepare a **Chicken and Cashew Stir-Fry**. It involves stir-frying seasoned chicken pieces until golden, then stir-frying red bell pepper, garlic, ginger, and scallion whites. A sauce made from chicken broth, soy sauce, cornstarch, and sugar is added and thickened. Finally, cashews, scallion greens, and the cooked chicken are stirred in.
