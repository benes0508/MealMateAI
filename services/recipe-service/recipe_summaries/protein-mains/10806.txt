This recipe for Eggplant Caprese with Grilled Tomato and Basil Vinaigrette involves grilling eggplant and a plum tomato, then blending the plum tomato with basil, white wine vinegar, and olive oil to create a vinaigrette. Fresh mozzarella and heirloom tomatoes are layered with the grilled eggplant, then drizzled with the homemade dressing and garnished with basil. The grilled eggplant and dressing can be prepared up to 2 hours ahead.
