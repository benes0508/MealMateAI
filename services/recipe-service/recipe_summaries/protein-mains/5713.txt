This recipe details how to prepare Roast Chicken with Kimchi Smashed Potatoes. Chicken thighs are seared until crispy, then roasted alongside par-cooked, seasoned fingerling potatoes. The potatoes are then lightly smashed and combined with kimchi. A dressing is made from reserved kimchi liquid, rice vinegar, and oil. The dish is finished by serving the chicken and kimchi potatoes with fresh bitter greens tossed in the dressing.
