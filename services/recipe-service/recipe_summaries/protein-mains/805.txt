Roasted Winter Squash with Kale Pipian is a vibrant vegetarian dish featuring tender roasted winter squash (such as delicata, kabocha, or honeynut). It's served with a unique "pipian" sauce made from blended tomatillos, wilted kale, onion, garlic, charred serrano chiles, and toasted pumpkin seeds. The dish is garnished with additional toasted pumpkin seeds, fresh cilantro, and a squeeze of lime.
