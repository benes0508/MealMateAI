This recipe details a **Chicken Caesar Salad with Crispy Kale**. It features **grilled chicken breast** and a **homemade creamy Caesar dressing** made with egg yolks, garlic, and Dijon mustard. The salad incorporates **fresh romaine lettuce, baby spinach, shredded Brussels sprouts, and pine nuts**. A unique element is the **crispy, oven-baked kale** tossed with olive oil, Parmesan, salt, and pepper.
