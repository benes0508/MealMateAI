This recipe for "Epi's 50-Ingredient Super Bowl Nachos" is an epic, large-format dish designed for entertaining. It features five distinct, layered flavor sections – Spinach Dip, Potato & Bacon, BBQ Pork, Pizza, and Buffalo Chicken – combining a vast array of meats, cheeses, vegetables, and sauces. Each section is built in layers on a large baking sheet, baked until hot and melty, then finished with fresh toppings. It is served with a central bowl of warm queso dip.
