This recipe details **Vegetable Enchiladas** featuring a hearty, spicy filling of roasted sweet potatoes, black beans, corn, roasted red peppers, and brown rice, seasoned with chili and cumin. Uniquely, the enchiladas are **stacked** (not rolled) with corn tortillas, layered with the vegetable and rice mixture, enchilada sauce, and pepper jack cheese, then baked and garnished with fresh avocado, scallions, and cilantro.
