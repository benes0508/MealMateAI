This recipe for Arctic Char Tostadas features quickly baked arctic char (or salmon) flaked and drizzled with a savory-tangy fish sauce and rice vinegar vinaigrette. Tostadas are spread with a lemon-zest cheese mixture, then topped with the fish and fresh garnishes like cilantro, serrano chiles, capers, and lime, creating a vibrant, fresh, and quick-to-assemble meal.
