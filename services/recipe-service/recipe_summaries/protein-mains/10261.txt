This recipe is for **Roasted Shrimp and Mushrooms with Ginger and Green Onions**. Large shrimp and oyster mushrooms are tossed with a flavorful mixture of canola oil, green onions, cilantro, pressed garlic, seasoned rice vinegar, grated ginger, and sesame oil. They are then roasted together at 500Â°F for about 7 minutes until the shrimp are opaque and mushrooms are tender. The recipe also provides a tip for deveining shrimp if needed.
