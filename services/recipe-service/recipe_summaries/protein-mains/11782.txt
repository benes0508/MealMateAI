This recipe details how to prepare a Wild Rice Salad with Smoked Fish and Snap Peas. It involves cooking wild rice until tender, blanching snap peas, and then combining them with flaked smoked salmon or trout, sliced scallions, and quartered hard-boiled eggs. A dressing made from cider vinegar, mustard, sugar, oil, and dill is whisked together and then tossed with the warm salad before serving.
