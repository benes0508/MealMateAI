This Bibimbap recipe is a customizable Korean rice dish featuring cooked rice topped with browned ground meat (turkey, beef, or pork), fried eggs, and a variety of shredded or steamed vegetables (carrots, corn, spinach, bean sprouts, red onion). It's finished with a savory sauce made from soy sauce, toasted sesame oil, and light brown sugar, then sprinkled with toasted sesame seeds.
