This recipe features a vibrant Chicken Tortilla Soup served with homemade Bean and Cheese Nachos. The soup is made with chicken, onion, garlic, chicken broth, fire-roasted diced tomatoes, and chipotle chiles, seasoned with cumin, salt, and pepper. It's finished with lime juice and garnished with avocado, radish, cilantro, and lime wedges. The nachos are prepared by baking corn tortillas, topping them with refried beans, cumin, and Monterey Jack cheese, then baking until golden and cutting into wedges.
