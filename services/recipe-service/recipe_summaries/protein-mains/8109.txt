This recipe is for **Green Onion Sausage and Shrimp Gravy**, a savory, rich dish featuring browned pork sausage and cooked shrimp. The gravy base is made from rendered bacon fat, flour, onions, bell pepper, garlic, tomatoes, and chicken stock, seasoned with allspice, Worcestershire, and red pepper flakes. The shrimp are briefly cooked and then added back to the simmering gravy with fresh thyme and green onions at the end.
