This recipe details how to prepare **Grilled Asian Chicken with Bok Choy, Shiitake Mushrooms, and Radishes**. Boneless chicken breasts and a variety of vegetables (bok choy, shiitake, radishes, red onion, bell pepper) are brushed with mango-sesame dressing, seasoned, and then grilled until tender and cooked through. The dish is served with extra dressing, with an option to reserve some chicken for a future salad.
