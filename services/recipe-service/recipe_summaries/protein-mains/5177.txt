This recipe for **Cod with Lemon, Green Olive, and Onion Relish** involves roasting cod fillets and serving them with a vibrant, chilled relish. The relish is made by combining lemon segments, thinly sliced and desalted red onion, chopped green olives, capers, lemon zest, and olive oil, then chilling it for at least 4 hours. Skinless cod fillets are simply roasted with olive oil and dried red chiles until cooked through. To serve, the relish (mixed with fresh parsley) is spooned over the roasted fish. The relish can be prepared a day in advance.
