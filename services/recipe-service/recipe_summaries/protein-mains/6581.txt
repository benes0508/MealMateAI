This recipe details how to prepare an Agave-Glazed Turkey Breast with Sherry Gravy. A bone-in turkey breast is oven-roasted, then brushed with a sweet and tangy agave-sherry vinegar glaze. Pan drippings are used to create a rich sherry gravy, thickened with a flour slurry, resulting in a savory dish with a hint of sweetness and a complex, flavorful gravy.
