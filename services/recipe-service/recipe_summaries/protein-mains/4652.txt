This recipe details **Smoked Salmon Tartines**, elegant open-faced sandwiches featuring rich smoked salmon, creamy crème fraîche, tangy pickled red onion, and crispy fried capers on toasted country-style bread. Garnished with fresh chives, they offer a delightful combination of savory, fresh, and briny flavors with varied textures, perfect as an appetizer, light meal, or brunch item. The pickled onions can be prepared a day in advance.
