This recipe for Roasted Carrots with Stracciatella and Buckwheat is a vibrant side dish or appetizer. It features tender, golden roasted carrots tossed in a light champagne vinegar (and optional carrot juice) dressing. These are served over creamy stracciatella di bufala (or burrata/mozzarella), then topped with fragrant toasted buckwheat, optional nigella seeds, and fresh mixed herbs, finished with a drizzle of olive oil. Carrots can be roasted ahead of time.
