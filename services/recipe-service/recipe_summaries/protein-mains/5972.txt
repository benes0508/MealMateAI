This recipe details how to make a flavorful butternut squash soup. The squash is first roasted until tender, then pureed with chicken broth and warming spices like cinnamon, marjoram, thyme, and nutmeg. Milk or half-and-half is whisked in for creaminess. The soup is served hot, topped with a dollop of pumpkin butter and a sprinkle of chopped pistachios.
