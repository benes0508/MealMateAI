This recipe for Grilled Flatbread details making a soft, yeast-leavened dough with all-purpose and whole wheat flours, and sour cream, which can be prepared up to two days in advance. The dough portions are rolled out and grilled on a medium-hot grill until lightly charred and cooked through. The warm flatbread can then be served with various suggested toppings like ancho chile oil, black beans, avocado, pico de gallo, or fresh tomatoes and basil.
