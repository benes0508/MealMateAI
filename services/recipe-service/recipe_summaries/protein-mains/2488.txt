This recipe details how to make a **Roasted Beet Dip With Hazelnuts and Goat Cheese**. It involves roasting red or golden beets until tender, toasting hazelnuts, and quick-pickling thin matchsticks of raw beet. The roasted beets are then blended with goat cheese, roasted hazelnuts, garlic, and seasonings until smooth. Coarsely chopped pickled beets are stirred into the dip, and the remaining pickled beets are used as a topping. The dip is served with vegetable crudit√© and crackers.
