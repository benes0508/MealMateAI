This recipe details a **Grilled Clambake with Miso-Lime Butter**, featuring littleneck clams, jumbo shrimp, baby new potatoes, corn, and scallions. The dish highlights a flavorful compound butter (miso, ginger, lime zest, red pepper flakes) used for both cooking and serving. Ingredients are grilled, some in foil packets (potatoes, clams) and others directly (corn, shrimp, scallions), then combined and garnished with cilantro and sesame seeds. The miso butter and pre-assembled foil packets can be prepared ahead of time.
