This recipe for **Sticky Spare Ribs Casserole** features pork spare ribs marinated in a mix of dijon mustard, blackstrap molasses, and Chinese five-spice powder. The ribs are slow-cooked in a Dutch oven on a bed of red cabbage, fuji apples, green onions, and optional dried cherries, with apple cider vinegar and ghee. After an hour of baking, the dish is broiled to achieve a browned, sticky finish.
