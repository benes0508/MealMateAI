This recipe details an **Almond-Macaroon Torte with Chocolate Frosting and Orange Compote**. It involves preparing an orange syrup (used for both the compote and frosting base), baking rectangular macaroon layers from ground almonds, sugar, vanilla, and egg whites, and then assembling the torte. The chocolate frosting is made by melting bittersweet chocolate into a portion of the orange syrup. The torte is layered with frosting, covered, and garnished with toasted sliced almonds on the sides. It's served with an orange compote made from sliced oranges steeped in the reserved orange syrup.
