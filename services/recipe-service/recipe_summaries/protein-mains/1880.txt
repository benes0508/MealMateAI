This recipe is for **Grilled Shrimp, Zucchini, and Tomatoes with Feta**. Jumbo shrimp, zucchini rounds, and cherry tomatoes are tossed with a dressing of garlic, oregano, salt, red pepper, and olive oil, then grilled in a basket until the shrimp is cooked and vegetables are lightly charred (about 6 minutes). It's served with warm pita bread and topped with crumbled feta.
