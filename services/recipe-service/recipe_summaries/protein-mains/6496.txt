This recipe details the preparation of Redeye-Glazed Pork Tenderloin served with Black-Eyed Peas. The pork tenderloin is seared and then roasted, brushed with a rich glaze made from country ham, strong coffee, stock, sorghum syrup, and apple cider vinegar. Black-eyed peas are cooked with onion, garlic, and chicken broth until tender, with some mashed for a creamy consistency.
