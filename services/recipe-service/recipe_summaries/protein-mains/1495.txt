This recipe details how to make **Roasted Cauliflower with Lemon Zest, Parsley, Capers, and Jalapeño**. Cauliflower florets are roasted until golden and crispy, then tossed with fresh parsley, capers, thinly sliced jalapeño, and lemon zest, finished with a squeeze of lemon juice, a drizzle of olive oil, and flaky sea salt.
