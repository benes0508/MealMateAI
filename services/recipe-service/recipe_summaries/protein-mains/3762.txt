The Double RL Ranch Burger features grilled beef patties seasoned with salt and topped with melted clothbound cheddar. The burger is built on toasted sesame buns with crispy bacon, fresh lettuce, tomato, onion, pickles, and a distinctive homemade "Ranch Burger" sauce made from mayonnaise, ketchup, relish, cornichons, red onion, Worcestershire, garlic and onion powder, and hot sauce.
