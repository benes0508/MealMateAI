This recipe details how to prepare **Stuffed Trout**, featuring rainbow trout filled with a flavorful mixture of sautéed mushrooms, bell peppers, jalapeño, onion, celery, and lump crabmeat, seasoned with Old Bay. The stuffed trout is baked at 425°F for 12-15 minutes, then finished with pan juices enhanced by fresh lemon juice and garnished with parsley.
