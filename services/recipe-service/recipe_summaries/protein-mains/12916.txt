This recipe details **Caramelized Spiced Carrots**, featuring medium carrots roasted in a sweet and savory glaze. The glaze is made from **pomegranate molasses**, olive oil, butter, and a blend of spices including **ginger, cumin, cardamom, nutmeg, and cayenne pepper**. The carrots are roasted until tender and glazed, then garnished with fresh **pomegranate seeds, toasted pine nuts, basil, and mint** for added freshness and crunch.
