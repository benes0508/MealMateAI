This recipe details **Persian Chicken with Turmeric and Lime**. It involves marinating chicken with turmeric, lime, cumin, and garlic, then cooking it. Alongside, aromatic basmati rice is prepared with cardamom, cinnamon, and cumin. A simple side salad of cucumber and fresh herbs is tossed. The dish is assembled by serving the chicken over the rice, topped with the cucumber salad, pistachios, and sumac, with extra lime for serving.
