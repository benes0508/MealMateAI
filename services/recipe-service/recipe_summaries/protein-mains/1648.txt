This recipe details how to prepare a Cast-Iron Roast Chicken with Winter Squash, Red Onions, and Pancetta. A whole chicken is generously seasoned and roasted in a preheated cast-iron skillet at 425°F for 50-60 minutes, surrounded by a mixture of winter squash, red onions, and chopped pancetta. The dish is cooked until the chicken reaches 155°F internal temperature, then rested before carving and serving with the tender vegetables.
