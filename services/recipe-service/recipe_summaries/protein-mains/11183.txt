This recipe for "Lasagne Rolls with Roasted Tomato and Eggplant" involves roasting plum tomatoes, eggplant, and garlic to create a savory base and topping. Lasagne noodles are boiled and then filled with a creamy mixture of ricotta, Parmigiano Reggiano, egg yolk, and spices. The filled noodles are rolled, placed over a layer of roasted vegetables, topped with more vegetables, and then baked until heated through. It's a portion-controlled variation of traditional lasagna.
