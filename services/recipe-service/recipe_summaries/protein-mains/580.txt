This recipe details how to make **Crispy Pan-Seared Chicken and Zucchini with Olives and Lemon**. It involves pan-searing skin-on chicken thighs until crispy, then cooking crosshatch-scored zucchini in the same skillet until charred. A flavorful garlic, Fresno chile, and Castelvetrano olive oil infused with oregano and lemon zest is then spooned over the chicken and zucchini before serving with fresh lemon.
