A recipe for **Roast Chicken with Rosemary-Garlic Paste** involves seasoning a 5-6lb whole chicken with a potent paste made from fresh rosemary, garlic, juniper berries, salt, pepper, and olive oil. The paste is applied generously, including under the skin. The chicken is then roasted uncovered on rosemary sprigs at 400°F for about 1.5 hours until an internal temperature of 170°F is reached. The recipe includes instructions for preparing the chicken up to 8 hours in advance.
