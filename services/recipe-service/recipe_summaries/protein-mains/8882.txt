This slow cooker recipe for White Beans with Tasso combines dried great northern beans with smoked ham hock and diced tasso (or andouille) sausage. Aromatic vegetables like onion, celery, red bell pepper, garlic, and bay leaves complete the dish. It cooks on high for about 4.5 hours until the beans are tender, with the shredded ham hock meat returned to the pot.
