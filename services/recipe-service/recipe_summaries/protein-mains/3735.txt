This recipe details how to make a Pan Bagnat, a pressed French sandwich. It features a crusty baguette filled with flaked tuna, anchovy fillets, hard-boiled eggs, olives, thinly sliced tomato, marinated onion, and fresh parsley, all seasoned with olive oil, garlic, red wine vinegar, salt, and pepper. After assembly, the sandwiches are tightly wrapped and pressed under weight for 40-60 minutes before serving.
