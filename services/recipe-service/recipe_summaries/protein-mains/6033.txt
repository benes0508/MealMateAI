This recipe for **Queso Fundido** describes a warm, melted cheese dip featuring a blend of cheddar and Monterey Jack cheeses. It's enriched with cooked chorizo or hot Italian sausage, onion, and beer, creating a smooth, savory sauce. The dish is topped with a fresh salsa made from chopped tomato, serrano chile, and oregano, and typically served hot with tortilla chips for dipping. It can be prepared up to 30 minutes in advance and reheated.
