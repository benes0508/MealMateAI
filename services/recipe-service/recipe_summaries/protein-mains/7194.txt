This recipe details how to make **Chicken Skewers with Tarragon-Pistachio Pesto**. Chicken tenders, red onion, red bell pepper, and lemon slices are threaded onto skewers and grilled. The skewers are brushed with a vibrant pesto made from fresh parsley, tarragon, pistachios, lemon juice, garlic, and olive oil, then seasoned and grilled until the chicken is cooked through and vegetables are tender. The dish is served with extra reserved pesto.
