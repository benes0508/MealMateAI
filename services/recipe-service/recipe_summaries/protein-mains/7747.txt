This recipe is for **Cambodian Grilled Chicken (Mann Oeng K'tem Sor, Marech)**. It features a whole spatchcocked chicken marinated for 1-4 hours in a blend of garlic, sugar, salt, soy sauce, and fish sauce. The chicken is then grilled until golden brown and cooked through, brushed with an annatto (or paprika) infused oil glaze during the final cooking stage. It is served with a simple dipping sauce made from lime juice, salt, pepper, and optional sugar.
