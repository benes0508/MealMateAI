This recipe details how to make **Chicken Alfredo with Zucchini Ribbons**. It involves thinly slicing zucchini into ribbons, quickly cooking them, and pan-searing chicken cutlets. A light Alfredo sauce is prepared using low-fat milk, evaporated skim milk, and Parmesan. Cooked fettuccine is then tossed with the sauce, zucchini, and parsley, topped with the chicken, and garnished with more sauce and parsley.
