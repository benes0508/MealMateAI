This recipe is for **Reginetti with Savoy Cabbage and Pancetta**. It involves cooking reginetti pasta until al dente, while simultaneously crisping pancetta and charring savoy cabbage in a skillet. Butter, thyme, and Parmesan are then added to the cabbage, followed by the partially cooked pasta and pasta water to create a thickened, emulsified sauce. Finally, the crispy pancetta is incorporated, and the dish is seasoned and served topped with poppy seeds.
