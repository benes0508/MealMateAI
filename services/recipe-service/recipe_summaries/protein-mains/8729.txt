This recipe features **Pecan-Crusted Pork Tenderloin Pinwheels with Carolina Mustard Sauce**. Pork tenderloin strips are wrapped with bacon, rolled into pinwheels, coated with Carolina mustard sauce and a pecan-salt-pepper mixture, then cut into medallions. These are **direct grilled** over medium-high heat until the bacon crisps, and served with additional mustard sauce.
