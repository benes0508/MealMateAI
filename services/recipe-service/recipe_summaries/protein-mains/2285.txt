This recipe for **Instant-Pot Red Chicken Enchiladas** features boneless chicken thighs cooked in an Instant Pot with ancho chile sauce, then shredded and mixed with the sauce and chopped onion. Corn tortillas are briefly warmed, filled with the chicken mixture, and rolled. The enchiladas are then covered with more sauce and Monterey Jack cheese, baked, and finished with crumbled queso fresco. It's described as a Mexican-American style dish.
