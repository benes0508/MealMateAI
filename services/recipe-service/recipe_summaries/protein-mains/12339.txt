This "Drunken Noodles" (Pad Kee Mao) recipe is a spicy Thai stir-fried dish featuring flat rice noodles and ground chicken. Key ingredients include garlic, fresh Thai chiles, fish sauce, various soy sauces, tomatoes, Anaheim chiles (or bell peppers), and fresh Thai basil, cooked quickly in a hot wok after the noodles are boiled.
