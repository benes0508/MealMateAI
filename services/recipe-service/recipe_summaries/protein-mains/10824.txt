This recipe details how to prepare a **Grilled Leg of Lamb with Thyme and Allspice**. A bone-in leg of lamb is marinated for 12 hours in a mixture of garlic, salt, olive oil, lemon zest and juice, fresh thyme, black pepper, and allspice.
