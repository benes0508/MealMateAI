A hearty Slow Cooker Calico Beans recipe featuring ground beef, bacon, and a mix of white and kidney beans. It's simmered in a sweet, tangy, and savory tomato-based sauce flavored with ketchup, brown sugar, apple cider vinegar, Worcestershire, and mustard, with onion and a hint of red pepper. Simply combine all ingredients in the slow cooker and cook on low for 4-8 hours.
