This recipe details how to prepare **Grilled Halibut with Lemongrass Tomato Sauce**. It involves making a flavorful, chunky tomato sauce by simmering shallots, garlic, tomatoes, and lemongrass, then straining it. Separately, halibut steaks are seasoned, oiled, and then grilled until cooked through (7-8 minutes), to be served with the prepared sauce.
