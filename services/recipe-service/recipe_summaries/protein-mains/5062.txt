This recipe details how to prepare a **Turkey Breast Stuffed with Italian Sausage and Marsala-Steeped Cranberries**. It involves first steeping cranberries in Marsala, then cooking Italian sausage with shallots, sage, and spices. This mixture is combined with the cranberries, eggs, Parmesan, and breadcrumbs to create a savory stuffing. The butterflied turkey breast is generously filled, secured, coated with duck or goose fat, and then roasted until cooked through and golden. The dish is rested before slicing and can be served with condiments like Italian mostarda.
