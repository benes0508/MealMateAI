"Angry Lobster with Lemon Rice and Crispy Basil" is a spicy seafood dish featuring live lobsters coated in cayenne and chili-spiced flour, seared, and then baked. It's served with a vibrant sauce of garlic, red pepper flakes, stock, butter, lemon juice, and oven-dried tomatoes. The meal is accompanied by lemon-infused rice (with optional zucchini) and garnished with crispy fried basil leaves.
