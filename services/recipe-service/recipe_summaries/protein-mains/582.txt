This recipe for **Vegetarian Piri Piri Chorizo Bake** features roasted sweet potatoes and bell peppers combined with cooked vegetarian chorizo sausages. A homemade, spicy Piri Piri sauce, made with fresh chiles, bell pepper, onion, garlic, paprika, oregano, and lemon, is mixed in along with cherry tomatoes. The entire dish is then baked until tender and hot, and served over basmati rice, garnished with fresh cilantro.
