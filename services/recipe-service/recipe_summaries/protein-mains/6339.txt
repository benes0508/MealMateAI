This recipe details Black Bean Tacos with Corn Salsa. It features a savory, oven-baked black bean crumble made with processed black beans, rolled oats, and cornmeal, served as the filling. This is complemented by a fresh, vibrant corn salsa with red bell pepper, green chile, scallions, cilantro, and lime, all served in warm corn tortillas. This is a plant-based/vegetarian dish.
