This recipe details how to prepare Grilled Eggplant Parmigiana Heros. It involves making a rich tomato sauce with onion, garlic, and red pepper flakes, then grilling eggplant slices until tender. The grilled eggplant is topped with cheese on the grill until melted. Finally, grilled hero rolls are layered with the tomato sauce, cheesy eggplant, basil, and more cheese to form hearty sandwiches.
