This recipe for **Spinach and Sorrel Spanakopita** creates savory pastry triangles. It involves sautéing spinach, scallions, and sorrel, then mixing them with feta, egg yolk, nutmeg, dill, salt, and pepper for the filling. Phyllo dough sheets are brushed with melted butter, cut into strips, filled with the mixture, and folded into triangles. These are then baked at 375°F for 20-25 minutes until golden brown. Freezing before baking is recommended for best results.
