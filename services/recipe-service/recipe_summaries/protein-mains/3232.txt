This recipe describes how to make Lobster Toasts with Avocado and Espelette Pepper, a gourmet appetizer. It involves preparing a creamy lobster salad (with mayonnaise, crème fraîche, lemon, and herbs), mashing avocado, and toasting brioche squares. The dish is assembled by spreading avocado on the toasts, topping with the lobster salad, and finishing with Espelette pepper and fresh tarragon.
