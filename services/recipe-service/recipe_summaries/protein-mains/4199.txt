This recipe features **sausage cooked with sauerkraut, apples, and bok choy**. Caraway seeds are toasted, then bok choy and apples are separately seared and reserved. Sausages are browned, then simmered with sauerkraut, broth, and the toasted caraway seeds. Finally, the reserved bok choy and apples are folded in with cider vinegar and butter, simmered briefly to finish, and served with grainy Dijon mustard.
