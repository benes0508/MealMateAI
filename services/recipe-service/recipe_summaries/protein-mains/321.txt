This recipe is for **Greek Turkey Burgers**, made by combining lean ground turkey with finely chopped spinach, black olives, lemon zest, red onion, fresh mint/parsley, garlic, oregano, salt, and pepper. The mixture is formed into six patties, then pan-fried in olive oil or ghee for 4-5 minutes per side until golden brown. They can be served on lettuce leaves with optional toppings like tomato, cucumber, pesto, or feta.
