This recipe for Roasted Potatoes and Haddock Puttanesca features roasted potatoes and shallots, along with roasted haddock (or halibut), served with a rich, savory puttanesca sauce. The sauce, made from tomatoes, garlic, anchovies, red pepper flakes, and olives, is simmered separately. Potatoes and fish are roasted simultaneously in the oven until tender and golden, then combined with the prepared sauce and garnished with capers and parsley.
