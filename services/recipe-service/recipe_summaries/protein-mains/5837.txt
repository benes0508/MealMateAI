This recipe details how to prepare a **Citrus-Sage Roast Turkey Breast with Gravy** for a small crowd. A 5-6 lb turkey breast is seasoned with a butter mixture containing fresh sage, lemon zest, and orange zest, pushed under the skin. It's roasted at 425°F then 350°F with orange and red onion wedges in the pan, until it reaches 165°F. Pan juices are then used to make a savory gravy, thickened with a roux and enriched with turkey stock.
