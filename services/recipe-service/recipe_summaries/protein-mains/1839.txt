This recipe features **Butterflied Chicken with Herbs and Cracked Olives**, preparing whole chickens by flattening and seasoning them, then chilling overnight. The chickens are rubbed with a mixture of garlic, Aleppo pepper, rosemary, and olive oil before being grilled over indirect medium-high heat until crisp and cooked through. A fresh topping of cracked Castelvetrano olives, lemon juice, oregano, and parsley is mixed with chicken juices and served over the carved chicken.
