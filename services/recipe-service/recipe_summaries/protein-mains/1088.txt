"Cold Roast Lamb with Marinated Eggplant" features a boneless leg of lamb seasoned with garlic, rosemary, and cumin, roasted to medium, and served chilled. It's accompanied by roasted Italian eggplants and shallots, which are marinated after cooking and also served cold. This make-ahead friendly dish is typically garnished with olive oil and sea salt, and can be served with Lemony Yogurt Sauce or Grilled Serrano Salsa Verde.
