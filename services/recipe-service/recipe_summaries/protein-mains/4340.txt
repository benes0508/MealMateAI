This recipe for "White Beans and Charred Broccoli with Parmesan" features large white beans simmered until tender with aromatics and Parmesan rind. Broccoli is roasted at high heat until charred. The cooked beans are then tossed with chopped charred broccoli, anchovies, lemon zest, lemon juice, and olive oil, finished with generous shavings of Parmesan.
