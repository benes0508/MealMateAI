This recipe details a **Grilled Frittata** featuring **zucchini and leeks** that are first grilled until tender. The frittata base is made with **eggs and crème fraîche**, into which the grilled vegetables are folded. It's cooked in a cast-iron skillet directly on the grill until set, then finished with dollops of **crème fraîche and harissa** on top, and garnished with **fresh basil**. It's served warm, with additional harissa on the side.
