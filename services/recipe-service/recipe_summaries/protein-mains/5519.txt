This recipe for "Grilled Radicchio and Kale, Sauerkraut Style" features grilled radicchio and kale tossed with a tangy, spiced vinaigrette. The dressing is made by cooking shallots with apple cider vinegar, juniper berries, caraway seeds, mustard seeds, and thyme, then emulsifying with oil. Both vegetables are grilled until tender and slightly charred before being combined with the dressing and allowed to meld.
