This recipe details how to prepare a **Salmon Niçoise salad**. It involves roasting salmon, boiling potatoes, crisp green beans (haricots verts), and hard-boiled eggs. These components are then assembled on a bed of frisée or mâche, along with Niçoise olives. The salad is dressed with a savory vinaigrette made from anchovies, Dijon mustard, lemon, and shallots, and finished with crispy fried capers.
