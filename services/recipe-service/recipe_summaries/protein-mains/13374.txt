This recipe is for **Grilled Shrimp Rémoulade**. Large shrimp are deveined (shells on) and grilled for 3-4 minutes until just cooked through. They are then tossed with a homemade rémoulade sauce, made from Creole mustard, white wine vinegar, oil, parsley, horseradish, pickle, scallion, paprika, cayenne, salt, and pepper. The dish can be served warm or at room temperature.
