This recipe details a **Chocolate-Nut Tart with Dried Fruit**. It features a pre-baked pie crust spread with raspberry preserves, then filled with a rich ganache of bittersweet chocolate, heavy cream, assorted toasted nuts (pecans, almonds, pistachios, hazelnuts), and dried fruits (cherries, raisins, cranberries, blueberries), seasoned with salt. The tart is garnished with toffee bits and chilled until set.
