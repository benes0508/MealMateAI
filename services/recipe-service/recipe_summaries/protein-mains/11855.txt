Katy's Dates with Ancho Chili Oil is a sweet and savory appetizer featuring Medjool dates. The dates are stuffed with a fragrant mixture of walnuts, fresh sage, garlic, and orange zest, then wrapped in thin slices of serrano ham or prosciutto. Briefly roasted, they are served drizzled with a homemade infused ancho chili oil. This dish can be prepped ahead and served warm or at room temperature.
