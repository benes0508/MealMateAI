This recipe prepares **Beets With Pecorino, Pecans, and Shishito Peppers**. Beets are roasted until tender, then tossed with red wine vinegar and olive oil. Separately, shishito peppers are blistered and pecans are toasted. The dish is assembled by combining the roasted beets, peppers, pecans, and thinly sliced red onion, then finished with a drizzle of hot chili sesame oil and grated Pecorino cheese.
