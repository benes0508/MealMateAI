This recipe for **Citrus-Marinated Chicken Thighs** involves marinating bone-in, skin-on chicken thighs in a vibrant blend of cilantro, garlic, lime and orange zests/juices, soy sauce, and scallions. After a minimum 20-minute chill, the chicken is broiled until cooked through and golden brown. It's served with a reserved portion of the fresh marinade and scallions.
