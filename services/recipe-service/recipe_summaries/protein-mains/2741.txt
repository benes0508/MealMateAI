Japanese eggplants are halved, scored, and liberally coated in a homemade paste made from Fresno chiles, garlic, lemongrass, ginger, cumin, sugar, and salt. After marinating for 10-60 minutes, the eggplants are grilled until tender and browned, then served with the reserved curry paste. The marinade can be prepared up to 3 days in advance.
