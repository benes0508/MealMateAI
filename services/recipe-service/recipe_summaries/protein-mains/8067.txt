A vibrant stir-fry featuring quinoa as its base, complemented by grilled chicken breast and a mix of fresh vegetables including carrots, red bell pepper, snow peas, ginger, and garlic. The dish incorporates an egg for texture and is seasoned with soy sauce, cilantro, and scallions for a flavorful finish. Prepared by cooking quinoa separately, stir-frying the vegetables, then combining all elements with the chicken for a quick and satisfying meal that serves 4.
