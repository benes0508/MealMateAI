This recipe for "12-Minute Saucy Chicken Breasts with Limes" features skin-on, bone-in chicken breasts seasoned with a blend of cumin, turmeric, paprika, and salt. The chicken is pan-seared skin-side down in a hot cast-iron skillet, then weighted and baked in the oven with lime halves for 10 minutes, resulting in a quick and flavorful chicken main dish.
