This recipe details how to prepare "Turkey for Two with Pan-Sauce Gravy." It involves searing a bone-in turkey breast, deglazing the pan with white wine, then roasting the turkey in the oven with herbs and garlic until cooked through. While the turkey rests, a pan gravy is made from the skillet liquids, thickened with cornstarch and enriched with butter. The dish is served with the homemade gravy.
