This recipe is for **Grilled Butterflied Leg of Lamb** marinated overnight in olive oil, Marsala (or dry red wine), garlic, rosemary, mint, salt, and pepper. The lamb is grilled until medium-rare to medium, then rested and sliced. It's served with a **Fresh Mint-Pepper Jelly** made by combining pepper jelly with fresh mint, red wine vinegar, and black pepper.
