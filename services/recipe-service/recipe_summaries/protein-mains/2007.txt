This recipe for Marinated Goat Cheese with Herbs and Spices features goat cheese pieces marinated in olive oil infused with allspice, coriander, smashed garlic, orange zest, bay leaves, star anise, and red pepper flakes. The oil is gently heated to release the aromatics, then poured over the cheese and allowed to cool. It's served with toasted baguette slices and a sprinkle of flaky sea salt, and can be prepared up to 3 hours in advance.
