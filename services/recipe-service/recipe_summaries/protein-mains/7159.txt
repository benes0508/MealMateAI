This recipe details how to make a **Grilled Cuban Sandwich (Sandwich Cubano)**. It features **Cuban bread** layered with **ham, roasted pork, Swiss cheese, dill pickles, mustard, and butter**. The sandwich is assembled, buttered on the outside, wrapped in foil, then **pressed and grilled** at high heat, followed by a second grilling unwrapped for a **crispy, golden-brown finish**.
