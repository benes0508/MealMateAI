This recipe details how to make a **Stir-Fried Chicken with Bell Peppers and Snow Cabbage**. Chicken breast is cut into matchstick strips, marinated, then quickly stir-fried and set aside. Red and green bell peppers are stir-fried until tender, followed by preserved snow cabbage. Finally, the chicken is returned to the wok and everything is stir-fried together until cooked through, finished with a touch of sesame oil.
