Spaghetti with Tomato and Walnut Pesto is a vibrant pasta dish featuring spaghetti tossed with a unique pesto. This flavorful pesto is made from toasted walnuts, broiled cherry tomatoes, anchovies, garlic, lemon zest, red pepper flakes, and Parmesan, blended with olive oil. The dish is finished with fresh basil and additional broiled tomatoes, then garnished with more Parmesan. The pesto can be prepared a day in advance.
