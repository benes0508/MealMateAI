This recipe is for **Pasta Carbonara with Cabbage and Mushrooms**. It involves cooking shiitake mushrooms and bacon until crisp, then saut√©ing savoy cabbage in the same skillet until lightly charred. Tagliatelle or linguine is cooked al dente.
