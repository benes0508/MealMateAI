This recipe is for **Chinese Hot-and-Sour Soup**, featuring a savory and tangy broth with a kick of spice. It includes thinly sliced **pork loin**, rehydrated **dried black mushrooms, tree ear mushrooms, and lily buds**, along with **bamboo shoots** and **firm tofu**. The soup is thickened with a cornstarch slurry and finished with a **thin stream of beaten eggs**, creating delicate ribbons. Seasoned with **dark and light soy sauce, red wine and rice vinegars, sugar, salt, white pepper, and sesame oil**, it's garnished with **scallions and cilantro** before serving.
