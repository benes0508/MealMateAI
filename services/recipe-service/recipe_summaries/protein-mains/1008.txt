This recipe for **Leek-Top Baking-Sheet Hash** features roasted smoked beef brisket (or a vegetarian alternative) and vegetables like fingerling potatoes, bell peppers, leeks, and garlic, seasoned and baked on a sheet pan until crisp. Separately, soft-boiled eggs are peeled, coated in dukkah, and halved. The roasted hash is then topped with the dukkah-coated eggs and fresh herbs, served optionally with harissa.
