This recipe creates "Spicy Marinated Vegetables and Sardines on Toast." It involves thinly slicing carrots, shallots, serrano chile, and radishes, then marinating them in a mixture of toasted coriander and fennel seeds, sherry vinegar, and olive oil. Baguette pieces are toasted, rubbed with garlic, spread with mayonnaise, and topped with drained sardines and the marinated vegetable mixture, finished with lemon zest.
