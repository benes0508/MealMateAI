This recipe details how to prepare **Asparagus with Roasted-Garlic Aïoli**. It involves roasting whole garlic heads until tender, then puréeing them with mayonnaise, apple cider vinegar, salt, pepper, and chives to create the aïoli. Asparagus stalks are peeled, boiled until crisp-tender, then quickly cooled. The dish is served by pairing the chilled asparagus with the homemade roasted-garlic aïoli.
