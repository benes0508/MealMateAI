This recipe details how to make **Fresh Pasta With Clams And Hot Italian Sausage**. It involves preparing homemade fresh pasta from scratch, then creating a flavorful sauce by browning hot Italian sausage, adding garlic, clams, and white wine until the clams open. The freshly cooked pasta is then combined with the clam-sausage sauce, butter, and parsley, finishing with reserved pasta water to coat.
