This recipe is for **Grilled Brined Vegetables**, featuring carrots, beets, or radishes. Vegetables are first simmered (or soaked for radishes) in a tangy brine made with vinegar, salt, and spices. They are then tossed with oil and grilled until lightly charred. Serve with optional Green Chile Charmoula, Spiced Seed Sprinkle, or Grilled Halloumi.
