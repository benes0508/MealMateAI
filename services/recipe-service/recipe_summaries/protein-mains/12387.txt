This recipe features **grilled pork chops** served with a **chunky, homemade barbecue sauce**. The distinctive sauce is made with diced **andouille sausage**, onion, tomato, balsamic vinegar, dark brown sugar, chili powder, and cumin, giving it a smoky, tangy, and subtly sweet flavor. The pork chops are seasoned simply with chili powder and cumin before grilling.
