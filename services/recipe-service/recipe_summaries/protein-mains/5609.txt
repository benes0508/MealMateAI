This recipe details how to make Chawan Mushi, a savory Japanese steamed egg custard. It features a base of dashi, mirin, and soy sauce, combined with eggs, and incorporates steamed shrimp, spring peas, and shiitake mushrooms. The custard is poured over the vegetables in individual heatproof bowls, steamed until set, and then garnished with a separate dashi sauce, reserved peas, and shrimp.
