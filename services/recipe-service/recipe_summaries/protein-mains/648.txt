This recipe details how to make **Grilled Chicken with Lemon and Thyme**. Bone-in, skin-on chicken breasts are seasoned, grilled using a two-zone method until cooked through, then pulled from the bone and sliced. It's served shingled on a platter, garnished with grilled and fresh lemon slices, thyme sprigs, Castelvetrano olives, and drizzled with a lemon-oil mixture.
