Roasted Chicken Thighs with Lemon and Oregano: This recipe features pan-seared, then oven-roasted chicken thighs with lemon slices for crispy skin and caramelized flavor. A simple pan sauce is made from wine, chicken broth, shallots, garlic, oregano, and red pepper flakes, finished with lemon juice. Served with the caramelized lemon slices.
