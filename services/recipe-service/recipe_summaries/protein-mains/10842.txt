This recipe details **Grilled Lamb and Fig Skewers** served with **Grilled Eggplant**. The lamb and fresh figs are seasoned with cumin, coriander, garlic, salt, and pepper, then threaded onto skewers and grilled. A sweet and savory **Mint-Pepper Glaze**, made from apricot jam, red wine vinegar, red pepper flakes, lemon zest, and fresh mint, is brushed onto the lamb during the final stage of grilling. Separately, sliced eggplant is oiled, seasoned, and grilled until tender.
