This recipe is for **Braised Chicken with Artichokes and Fava Beans**. It involves searing chicken and artichokes until browned, then braising them with shallots, white wine, white wine vinegar, and chicken broth in a Dutch oven until the chicken is cooked through. Freshly prepared fava beans are added at the end, and the dish is finished with fresh chives, parsley, and sea salt.
