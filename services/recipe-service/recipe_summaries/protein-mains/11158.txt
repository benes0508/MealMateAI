This recipe details a rich **Smoked Turkey and Bacon Chowder** featuring a creamy, pureed base of vegetables (potatoes, onions, celery, carrots, garlic, tomato paste) and smoked turkey. It's garnished with crispy bacon, pearl onions, halved Brussels sprouts, fresh sage, and served with unique pumpernickel and cranberry croutons. The chowder can be made ahead.
