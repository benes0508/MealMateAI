This recipe is for Herb-Roasted Pork Loin, featuring a boneless pork loin roast seasoned and browned, then roasted with an assortment of fresh herbs (rosemary, thyme, sage, savory). It's coated with a mixture of shallots, garlic, and Dijon mustard before roasting. The recipe concludes with a pan sauce made from the deglazed roasting pan, vermouth, mustard, and chicken broth, thickened with a roux.
