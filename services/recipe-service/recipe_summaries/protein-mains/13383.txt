This recipe features **Jerk Pork served atop Black-Eyed-Pea Cakes and Red Pepper Mayo**. The cakes are made from mashed and whole black-eyed peas mixed with sautéed red peppers, garlic, breadcrumbs, cilantro, and eggs, then formed into patties, coated in cornmeal, pan-fried, and finished in the oven. The creamy Red Pepper Mayo is prepared by blending a portion of the sautéed red pepper and garlic mixture with mayonnaise. The dish is assembled by topping the warm black-eyed-pea cakes with the red pepper mayo and jerk pork.
