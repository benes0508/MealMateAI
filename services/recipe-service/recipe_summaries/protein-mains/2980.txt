This recipe features Chipotle Pork Loin served with a fresh Blueberry–Kiwi Salsa. The pork loin is seasoned with a spice rub including paprika/chipotle, turmeric, cumin, salt, and pepper, then grilled or roasted to 145°F. The accompanying salsa is a mix of diced kiwis, chopped blueberries, cilantro, red onion, balsamic vinegar, and salt.
