This recipe for "Baked Pasta With Merguez and Harissa-Spiked Sauce" combines spicy merguez sausage with a vibrant harissa-spiked tomato sauce, al dente pasta, and crumbled feta. The dish is prepared by browning the merguez, building a sauce with onion, garlic, spices (caraway, cumin), crushed tomatoes, and harissa, then mixing it with partially cooked pasta and sliced sausage before baking until golden and bubbling.
