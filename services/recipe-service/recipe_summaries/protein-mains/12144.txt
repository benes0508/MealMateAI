This recipe details how to make **Chinese Egg Rolls** filled with a savory mixture of **shrimp, Chinese roast pork (char siu), shiitake mushrooms, carrots, celery, scallions, ginger, and garlic**, seasoned with **oyster sauce, soy sauce, and sesame oil**. The filling is stir-fried, cooled, then wrapped tightly in **egg roll wrappers** and sealed with egg. The egg rolls are then **deep-fried** until golden brown and crispy, and kept warm in the oven.
