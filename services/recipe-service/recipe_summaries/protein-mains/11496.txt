Spiced Kurobuta Pork Chops features premium Kurobuta Berkshire bone-in rib pork chops seasoned with a distinctive blend of Aleppo pepper, Hungarian sweet paprika, Malabar black peppercorns, and fresh sage. The chops are floured, seared, and then finished by baking in the oven. They are served alongside a unique Braised Bacon, Pomegranate, and Pine Nut Relish.
