This recipe details **Poached Chicken, Crunchy Vegetables, and Herb Dressing**. It involves gently **poaching chicken breasts** and combining them with a variety of prepared vegetables: blanched green beans, thinly shaved fennel, carrot, and zucchini, and lightly broiled cherry tomatoes. The dish is served with a **fresh herb and almond dressing** (cilantro, basil, parsley, tarragon, garlic, olive oil) and lemon wedges, resulting in a light and flavorful meal.
