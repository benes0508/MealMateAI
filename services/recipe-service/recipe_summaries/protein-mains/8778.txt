This recipe details how to prepare **Spicy Black Beans with Chorizo and Chipotle Cream**. It involves soaking and simmering black beans until tender, then combining them with browned chorizo, sautéed onions, garlic, jalapeño, and spices. The dish is topped with a creamy, zesty sauce made from sour cream, chipotle hot pepper sauce, and lime juice, then garnished with fresh cilantro. Both the beans and the cream can be prepared a day in advance.
