This recipe details how to make **Instant Pot Lemon Chicken With Garlic and Olives**. Bone-in, skin-on chicken thighs are browned, then pressure cooked with chicken stock, mixed olives, lemon slices, garlic, and herbs like rosemary, thyme, and fennel seeds. An optional slow-cook method is also provided. The dish is finished by thickening the sauce and serving it over the chicken.
