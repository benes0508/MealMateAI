This recipe details how to make **Twice-Baked Sweet Potatoes with Bacon-Sesame Brittle**. It involves first preparing a unique sweet and savory bacon-sesame brittle. Then, sweet potatoes are roasted, their flesh is scooped out and mashed with eggs, butter, white miso, and ginger, and then re-stuffed into the potato skins. Finally, these filled potatoes are baked until golden and topped with the crunchy brittle and fresh scallions.
