This recipe details **Slow-Roasted Salmon with Fennel, Citrus, and Chiles**. A skinless salmon fillet is slow-roasted at 275°F with thinly sliced fennel, blood/navel oranges, lemons, and red fresno/jalapeño chiles. Seasoned with dill, salt, pepper, and olive oil, the dish cooks for 30-40 minutes until the salmon is tender and medium-rare, offering bright, aromatic, and subtly spicy flavors.
