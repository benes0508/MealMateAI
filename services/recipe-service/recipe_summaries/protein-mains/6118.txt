This recipe details a method for **Chicken with Herb-Roasted Tomatoes and Pan Sauce**. Chicken breasts are seared and then roasted until cooked through. Simultaneously, cherry tomatoes are roasted with olive oil and herbes de Provence until bursting, then tossed with Worcestershire sauce. A pan sauce is made by deglazing the chicken skillet with red wine vinegar, adding the roasted tomatoes and their juices, and simmering with saut√©ed shallots. The dish is served with sliced chicken topped with the tomato sauce and fresh herbs.
