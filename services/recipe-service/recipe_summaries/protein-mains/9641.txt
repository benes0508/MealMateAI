This recipe is for **Yangzhou Fried Rice**, a classic Chinese stir-fry. It features cooked long-grain white rice combined with shrimp, eggs, shiitake mushrooms, peas, and ham, all stir-fried with scallions, oyster sauce, sugar, salt, and white pepper. The dish is cooked in a wok, first stir-frying the aromatics and shrimp, then incorporating the eggs and rice, and finally adding the remaining ingredients and seasonings. It's garnished with fresh scallions.
