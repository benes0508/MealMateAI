This recipe details how to prepare "Cold Paprika-Grilled Chicken with Marinated Bell Peppers." Bone-in chicken pieces are seasoned with paprika, salt, and pepper, then grilled until charred and cooked through. Bell peppers are grilled, blackened, peeled, and marinated in a Master Vegetable Marinade with oregano. Both the chicken and peppers are cooled, then tossed together and served at room temperature, optionally with Lemony Yogurt Sauce or Grilled Serrano Salsa Verde. The dish can be prepared a day in advance.
