Bitter Greens with Sautéed Corn & Shallots is a vibrant dish featuring bitter greens (dandelion or arugula) tossed with a warm dressing made from crispy guanciale (or bacon/pancetta), sautéed corn, and shallots, all brightened with sherry or red wine vinegar. It's garnished with sweet Medjool dates, shaved Parmesan, and fresh chives.
