This recipe for Seared Salmon with Winter Vegetables and Kombu Broth features salmon fillets seared until crispy and cooked rare, which then finish cooking in a hot, flavorful broth. The broth is prepared by simmering browned onion with kombu, ginger, shallots, turnips, and butternut squash, enhanced with dry vermouth. The dish is served with turnip greens and a squeeze of fresh lemon. The broth and vegetables can be made a day in advance, adding greens just before serving.
