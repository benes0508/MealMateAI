This recipe details a rich **Beef Chili** featuring boneless beef chuck. It uses a homemade purée from rehydrated ancho, guajillo, and pasilla chiles for depth. Other key ingredients include onions, garlic, cumin, oregano, fire-roasted crushed tomatoes, brown sugar, and lager beer, finished with apple cider vinegar. The beef is browned, then slow-simmered for 1.5-2 hours with the chile purée and other aromatics until tender. It's designed to be served with various toppings like sour cream, cheese, and Fritos. The chili can also be prepared up to 4 days in advance.
