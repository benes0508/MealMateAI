This recipe details **Braised Veal Breast with Herbs, Pernod, and Tomatoes**. Veal ribs are seasoned and browned, then simmered with onions, garlic, shallots, and anchovies in a liquid base of white wine, Pernod, diced tomatoes, and chicken broth, along with fresh thyme, sage, and tarragon. Pearl onions and green olives are added later. The dish is slow-cooked until tender, then the sauce is thickened and finished with grated lemon peel. It's a rich, savory main course with anise and herbal notes.
