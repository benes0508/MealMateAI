"Chili of Forgiveness" is a hearty chili featuring ground dark meat turkey (or pork), whole peeled tomatoes, butternut squash, cannellini beans, and Tuscan kale, seasoned with onion, garlic, rosemary, sage, and crushed red pepper. The recipe involves browning the meat and aromatics, simmering with tomatoes, then adding squash, beans, and kale until tender. It's served topped with grated Parmesan cheese and can be refrigerated for up to a week or frozen for up to two months.
