This recipe details a Sweet Potato and Poblano Salad with Honey and Rosemary. It involves preparing a honey-rosemary vinaigrette, par-boiling and chilling sweet potato wedges, then grilling them until marked. The grilled sweet potatoes are combined with diced poblano chiles, green onions, and parsley, then tossed with the prepared dressing. The salad can be served warm or at room temperature and can be made up to a day in advance.
