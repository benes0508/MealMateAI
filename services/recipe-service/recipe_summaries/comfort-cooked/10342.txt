This recipe provides instructions for preparing a **Rib Eye Roast**. Season a boneless prime rib eye roast with salt and pepper, then roast at **350°F**. Cook until a meat thermometer reaches **130°F for rare** (approx. 2h 20m) or **140-145°F for medium-rare**. Finish by letting the roast **rest for 15 minutes** before carving.
