This recipe details how to prepare a **Roast Leg of Lamb with Mint, Garlic, and Lima Bean Purée**. A butterflied boneless leg of lamb is seasoned internally with a rub of olive oil, fresh mint, parsley, rosemary, and garlic, then rolled and tied. It's roasted at 450°F for approximately 1 hour 15 minutes until it reaches 130°F for medium-rare. After resting, the lamb is sliced and served, typically garnished with mint sprigs and accompanied by Lima Bean Purée.
