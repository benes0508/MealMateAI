This recipe is for a hearty Three-Bean Soup featuring dried lima beans, Romano beans, and sugar snap peas. It starts with a savory base of bacon, leeks, fennel, celery, and garlic, simmered with thyme and bay leaf. The soup is finished with crème fraîche and lemon juice for creaminess and brightness. It's served with a fresh dill, lemon, and fennel frond pesto, shaved Parmesan, and garlic-rubbed crusty bread.
