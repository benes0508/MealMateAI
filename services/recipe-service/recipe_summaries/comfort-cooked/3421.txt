A sophisticated Seafood Stew for Two, featuring a rich, homemade broth made from prawn shells, white wine, and tomatoes, infused with fennel, leek, and garlic. It combines a diverse selection of seafood, including prawns, snapper (seared separately for crispy skin), sea scallops (also seared), squid, cockles, and mussels. Served with crusty baguette and fresh tarragon.
