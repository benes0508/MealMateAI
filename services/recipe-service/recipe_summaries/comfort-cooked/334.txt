This recipe for **Cauliflower Bolognese** transforms humble vegetables into a hearty, rich pasta dish. It features finely pulsed mushrooms and rice-sized cauliflower florets cooked down with onions, garlic, chile, rosemary, and double-concentrated tomato paste to create a flavorful, textured sauce. The sauce is then combined with rigatoni, Parmesan, and butter, finishing with fresh parsley and lemon zest for a vibrant, comforting meal.
