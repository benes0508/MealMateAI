This recipe details how to prepare a **Lemon-and Prosciutto-Stuffed Pork Loin Roast with Broccolini**. A boneless pork loin is butterflied, filled with prosciutto, thinly sliced lemon, panko, and chives, then rolled, tied, and roasted to a tender finish. Broccolini is blanched then roasted until tender, and a flavorful pan sauce made from the roasting drippings, chicken broth, and white wine accompanies the meal.
