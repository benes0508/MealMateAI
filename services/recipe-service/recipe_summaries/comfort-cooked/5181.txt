This recipe for Seafood en Brodo features a rich, homemade broth made from roasted fish bones and vegetables, enhanced with saffron, leeks, and vermouth. It includes clams (or cockles) and firm white fish fillets (like halibut or cod), gently cooked in the flavorful broth. The dish is finished with a vibrant, fresh tarragon pesto and typically served with toasted country bread. Both the brodo and pesto can be prepared in advance.
