This recipe details how to make **Ecuadoran Potato Cakes with Peanut Sauce**.

The **potato cakes** are made from mashed Yukon gold potatoes mixed with sautéed scallions and grated Münster cheese, then formed into patties and pan-fried until crusty.

The **peanut sauce** is prepared by cooking garlic, scallions, cumin, and tomato, then adding milk and crunchy peanut butter, stirring until smooth.

The dish is served with the warm peanut sauce accompanying the crispy potato cakes.
