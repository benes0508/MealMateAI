This recipe for **Black Bean Chili with Crispy Pork and Poblano Salsa** features a rich, long-simmered black bean chili flavored with New Mexico chili powder, cumin, oregano, chipotle chiles, and a hint of orange peel. It's served topped with tender, pan-braised crispy country-style pork ribs, a vibrant charred poblano chile and salsa verde salsa, a creamy chipotle sour cream, and grated Monterey Jack cheese.
