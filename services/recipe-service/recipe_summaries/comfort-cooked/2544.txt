This recipe is for a **Slow-Cooker White Chicken Chili** made with boneless skinless chicken thighs, white beans, green chiles, corn, and a blend of spices. It can be prepared immediately or as a freezer meal, cooked in a slow cooker for 4 hours on high or 6-8 hours on low. Beans and corn are added in the final 30 minutes. The chili is typically served with sour cream, cilantro, lime wedges, and cornbread. It can be made up to 3 days ahead or frozen for up to 3 months.
