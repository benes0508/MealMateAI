This recipe is for **Beer-Braised Pork Belly**, featuring skin-on boneless pork belly scored and seared, then slow-braised for 4-5 hours in the oven with stout or porter, apple juice, onion, carrots, star anise, cloves, coriander, fennel, and dried apricots. After braising, the pork skin is crisped at a higher temperature. A rich sauce is made from the reduced braising liquid, finished with brown butter, shallot, and lemon juice. It's served with the sauce, vegetables, and fresh horseradish.
