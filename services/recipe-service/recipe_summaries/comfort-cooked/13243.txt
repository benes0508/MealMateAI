This recipe features roasted pork tenderloin served with a vibrant homemade spiced rhubarb chutney. The chutney combines fresh rhubarb, red onion, and dried cherries with a blend of ginger, cinnamon, cloves, cumin, and red pepper. The pork is seared for browning, then roasted while being basted with the sweet-savory chutney, resulting in a flavorful main course.
