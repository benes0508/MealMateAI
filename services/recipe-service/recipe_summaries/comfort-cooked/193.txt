Khara Huggi or Pongal is a savory Indian rice and lentil porridge. Basmati rice and moong dal (or red lentils) are cooked together with turmeric, ghee, and ginger. A separate tempering of ghee, fried cashews, cumin, black peppercorns, and asafoetida is prepared and mixed into the dish along with shredded coconut and salt, then garnished with cashews.
