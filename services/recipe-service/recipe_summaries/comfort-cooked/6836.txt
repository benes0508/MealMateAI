This recipe details how to prepare an **Herb Roasted Turkey**, featuring a 17-pound turkey seasoned with a compound butter made from parsley, sage, rosemary, and thyme, plus salt and pepper. The turkey is roasted with carrots, celery, and onions, and basted with chicken stock. It starts at 450°F for 45 minutes, then reduces to 350°F for about 2.5 hours until the internal temperature reaches 160°F. After resting, the pan drippings are reduced to a sauce for drizzling.
