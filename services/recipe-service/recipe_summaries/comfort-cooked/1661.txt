This recipe is for a **Grain Salad with Olives and Whole-Lemon Vinaigrette**. It features toasted and cooked farro (or spelt) combined with a unique warm vinaigrette made from coarsely crushed green olives, finely chopped whole lemon (including peel and flesh), and shallots, warmed in olive oil. Fresh mint/cilantro and parsley are folded in before serving. It can be prepared ahead, making it suitable for quick meals.
