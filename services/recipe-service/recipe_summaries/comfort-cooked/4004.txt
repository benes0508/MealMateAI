This recipe for "Double-Dark Chicken Noodle Soup" features a rich, deep amber broth made from browned chicken wings and aromatics (leek, onion, carrots, celery, garlic, tomato paste), simmered for up to 2 hours. The strained stock is then used to cook chicken thighs, fresh carrots, and pearl onions, with egg noodles added at the end. It's served garnished with fresh chervil.
