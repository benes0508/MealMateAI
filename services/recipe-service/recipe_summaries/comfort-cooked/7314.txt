This recipe for **Pork Chile Verde with Red Chile Salsa** features cubes of pork shoulder slow-simmered in a vibrant salsa verde. The salsa verde is prepared from pureed tomatillos, green onions, cilantro, garlic, and chicken broth. After browning the pork, it's cooked with onions, cumin, green chiles, and oregano for two hours until tender, then Yukon Gold potatoes are added for the final 30 minutes. The stew can be made ahead and is served topped with fresh cilantro and a dollop of red chile salsa.
