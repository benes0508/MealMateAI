This recipe is for **Pumpkin-Turkey "Ghoulash" with Caraway Noodles**. It's a hearty stew featuring seasoned turkey thighs, onions, and potatoes simmered with pumpkin, chicken broth, and paprika until tender. The "ghoulash" is served over buttered egg noodles seasoned with caraway seeds, garnished with fresh thyme, and accompanied by sour cream.
