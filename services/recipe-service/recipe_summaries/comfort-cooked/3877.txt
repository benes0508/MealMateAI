This recipe details how to make "Our Favorite Spaghetti and Meatballs," featuring tender meatballs made from a blend of beef, pork, and veal, moistened bread, and savory seasonings. These are browned and then simmered in a rich, long-cooked tomato sauce, infused with garlic, herbs, and a hint of spice. The dish is served over al dente spaghetti and garnished with Parmesan and fresh parsley.
