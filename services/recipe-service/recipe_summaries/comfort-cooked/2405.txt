This recipe details how to make **Mashed Baked Potatoes with Chives**. It involves baking whole russet and Yukon gold potatoes along with a foil-wrapped head of garlic until tender. The flesh of the potatoes and roasted garlic are then scooped into a pan, mashed, and combined with warm half-and-half and melted butter until smooth. The dish is seasoned with salt and pepper and topped with fresh chives. An option for making ahead is provided, allowing the potatoes to be prepared up to 3 hours in advance and reheated before serving.
