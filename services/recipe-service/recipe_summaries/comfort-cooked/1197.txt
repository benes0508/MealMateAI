This recipe is for **Big-Batch Black Beans**, made with 2 lbs of dried black beans, onion, garlic, bay leaves, salt, oregano, and cumin. The beans are soaked for an hour, then simmered with the other ingredients for 1.5-2 hours until tender and creamy. This recipe yields a large quantity that can be refrigerated for up to 5 days or frozen for 3 months.
