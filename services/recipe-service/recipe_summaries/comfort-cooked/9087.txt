This recipe details a rich Mashed-Potato Casserole with Gouda and Bacon. Russet potatoes are boiled, mashed with sour cream, milk, and butter, then combined with smoked Gouda cheese and a mixture of crispy bacon and green onions. The casserole is baked until bubbly and golden, then topped with more bacon before serving. It can be prepared up to 8 hours in advance.
