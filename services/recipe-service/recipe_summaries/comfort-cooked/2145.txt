This recipe for "Herb Rice with Green Garlic, Saffron, and Crispy Shallots" features basmati rice layered with a generous mix of fresh herbs (cilantro, dill, parsley, mint, tarragon) and green garlic. The rice is par-boiled, then layered in a pot over a lavash or romaine base with saffron-infused shallot oil, and steamed until fluffy. It's served with crispy fried shallots, reserved herbs, and the crispy lavash crust from the bottom of the pot.
