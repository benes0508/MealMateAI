This recipe details how to make **Our Favorite Creamy Mashed Potatoes**, a rich and smooth side dish. It uses Yukon Gold potatoes, garlic, whole milk, butter, thyme, and sour cream. Key steps involve boiling potatoes and garlic until tender, warming the milk mixture, pressing the potatoes and garlic through a ricer or food mill, then gradually incorporating the milk mixture and sour cream. The recipe emphasizes not overmixing to prevent gumminess and includes instructions for making ahead.
