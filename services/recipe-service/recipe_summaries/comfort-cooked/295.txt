This recipe details how to prepare a Coconut Cod Chowder featuring cod fillets, baby Yukon Gold potatoes, leeks, garlic, ginger, and serrano chile simmered in coconut milk with aromatic spices like turmeric, coriander, and cardamom. The chowder is finished with fresh lime juice and served topped with savory, ghee-toasted oyster crackers seasoned with paprika.
