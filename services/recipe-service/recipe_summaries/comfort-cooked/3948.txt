This recipe for "Smashed Twice-Cooked Potatoes with Leeks and Green Garlic" involves first steaming Yukon gold potatoes until tender, then smashing them flat. The potatoes are then pan-fried in olive oil until crispy and browned, with leeks and green garlic (or regular garlic) added and cooked until golden and soft. The dish is finished with fresh lemon zest and juice, salt, and pepper.
