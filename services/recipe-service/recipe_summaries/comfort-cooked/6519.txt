This recipe details how to prepare a **Rosemary-Crumb Beef Tenderloin with Pancetta-Roasted Tomatoes**. A trimmed beef tenderloin is seasoned, seared, then coated with a Dijon-rosemary mixture and Panko breadcrumbs before roasting to medium-rare. Alongside, grape tomatoes, garlic, and Kalamata olives are roasted with crispy pancetta, then deglazed to create a flavorful sauce. The sliced tenderloin is served with this rich tomato-pancetta accompaniment.
