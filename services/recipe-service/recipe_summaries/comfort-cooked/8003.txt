This recipe is for **Sichuan Beef Noodle Soup with Pickled Mustard Greens**. It features tender beef shank simmered in a rich, spicy broth flavored with Sichuan chili bean paste, Sichuan peppercorns, star anise, ginger, garlic, and Chinese rock sugar. The dish is served over Chinese wheat noodles, with optional baby bok choy, and garnished with fresh cilantro, green onions, and pickled mustard greens for a complete, aromatic, and flavorful meal.
