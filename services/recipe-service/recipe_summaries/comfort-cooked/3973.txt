This recipe details how to make **Chipotle Roast Chicken Tacos**. A whole roasting chicken is seasoned with a flavorful butter mixture (chipotle, cilantro, oregano, rosemary, coriander) spread under the skin and rubbed on the exterior. It's roasted with onions and garlic until tender. The chicken meat is then served in warm corn tortillas with the roasted onions, garlic, sliced avocado, and drizzled with reduced pan juices.
