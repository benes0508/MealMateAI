This recipe details a Rolled Pork Loin Roast Stuffed With Olives and Herbs. A boneless pork loin is butterflied, seasoned, and filled with a mixture of finely chopped olives, capers, garlic, rosemary, sage, thyme, and parsley. The roast is then rolled, tied, seared on all sides, and roasted at 425Â°F for about 20 minutes. After resting for 10 minutes, it's sliced and served.
