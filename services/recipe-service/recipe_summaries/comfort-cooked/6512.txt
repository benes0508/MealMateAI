This recipe details a Slow-Cooked Venison dish, featuring a venison shoulder seared in bacon drippings, then braised with diced onions, carrots, celery, tomatoes, dried mushrooms, garlic, beef broth, and red wine. Enhanced with fresh thyme, rosemary, and bay leaves, the venison is slow-cooked until tender, then shredded and served over Louisiana rice, pasta, or mashed potatoes.
