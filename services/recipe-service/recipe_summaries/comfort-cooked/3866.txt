This recipe details how to prepare Fettuccine with Asparagus, Beet Green Pesto, and Poached Egg. It involves making a unique pesto from toasted walnuts, beet greens, garlic, parsley, lemon, and Parmesan, cooking fettuccine and shaved asparagus, and then tossing them with the pesto sauce. The dish is finished with perfectly poached eggs on top.
