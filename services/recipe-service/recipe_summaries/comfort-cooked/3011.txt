This recipe for **Braised Lamb Shanks With Fish Sauce** features lamb shanks marinated in fish sauce and garlic, then browned and braised with shallots, cilantro stems, chiles, lime zest/kaffir lime leaves, and bay leaves in chicken stock. Fennel and carrots are added later in the braising process. The dish is cooked until the lamb is fall-off-the-bone tender, and served with the braising liquid, fresh herbs (cilantro, basil, celery leaves), and an optional touch more fish sauce.
