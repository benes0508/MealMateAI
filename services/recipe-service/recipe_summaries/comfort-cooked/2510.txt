This recipe is for **Stewed Cannellini Beans with Chiles and Thyme**. It features cannellini beans simmered with aromatic vegetables (onion, fennel, carrot, garlic), thyme, and chiles in olive oil and water until creamy (50-65 minutes). The dish is seasoned with salt and pepper and served with a drizzle of olive oil. It can be prepared up to 3 days in advance.
