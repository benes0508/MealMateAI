This is a vegetarian One-Pot Curried Cauliflower with Couscous and Chickpeas. It features cauliflower, red onion, garlic, chickpeas, pearled couscous, and red lentils simmered in a spiced vegetable broth. The dish is finished with fresh spinach and served with a tangy lime-yogurt-cilantro sauce. It's designed for a single pot and can be partially prepared ahead of time.
