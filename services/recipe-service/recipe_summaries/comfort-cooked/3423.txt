This recipe for Green Posole with Cod and Cilantro features a vibrant tomatillo and cilantro base. It involves sautéing aromatics, simmering cod and hominy in a tomatillo-clam juice broth, and finishing with fresh tomatillo-cilantro purée for a bright, flavorful stew. Garnished with radishes, cilantro, and chiles, and served with lime.
