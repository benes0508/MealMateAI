This recipe details how to make **Smoked Haddock Chowder**. It involves poaching **smoked haddock** fillets, then simmering **leeks, celery, and Yukon Gold potatoes** in the reserved poaching liquid, thickened with some mashed potatoes. Finally, the flaked haddock, **heavy cream**, and chives are stirred in before serving with common crackers. An alternative method using smoked whitefish and bacon is also provided.
