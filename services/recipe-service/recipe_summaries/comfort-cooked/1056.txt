This recipe describes how to make **Spaghetti Sauce Chicken Afritada**, a Filipino-style chicken stew. It involves browning bone-in chicken pieces with saut√©ed garlic and onions, then simmering them with potatoes, carrots, chicken broth, spaghetti sauce, soy sauce, sugar, black pepper, and bay leaves.
