This recipe details how to prepare Zamosc Gefilte Fish, a traditional Jewish dish. It involves making a savory stock from fish bones and vegetables, then preparing fish patties from ground carp, whitefish, and pike blended with onions, eggs, matzah meal, and seasonings. The patties are simmered in the stock, then chilled along with the strained, jellied stock, and served with horseradish and carrots.
