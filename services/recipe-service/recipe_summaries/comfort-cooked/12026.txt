This recipe is for a hearty **Winter Vegetable and Beef Soup**. It features **boneless beef chuck roast** browned and then simmered with a variety of vegetables including **carrots, onion, garlic, green cabbage, Yukon gold potatoes, corn, and peas**. The soup base is made with **beef broth** and **diced tomatoes**, seasoned with bay leaves and fresh thyme/marjoram, resulting in a tender and flavorful dish.
