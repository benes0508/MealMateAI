This recipe is for a **Chunky Red Chili** featuring both ground beef and beef stew meat, cooked with onions, green bell pepper, plum tomatoes, tomato sauce, kidney beans, and black beans. It's seasoned with chili powder, cumin, basil, Worcestershire, and a touch of brown sugar. The meats are browned first, then simmered with the other ingredients for about 2 hours until the stew meat is tender. It's served warm, optionally with tortilla chips.
