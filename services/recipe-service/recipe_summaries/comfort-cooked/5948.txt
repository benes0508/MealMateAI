This recipe features **mashed root vegetables** (parsnips, kohlrabi, celery root, turnips, rutabagas) steamed until tender, then combined with a rich **bacon vinaigrette**. The vinaigrette is made from apple cider vinegar, plumped mustard seeds, brown sugar, and rendered bacon fat, along with crispy bacon bits and caramelized onions. The dish is seasoned with salt and pepper, can be prepared a day ahead, and is garnished with fresh parsley.
