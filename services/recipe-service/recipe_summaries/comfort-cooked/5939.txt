This recipe describes how to make **Whipped Potatoes with Horseradish**, a flavorful side dish. Potatoes are boiled until tender, then mashed and beaten with butter, cream cheese, horseradish, and milk until light and fluffy. Scallions are stirred in, and the mixture is transferred to a baking dish, topped with butter and paprika, then baked until heated through and golden. It can be prepared a day in advance.
