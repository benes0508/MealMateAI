This recipe details **Mussels in Lager**, a dish featuring fresh mussels steamed in a savory, aromatic broth. The base of the broth is made with butter, lager beer, onion, celery, diced tomatoes, garlic, and herbs (thyme, bay leaf). After the mussels open, heavy cream, Dijon mustard, and fresh parsley are whisked into the hot broth to create a rich, flavorful sauce. It is recommended to serve with crusty bread for dipping.
