This recipe for Hamburger Soup features ground beef browned with onion, celery, and garlic, then combined with whole tomatoes, bell peppers, carrots, red potatoes, beef broth, tomato paste, and various seasonings (salt, pepper, parsley, oregano, cayenne). The soup is brought to a boil and simmered until the potatoes are tender, with options to adjust consistency using extra beef broth. It can be made ahead and stored for up to 3 days.
