This recipe details how to make a Caesar Potato Salad with Sugar Snap Peas. It involves steaming sliced fingerling or baby potatoes and sugar snap peas until tender, then combining them with radishes and red onion. A dressing is prepared from olive oil, lemon juice, Dijon mustard, pressed garlic, and Parmesan cheese, seasoned with salt and pepper, and then tossed with the vegetables. The salad can be served warm or at room temperature.
