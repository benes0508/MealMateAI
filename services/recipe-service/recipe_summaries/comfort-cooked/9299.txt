This recipe for "Pork Roast with Winter Fruits and Port Sauce" features a bone-in pork loin roast stuffed with a sweet and savory mixture of dried apricots, prunes, apples, onion, and shallot. The roast is wrapped in bacon and roasted until tender. A rich sauce is then prepared from the pan drippings, Port wine, shallots, and reserved fruit stuffing, thickened with arrowroot, to accompany the carved pork.
