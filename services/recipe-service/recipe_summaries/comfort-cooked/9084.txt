This recipe for **Lamb and Eggplant Shepherd's Pie** features a rich, savory lamb and eggplant filling topped with cheesy mashed potatoes. The lamb is browned and then slow-simmered with onions, garlic, wine, diced tomatoes, and broth until tender. Eggplant is salted, saut√©ed, and then folded into the thickened lamb gravy. The dish is topped with mashed russet potatoes enriched with butter, milk, and kasseri cheese, then baked until golden and bubbling.
