This recipe details how to prepare tender **Braised Chile-Spiced Short Ribs with Black Beans**. Beef short ribs are browned and then slowly braised in a rich, aromatic sauce made with dried ancho chiles, chipotles in adobo, onion, garlic, tomato paste, molasses, and a blend of spices including cumin, cloves, and cinnamon. Separately, black beans are simmered until tender. The dish is served with the beans and garnished with fresh chopped onion and cilantro.
