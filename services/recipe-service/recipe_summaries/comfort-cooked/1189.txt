This Instant Pot recipe for Hidden Cauliflower Mac 'n' Cheese features whole wheat macaroni and cauliflower, which is mashed into the sauce for a "hidden" vegetable boost. It uses extra sharp cheddar and parmesan cheese, with unique flavor notes from soy sauce/tamari and spicy brown mustard. The dish cooks quickly under high pressure in 3 minutes, followed by a 10-minute natural release.
