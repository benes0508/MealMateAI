This recipe details how to make a **Spicy Curry Noodle Soup with Chicken and Sweet Potato**. It involves creating a flavorful, warming broth by saut√©ing aromatics (shallots, garlic, lemongrass, ginger) with Thai yellow curry paste, curry powder, and chili paste, then simmering with coconut milk, chicken broth, fish sauce, and sugar. Separately, snow peas, sweet potato, and rice vermicelli noodles are cooked. The dish is assembled by simmering thinly sliced chicken in the broth, adding the sweet potato, and serving over noodles and snow peas. It's garnished with fresh red and green onions, cilantro, chiles, and lime wedges.
