This recipe details how to prepare a **Roasted Double Rack of Pork** with a Morel Mushroom Pan Sauce. Two bone-in pork racks are seasoned with an aromatic paste of garlic, shallots, sage, rosemary, olive oil, salt, and pepper. The racks are interlocked and roasted at 350°F until an internal temperature of 140°F is reached (approx. 1 hour 20 minutes). After resting, the pork is cut into chops and served with the pan sauce.
