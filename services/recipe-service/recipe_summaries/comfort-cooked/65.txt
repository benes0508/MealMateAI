A potato salad with a sour cream and onion flavor profile, featuring heavily salted, slightly crushed baby Yukon Gold potatoes. The creamy dressing blends sour cream, mayonnaise, onion powder, Dijon, garlic, chives, and rinsed red onion. Mixed while warm for maximum absorption, it's garnished with fresh chives, red onion, and crumbled sour cream and onion potato chips for texture.
