This recipe details how to make individual Pumpkin Bread Puddings with a crisp brûléed topping. It involves soaking buttered cinnamon raisin bread in a rich pumpkin custard, then baking the puddings in a water bath until set. After chilling thoroughly, each pudding is topped with whipping cream and brown sugar, then broiled until caramelized and bubbly for a delightful crunchy finish.
