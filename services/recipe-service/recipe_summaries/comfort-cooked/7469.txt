This recipe for Charred Octopus with Peach, Arugula and Aged Balsamic involves first braising octopus with aromatic vegetables, prosciutto, and cayenne until tender. After cooling, the octopus is charred in a hot skillet until caramelized. It's then sliced and served with fresh arugula, peach slices, a drizzle of aged balsamic vinegar, and a squeeze of lemon.
