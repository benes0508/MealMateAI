This recipe is for **Pork Rib Roast with Fig and Pistachio Stuffing**. It involves brining two 2-3lb pork rib roasts for 12-16 hours in a mixture of salt, brown sugar, honey, rosemary, garlic, and red pepper. After brining and drying, the roasts are seared, then slow-roasted at 325°F for about 1.5 hours until 145°F internal temperature. After resting, the roasts are carved into chops and served with a separate Fig and Pistachio Stuffing.
