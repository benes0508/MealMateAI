This recipe is for a **Sunday Rib Roast**, focusing on a **prime beef rib roast**. It involves seasoning the roast with **kosher salt, black pepper, flour, and dry mustard** to form a coating. The roast is cooked at **450°F for 20 minutes**, then at **350°F for about 1 hour 15 minutes** until it reaches 115°F internal temperature (medium-rare after resting). It is served with an accompanying **onion gravy**.
