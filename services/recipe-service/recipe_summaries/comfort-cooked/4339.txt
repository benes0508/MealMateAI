This recipe for Halibut Confit with Leeks, Coriander, and Lemon involves gently roasting halibut in a fragrant olive oil infused with pre-roasted leeks, lemon, and ground coriander. Leeks are initially roasted to tenderize and flavor the oil, which is then poured over the halibut for a slow cook until flaky. The dish is garnished with fresh coriander and cilantro.
