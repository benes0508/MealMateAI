This recipe is for **Spiced Chicken Stew with Carrots**, a slow-cooker dish featuring bone-in chicken thighs, carrots, garlic, cinnamon, cumin, golden raisins, fresh cilantro, and optional toasted almonds. The chicken is seasoned and browned (if possible) before being cooked with vegetables and spices in a slow cooker for 4 hours on high or 8 hours on low, with raisins added towards the end. It's served topped with cilantro and almonds, with the seasoned cooking liquid spooned over.
