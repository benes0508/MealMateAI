This recipe describes how to make Orecchiette with Fresh Mozzarella, Grape Tomatoes, and Garlic Chives. It involves cooking orecchiette pasta, then adding diced fresh mozzarella until it begins to melt. Finally, halved grape tomatoes, extra virgin olive oil, white balsamic vinegar, minced garlic chives, and fresh oregano are tossed in, seasoned with salt and pepper to taste.
