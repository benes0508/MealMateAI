This recipe details how to prepare **Wood-Smoked Tri-Tip with Sicilian Herb Sauce**. A tri-tip beef roast is seasoned with salt and pepper, then smoked on a grill using soaked wood chips (oak, mesquite, or hickory) over medium-high heat, then indirect medium-hot heat, until it reaches medium-rare. It's served thinly sliced with a vibrant **Sicilian Herb Sauce** made from fresh thyme, garlic, dried oregano, lemon juice, and olive oil.
