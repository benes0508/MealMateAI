This recipe is for **Chicken and Corn Soup with Chile-Mint Salsa**.

It involves:
1.  **Preparing a smoky Chile-Mint Salsa** with charred jalape√±os, garlic, and fresh mint.
2.  **Making a chicken broth base** by simmering a whole chicken with corn cobs and aromatics (onion, garlic, carrot, celery, peppercorns, bay leaves).
3.  **Shredding the cooked chicken** and straining the flavorful broth.
4.  **Finishing the soup** by adding corn kernels, red onion, and shredded chicken to the simmering broth.
5.  **Serving** the soup garnished with diced tomato, tortilla chips, and lime, with the chile-mint salsa and sea salt passed alongside.
