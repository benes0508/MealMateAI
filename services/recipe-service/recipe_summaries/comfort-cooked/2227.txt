This recipe is for a **Curried Yellow Split Pea Soup** with a distinctive **Spiced Coconut** topping. The hearty, savory soup features yellow split peas, carrots, and red onion, flavored with Madras curry powder, fennel, and mustard seeds, simmered in stock until creamy and textured. It's finished with a unique crispy topping of toasted coconut flakes and crushed coriander, fennel, and mustard seeds, served with fresh cilantro.
