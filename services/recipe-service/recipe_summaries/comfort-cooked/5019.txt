This recipe details how to make **Roast Beef Stock**. It involves roasting beef or veal marrow bones with vegetables (carrots, celery, onions, garlic) until browned, then transferring them to a stockpot with water and aromatics (parsley stems, thyme, bay leaves, peppercorns). The mixture is simmered for 4 hours, then strained. The stock can be chilled for 2 days or frozen for up to 3 months.
