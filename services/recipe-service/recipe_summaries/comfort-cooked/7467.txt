This recipe provides instructions for making **Fresh Gluten-Free Pasta** from scratch. Key ingredients include corn, quinoa, and potato flours, along with xanthan and guar gums for binding, and eggs/egg yolks. The dough is prepared using a stand mixer, then rolled out using either a pasta machine or by hand. It details how to cut the dough for various shapes like fettuccine, spaghetti, ravioli, or lasagna, and includes important cooking tips emphasizing careful timing to avoid overcooking gluten-free pasta.
