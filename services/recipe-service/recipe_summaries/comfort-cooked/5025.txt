This recipe for "Pork Shoulder Al'Diavolo" features a boneless pork shoulder seasoned with a bold, spicy, and aromatic marinade containing peppercorns, coriander, red pepper flakes, oregano, mustard seeds, garlic, lemon zest, and smoked paprika. The pork is marinated, then slow-roasted in the oven until very tender, and finally rested before slicing.
