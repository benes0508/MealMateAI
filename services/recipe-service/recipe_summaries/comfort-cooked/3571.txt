This recipe for Sweet Potato Avocado Burgers outlines how to create plant-based patties from roasted sweet potatoes, chickpeas, rolled oats, walnuts, and flaxseed, seasoned with tamari and Worcestershire sauce (vegan options noted). The preparation involves roasting sweet potatoes, blending them with wet ingredients in a food processor, combining with dry ingredients, forming into patties, and then pan-frying until golden brown. The burgers are served on buns with toppings like fresh avocado, kale, and optional condiments.
