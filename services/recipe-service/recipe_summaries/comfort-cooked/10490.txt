This recipe describes "Roasted Fingerlings with Red and Yellow Pipérade," a side dish featuring fingerling potatoes, red and yellow bell peppers, and red onion, all roasted together at 425°F with olive oil. The dish is seasoned with salt, pepper, and various fresh herbs like parsley, shallots, chives, basil, and thyme, finished with a drizzle of Champagne vinegar. It can be prepared up to two hours ahead.
