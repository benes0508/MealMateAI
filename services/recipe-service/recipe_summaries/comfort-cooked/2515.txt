This recipe details how to make Rock Shrimp Pasta with Spicy Tomato Sauce. It involves cooking rock shrimp and strozzapreti/casarecce pasta with a rich sauce made from whole peeled tomatoes, fennel, garlic, and Fresno chile, deglazed with white wine. The pasta is finished in the sauce with shrimp, butter, and pasta cooking liquid, then garnished with fennel fronds and fresh chile.
