This recipe for Shrimp and Roasted Fennel Ditalini involves roasting shrimp with toasted fennel seeds, roasted fennel bulb, garlic, and lemon zest. A flavorful stock is made from reserved shrimp shells and then added to the dish. Ditalini pasta is cooked separately and then combined with the roasted ingredients in the pan, incorporating the stock and some pasta water for a cohesive dish.
