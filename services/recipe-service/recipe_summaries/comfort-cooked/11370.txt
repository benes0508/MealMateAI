This recipe is for a **Standing Rib Roast with Porcini and Bacon Sauce**. An 8.5-9lb beef rib roast is seasoned with a garlic, thyme, olive oil, salt, and pepper rub. It's roasted at 450°F for 20 minutes, then at 350°F for approximately 1 hour 50 minutes, until it reaches an internal temperature of 125-130°F for medium-rare. After resting, it's served with the eponymous Porcini and Bacon Sauce, Horseradish Cream Sauce, and Green Onion Parmesan Popovers.
