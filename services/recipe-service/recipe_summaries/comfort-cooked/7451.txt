This recipe details how to prepare a **Mustard-Seed-Crusted Prime Rib Roast with Roasted Balsamic Onions**. A bone-in prime rib is coated in a Dijon mustard-garlic rub and mustard seeds, then roasted with balsamic-glazed onions and shallots until medium-rare. The dish is served with a rich pan jus and a creamy horseradish-Dijon crème fraîche.
