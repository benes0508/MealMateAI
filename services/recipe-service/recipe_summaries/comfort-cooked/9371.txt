This recipe details preparing a **Standing Rib Roast** internally stuffed with a **Spinach-Porcini Stuffing**. The roast is seasoned with a garlic-rosemary rub and cooked at an initial high temperature before reducing the heat to finish. It's served with a rich **Irish Whiskey Gravy**, made from red wine, chicken broth, cream, and pan drippings, alongside a **Horseradish Cream**.
