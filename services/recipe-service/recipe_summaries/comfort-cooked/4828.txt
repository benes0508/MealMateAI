This recipe details how to make classic Corned Beef Hash. It involves slow-cooking a corned beef brisket with herbs and spices until tender, then adding onion and potato. The chilled, shredded beef, sliced onion, and diced potato are pan-fried into crispy hash cakes. The dish is traditionally served topped with perfectly poached eggs and fresh herbs. The recipe includes convenient "do-ahead" steps for preparing the beef, vegetables, and eggs.
