This recipe is for a **Roast Leg of Lamb with Tarragon-Mint Butter**. It features a leg of lamb seared then roasted to medium-rare, flavored with slits of orange peel and basted with a fragrant tarragon-mint compound butter. A pan sauce is made from red wine and chicken broth, enriched with the remaining herb butter and grated orange peel. It's garnished with fresh herbs and served with the sauce and vegetables.
