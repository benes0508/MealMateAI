This recipe is for "Soy and Sesame Short Ribs," featuring flanken-style beef short ribs marinated in a flavorful blend of pulsed apple, garlic, orange marmalade, brown sugar, sesame oil, sake, gochugaru, and soy sauce. The ribs are then grilled until lightly charred and cooked through, offering a sweet, savory, and slightly spicy Asian-inspired dish.
