This recipe prepares a **Curried Potato and Spinach Soup with Onion Salsa and Minted Yogurt**. The soup features potatoes, spinach, tofu, and cauliflower simmered in a turmeric-curry vegetable broth. It is served with a separately prepared slow-cooked red onion salsa (with nigella and mustard seeds), an aromatic curry oil, and a refreshing minted Greek yogurt.
