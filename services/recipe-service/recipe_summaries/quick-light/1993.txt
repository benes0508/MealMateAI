Coconut Rice and Peas is an aromatic dish featuring tender red kidney beans cooked with shallot, garlic, oregano, and thyme. Basmati rice is then simmered in a flavorful liquid of coconut milk and reserved bean broth before the seasoned beans are stirred in. Ideal as a hearty side or light main. Requires overnight bean soaking and about 1.5-2 hours of cooking.
