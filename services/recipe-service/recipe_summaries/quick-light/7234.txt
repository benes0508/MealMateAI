Aromatic Wild Garlic and White Bean Curry featuring white beans and vibrant wild garlic, simmered in a coconut milk and tomato base. It's infused with a rich blend of aromatic spices (coriander, mustard, cardamom, curry, kaffir lime leaves), chiles, garlic, and cilantro root, balanced with jaggery, lime juice, and fish sauce. The resulting dish is described as soft, fragrant, gently sweet, sharp, salty, and warming.
