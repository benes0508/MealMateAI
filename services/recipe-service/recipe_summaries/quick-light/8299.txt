This recipe details a Pork Tenderloin Stir-Fry with Tangerines and Chili Sauce. Thin strips of pork tenderloin are seasoned, coated in cornstarch, and stir-fried with ginger. Tangerine pieces, sweet chili sauce, soy sauce, and Chinese five-spice powder are added to create a slightly thickened sauce. Finally, baby bok choy and green onions are stirred in until wilted, with the dish garnished with more green onions before serving.
