This recipe details how to prepare a flavorful Leg of Lamb with Garlic and Rosemary. A semi-boneless leg of lamb is seasoned with a paste of garlic, sea salt, rosemary, and pepper, then roasted at 350°F for 1.5 to 1.75 hours until it reaches 130°F for medium-rare. After resting, the pan is deglazed with red wine or beef broth to create a flavorful sauce.
