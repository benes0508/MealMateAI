This recipe is for a **Chilled Indian-Spiced Tomato Soup with Crabmeat**. It features a flavorful vegetable base of tomatoes, celery, onion, carrots, ginger, garlic, bell peppers, and fennel, infused with a freshly ground blend of toasted Indian spices (cumin, coriander, fennel, mustard, peppercorns). The soup is simmered, strained for a smooth consistency, then seasoned, chilled thoroughly, and served cold, garnished with fresh crabmeat, radishes, and chives.
