This recipe details how to make **Lemon Panna Cotta** served with a homemade **Lemon Marmalade**. The marmalade involves boiling and slicing lemon peels, then simmering them with lemon juice and sugar until reduced. The panna cotta is prepared by infusing cream, milk, sugar, vanilla, and lemon peel, dissolving gelatin in the mixture, straining, and then chilling until set. The dish is served by inverting the set panna cotta onto a plate and topping it with the prepared lemon marmalade.
