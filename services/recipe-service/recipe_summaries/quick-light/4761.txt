This recipe is for **Baked Herbed Gefilte Fish**. It involves baking a frozen gefilte fish loaf (unthawed) in a 9x5-inch pan with olive oil, paprika, parsley, allspice, salt, pepper, sliced onions, and minced garlic. After baking covered for 2 hours at 350Â°F, the dish is cooled, refrigerated for at least 4 hours (or overnight), and served cold, sliced, and topped with the onions.
