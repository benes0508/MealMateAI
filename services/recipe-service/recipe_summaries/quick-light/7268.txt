This recipe details **oven-roasted asparagus** prepared with a **fragrant garlic-infused olive oil**. Thin asparagus spears are drizzled with a warm mixture of olive oil, minced fresh garlic, onion powder, and parsley, then seasoned with sea salt and pepper. It's **roasted for 8-10 minutes** until bright green, offering a quick and flavorful vegetable side dish.
