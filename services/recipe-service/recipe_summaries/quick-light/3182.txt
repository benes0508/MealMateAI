This recipe details how to prepare **Herbed Chickpeas**, a quick and easy plant-based dish. Canned chickpeas are pan-fried with garlic and olive oil until crisp and some split open, then finished off-heat with a generous mix of fresh tender herbs like parsley, cilantro, chives, and/or basil. It's a flavorful and texturally appealing side or snack, ready in 10-15 minutes.
