This recipe features **Roast Beef with Wild Mushroom Sauce and Caramelized-Shallot Mashed Potatoes**. A beef tenderloin is seasoned, chilled for 1-3 days, then seared and roasted to rare (or desired doneness). The accompanying wild mushroom sauce is made with oyster and chanterelle mushrooms, shallots, garlic, thyme, brandy, cream, and thickened with flour and beef reduction from the roasting pan. The dish is served with pre-made caramelized-shallot mashed potatoes and garnished with fresh parsley, yielding 6 servings.
