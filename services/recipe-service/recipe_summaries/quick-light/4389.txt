"Tuna Tostadas, Contramar Style" features crispy deep-fried corn tortillas topped with a spicy chipotle aioli, sashimi-grade tuna briefly marinated in soy and lime, crispy fried leeks, and sliced avocado. The recipe involves preparing the aioli, frying the tortillas and leeks, marinating the tuna, and then assembling the components for immediate serving with lime wedges.
