This recipe details how to prepare Quesadillas with Oaxacan Cheese and Squash Blossoms. It involves sautéing onion and garlic until soft, then toasting corn tortillas. Each tortilla is filled with Oaxacan cheese, the sautéed onion-garlic mixture, and squash blossoms, folded, and cooked until the cheese is melted and the tortilla is golden and crisp.
