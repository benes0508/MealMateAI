This recipe for Tagliatelle with Fresh Corn Pesto features pasta tossed with a vibrant pesto made from sautéed fresh corn, Parmesan cheese, pine nuts, and olive oil. Crisp bacon is cooked separately and used as a garnish, along with reserved sautéed corn kernels and fresh basil. The dish is finished by warming the pasta with the pesto, adjusting consistency with pasta water, and serving with extra Parmesan.
