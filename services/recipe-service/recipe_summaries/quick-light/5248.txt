This recipe details how to make **Fried Calamari with Roasted Tomato Sauce**. Calamari rings and tentacles are coated in a seasoned flour mixture after being dipped in egg, then deep-fried until golden brown. A simple sauce of roasted tomatoes, garlic, salt, and crushed red pepper is simmered and blended smooth. The fried calamari is served hot with the warm tomato sauce and optional lemon wedges.
