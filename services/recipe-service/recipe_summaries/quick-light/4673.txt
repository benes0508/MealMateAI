This recipe for Tripe Tacos features slow-poached and pan-fried honeycomb tripe, tossed in a vibrant herbal tomatillo sauce made from broiled vegetables (tomatillos, onions, jalapeños, garlic) puréed with cilantro, mint, oregano, fish sauce, and lime. The tacos are assembled with warm corn tortillas and topped with a crunchy mix of toasted sunflower seeds, pumpkin seeds, and peanuts.
