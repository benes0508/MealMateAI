This recipe describes how to make **Pan-Seared Polenta with Spicy Tomato-Basil Sauce**. It involves baking coarse cornmeal polenta until very thick, cooling and firming it, then cutting it into squares and pan-searing them until crisp. The crispy polenta is then served topped with a warm spicy tomato-basil sauce and garnished with fresh basil. The polenta can be prepared up to two days in advance.
