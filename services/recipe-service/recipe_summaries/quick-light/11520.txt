This recipe for **Chicken with Olives, Caramelized Onions, and Sage** features seasoned chicken quarters browned and then slow-roasted with deeply caramelized white and red onions. A sauce is made by reducing dry white wine in the chicken's pan, then combined with sage, shallots, garlic, and bay leaves. The chicken is baked covered, then uncovered with assorted Mediterranean olives, resulting in tender chicken served with flavorful pan juices.
