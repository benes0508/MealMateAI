Sesame Noodles with Chili Oil and Scallions is a quick, flavorful dish featuring thin noodles tossed in a sauce made from tahini, rice vinegar, soy sauce, and toasted sesame oil, seasoned with sugar and salt. It's topped with a homemade chili oil infused with scallion whites, red pepper flakes, sesame seeds, and Sichuan pepper, then garnished with fresh scallion greens.
