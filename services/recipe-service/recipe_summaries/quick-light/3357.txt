This recipe for Penne with Roasted Butternut Squash features roasted butternut squash (half pureed for sauce, half cubed), penne pasta, sautéed onion, Parmesan cheese, and fresh parsley. The squash is roasted and then part of it is blended with pasta water to create a creamy sauce, which is combined with the remaining roasted squash, sautéed onion, and al dente penne.
