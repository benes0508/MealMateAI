This recipe details how to make spicy, crunchy toasted garbanzo beans (chickpeas) and pistachios. Chickpeas are seasoned with oil, sea salt, cumin, black pepper, and cayenne, then baked until golden and crisp. Pistachios and fresh thyme are stirred in and baked further until the entire mixture is crunchy. It serves as a savory snack or side dish and can be prepared up to 4 hours in advance.
