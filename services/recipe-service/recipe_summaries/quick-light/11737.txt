This recipe details how to make a **Roasted-Tomato Tart**. It involves preparing a puff pastry round and separately roasting seasoned plum tomatoes with olive oil, thyme, salt, and pepper until tender. The pastry is then baked until golden. Finally, warm roasted tomatoes are layered over the baked pastry, topped with Parmigiano Reggiano shavings and fresh thyme for a savory tart.
