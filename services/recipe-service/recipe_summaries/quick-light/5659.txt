This recipe details how to make **Lemon Marshmallows** from scratch. It involves preparing a sugar syrup infused with lemon zest and granulated sugar, then combining it with softened gelatin and lemon juice. The mixture is whipped until thick, spread into a prepared pan, allowed to set for several hours, and finally cut into squares and coated with confectioners' sugar. The marshmallows can be stored for up to a month.
