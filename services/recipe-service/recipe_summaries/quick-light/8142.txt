This recipe details how to make potato latkes served with a spicy ancho-chile salt and a fresh watercress guacamole. Key steps involve preparing the ancho-chile salt by toasting and grinding chiles with salt, making the latke mixture from finely ground potatoes and onions, egg, cilantro, and masa (or flour), frying the latkes until golden, and finally assembling the dish with the chile salt and guacamole.
