This recipe for **Tortilla de Patatas** (Spanish Potato Omelet) features Yukon Gold potatoes cooked with onion until tender, then combined with beaten eggs and pan-fried until golden and set. The potatoes are pre-salted, cooked in olive oil, then mixed with eggs and cooked in a skillet, flipped, and cooked on the other side. It can be served warm or at room temperature, cut into wedges.
