This recipe details how to make Rösti with Fried Eggs. It involves par-boiling, chilling, and then grating russet potatoes, which are then pan-fried with butter until golden and crispy on both sides. The Rösti is topped with raclette or gruyère cheese and baked until melted. Concurrently, eggs are fried. The dish is served by topping quarters of the Rösti with fried eggs and garnishing with parsley.
