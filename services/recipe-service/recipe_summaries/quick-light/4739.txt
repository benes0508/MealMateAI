This recipe details how to make **Ricotta Gnocchi with Asparagus, Peas, and Morels**. Homemade ricotta gnocchi are prepared by draining ricotta, then blending it with eggs, Parmesan, and flour, which are then piped and cooked in simmering water. Separately, asparagus is blanched, and morel mushrooms and shallots are saut√©ed. Finally, the cooked gnocchi, asparagus, peas, and butter are combined with the morel mixture in a skillet, gently tossed to warm through, and finished with fresh chives, Parmesan, and lemon zest.
