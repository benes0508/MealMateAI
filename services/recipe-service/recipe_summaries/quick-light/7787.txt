This recipe details how to prepare **Curried Scallops with Tomatoes**. Sea scallops are lightly dredged in curry powder, pan-seared until browned, then finished with chopped tomatoes, lime juice, and fresh cilantro. An optional addition of heavy cream, sour cream, or yogurt can be included. Key tips emphasize using a mild, fine curry powder, dredging scallops lightly, and cooking them to a rare doneness for optimal texture.
