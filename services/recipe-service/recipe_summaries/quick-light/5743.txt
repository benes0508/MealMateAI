This recipe features orecchiette pasta tossed with a savory kale sauce and crispy breadcrumbs. Kale is blanched and chopped, then combined with a sauce made from butter, olive oil, anchovies, garlic, and red pepper flakes. Toasted breadcrumbs, seasoned with garlic, are incorporated into the dish and also used as a topping, along with Parmesan cheese and a final drizzle of olive oil.
