This recipe details how to make Cheesy Baked Penne with Cauliflower and Crème Fraîche. It involves blanching and sautéing cauliflower and tomatoes, preparing a rich sauce with heavy cream, Comté, Parmesan, crème fraîche, and Dijon mustard, then combining these with cooked penne. The mixture is layered in a baking dish with more cheese, topped with buttered breadcrumbs, and baked until bubbly and golden.
