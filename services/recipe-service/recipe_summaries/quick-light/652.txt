This recipe is for **Jalapeño-Marinated Grilled Pork Chops**. It features bone-in pork chops seasoned and grilled, alongside grilled jalapeños. A marinade made from toasted and crushed coriander seeds, apple cider vinegar, sugar, and olive oil is prepared. After grilling, the pork and jalapeños are sliced, then tossed with the marinade and thinly sliced raw white onion before serving.
