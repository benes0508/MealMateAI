This recipe for **Five-Minute Marzipan** provides a quick and easy method for homemade marzipan using **almond paste, confectioners' sugar, and kirsch or brandy**. The ingredients are combined and kneaded, either by hand or with a stand mixer, until smooth and pliable (taking about 5 minutes with a mixer). Instructions include tips for adjusting consistency, and the prepared marzipan can be stored, wrapped, in the refrigerator for at least 3 months.
