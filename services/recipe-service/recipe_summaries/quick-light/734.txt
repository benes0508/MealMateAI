A simple, tangy, and spicy condiment featuring thinly sliced Fresno chiles pickled in a flavorful brine of white wine vinegar, sugar, salt, and garlic. It's easy to prepare, requiring only a quick simmer of the brine before pouring over the chiles to cool. These pickled chiles can be made up to a month in advance and stored in the refrigerator, offering a zesty kick to various dishes.
