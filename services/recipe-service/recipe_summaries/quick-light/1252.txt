This recipe details how to make grilled Jalapeño Poppers with Smoked Gouda. Halved and deseeded jalapeño chiles are filled with a mixture of cream cheese, smoked gouda, and salt, then grilled for 5-10 minutes until softened and the cheese is melted. The dish is garnished with fresh cilantro. Variations are provided for Honey-Orange (goat cheese, honey, orange zest), Pimento Cheese (cheddar, pimentos, mayo), and Pesto Mozzarella fillings.
