This recipe is for **Cold Sesame Noodles with Summer Vegetables**, featuring buckwheat soba or vermicelli noodles tossed with a mix of matchstick-cut summer vegetables (carrots, radishes, cucumbers, bell peppers) and a savory-spicy dressing made from rice vinegar, vegetable oil, sriracha, and toasted sesame oil. It's finished with fresh cilantro, scallions, and sesame seeds, served cold.
