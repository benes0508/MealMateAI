This recipe details **Pan-Grilled Black Bass**, offering alternatives like trout, cod, or haddock. The fish is lightly floured, buttered, and pan-grilled for a crisp skin. It's served with lemon-pepper sautéed broccolini and a distinctive trio of flavored butters: maître d'hôtel, nasturtium, and chile cilantro, which soften on the warm fish.
