"Relish Tray With D.I.Y. Eggs" is a recipe for a customizable relish tray featuring hard-boiled eggs, a variety of quick-pickled vegetables (fennel, scallions, celery, carrots, beets, chiles), and a turmeric-cayenne mayonnaise sauce. Guests assemble their eggs with the provided pickles, olives, smoked almonds, and sea salt.
