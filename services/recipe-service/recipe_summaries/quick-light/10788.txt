This recipe is for a Cabbage and Corn Slaw with a vibrant Cilantro and Orange Dressing. It combines coleslaw mix, fresh corn, grated carrots, red bell pepper, green onions, and cilantro. The dressing is a simple whisk of thawed orange juice concentrate, rice vinegar, and oil, seasoned to taste. The ingredients are tossed with the dressing and allowed to meld for 15 minutes before serving.
