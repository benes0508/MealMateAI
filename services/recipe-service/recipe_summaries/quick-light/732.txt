This recipe details a Brothy Noodle Bowl with Mushrooms and Chiles. It involves preparing a savory dashi broth infused with dried shiitake mushrooms, ginger, garlic, and chile, then enriching it with miso and soy sauce. Rice vermicelli noodles are steeped in the hot broth and topped with pan-fried fresh shiitake mushrooms, radishes, mixed herbs, fermented vegetables, and sesame seeds, finished with hot sauce and sesame oil.
