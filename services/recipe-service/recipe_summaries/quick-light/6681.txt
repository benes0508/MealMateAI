This recipe for "Endive with Chicken Liver Pâté and Dried-Cherry Marmalade" features individual endive leaves filled with a chunky chicken liver pâté and topped with a sweet and savory dried-cherry and caramelized onion marmalade, finished with orange zest. The marmalade is made by sautéing onions, then adding wine-soaked dried cherries, red wine vinegar, and thyme. The chicken livers are seasoned, broiled until tender, then coarsely chopped and mixed with chicken fat or olive oil. The dish is assembled by filling endive leaves with the pâté, topping with marmalade, and sprinkling with orange zest, served immediately.
