This recipe creates Gluten-Free Shells with Beets, Ricotta, and Pistachios, a vibrant and textural vegetarian pasta dish. It features golden beets that are first boiled and then seared until caramelized, tossed with gluten-free pasta shells. The dish is served atop a creamy, seasoned ricotta base, garnished with crunchy toasted pistachios and fresh chives.
