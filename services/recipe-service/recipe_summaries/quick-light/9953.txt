This recipe describes how to make **Spiced Pears with Oranges and Caramel Sauce**. Anjou pears are cored, then simmered in a homemade amber caramel sauce with cinnamon and ras el hanout (a Moroccan spice blend). Orange slices are added to soften, and the dish is served warm or at room temperature, garnished with toasted sliced almonds and extra caramel sauce.
