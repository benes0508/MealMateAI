This recipe details a Roast Chicken with Bell Peppers, Lemon, and Thyme. A whole chicken is roasted alongside bell peppers (first blistered in a skillet), shallots, and fresno chile, seasoned with salt, pepper, olive oil, and thyme. Lemon halves are placed in the chicken cavity and squeezed over the dish before serving. The goal is a chicken with golden, crisp skin and tender, aromatic vegetables.
