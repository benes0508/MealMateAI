This recipe for "Royal Chicken Cooked in Yogurt" features chicken pieces seasoned with salt and pepper, then browned with whole spices (cardamom, cloves, cinnamon, bay leaves). A sauce is made from smooth yogurt, cumin, coriander, cayenne, salt, pepper, and cilantro. After browning the chicken, slivered almonds and golden raisins are quickly fried in the same pan. The chicken is returned to the pan with its juices, the seasoned yogurt is added, and the dish is simmered gently for 20 minutes before the sauce is reduced until thick and clinging to the chicken.
