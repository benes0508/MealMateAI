This recipe features **Roasted Carrots with Carrot-Top Pesto**. Carrots are tossed with oil, salt, and pepper, then roasted until tender and golden. The pesto is made by blending reserved carrot tops with garlic, nuts (macadamia or pine), basil, Parmesan, and olive oil. The roasted carrots are served with the homemade pesto. The pesto can be made a day ahead, and carrots can be roasted up to 4 hours in advance.
