This recipe for Crisp Oven-Browned Potatoes features thinly sliced Yukon gold potatoes (prepared with an adjustable blade slicer) tossed with melted butter, olive oil, salt, and pepper. They are baked in a two-stage process: first covered at 350°F for 20 minutes, then uncovered at 450°F for about 25 minutes until browned and crisp.
