This recipe for 'My Favorite Falafel' details how to prepare classic deep-fried falafel. It uses dried (or canned) chickpeas, blended with onion, parsley, cilantro, garlic, cumin, and hot pepper. The mixture is formed into balls, deep-fried until golden, and traditionally served in pita bread with tahina sauce and fresh garnishes like tomato, onion, and green bell pepper. The instructions include tips for soaking chickpeas, forming the mixture, and offer notes on Egyptian variations and Israeli-style condiments. This is a plant-based (vegan/vegetarian) main dish.
