This recipe details an elegant, multi-component dish featuring **barely cooked, delicate poached salmon**. It's served with **creamy Parmesan polenta** and two distinct mushroom preparations: a **light, savory mushroom consommé** (made from button mushrooms simmered for hours) and **sautéed wild mushrooms** (like morels or gyromitres, cooked with shallots, garlic, and parsley). The dish emphasizes precise cooking and layered flavors.
