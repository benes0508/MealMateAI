This recipe is for **Paccheri and Cheese with Peas and Mint**, a baked pasta dish. It features paccheri (or rigatoni) in a creamy **Fontina and Parmesan cheese sauce**, with added **peas, arugula, parsley, and fresh mint**. A ricotta and lemon zest mixture is dotted throughout the layers. The dish is baked until golden brown and garnished with fresh herbs.
