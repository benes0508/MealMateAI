This recipe is for **Mini Sage-and-Fontina Grilled Cheese Sandwiches**. It involves preparing 16 small sandwiches by brushing country-style bread with sage-infused melted butter, layering with Fontina and Parmesan cheese, and topping with whole sage leaves. The sandwiches are then grilled in a skillet until golden brown and the cheese is melted, then cut into quarters for serving.
