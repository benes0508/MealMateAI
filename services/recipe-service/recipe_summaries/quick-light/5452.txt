This recipe is for **Phyllo Triangles Stuffed with Fresh Cheese (briouats bil jben)**. It involves blending ricotta or queso fresco with egg and cilantro to create a filling, which is then spooned onto phyllo dough strips, folded into triangles, and brushed with olive oil and egg yolk. The triangles are then pan-fried until golden brown and served hot, drizzled with honey and sprinkled with toasted sesame seeds.
