This recipe is for **Mango Puddings**, a chilled dessert made primarily from very ripe mango purée, sweetened with sugar, thickened with unflavored gelatin, and enriched with evaporated milk. The process involves puréeing and straining mangoes, dissolving gelatin and sugar in water, combining all ingredients, and then chilling for at least 8 hours until softly set. It yields 8 individual servings.
