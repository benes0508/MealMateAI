This recipe for **Salted Chocolate Halva** involves combining a hot sugar syrup with a tahini mixture (tahini, salt, sesame seeds) to form a smooth halva block. Once cooled, it's topped with melted bittersweet chocolate, flaky sea salt, more sesame seeds, and optional edible flowers, then allowed to set before serving. It can be prepared up to 3 days in advance and stored at room temperature.
