This recipe features Jammy Eggs paired with a rich Smoked Paprika Aioli. Twenty-four eggs are perfectly boiled for a jammy yolk, then peeled. The aioli is a blend of mayonnaise, lemon, garlic, and smoked paprika. Served halved and dolloped with aioli, garnished with optional anchovies or roasted bell peppers and fresh parsley. Both the aioli and boiled, peeled eggs can be made up to two days ahead.
