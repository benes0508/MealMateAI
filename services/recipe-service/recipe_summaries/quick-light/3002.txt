This recipe for **Stir-Fried Asparagus With Bacon and Crispy Shallots** involves several steps: shallots are first marinated in black and sherry vinegar, then dredged in flour/cornmeal and deep-fried until crispy. Separately, bacon is cooked until crisp and its fat rendered. Finally, asparagus is quickly stir-fried with the cooked bacon and a splash of water and white soy sauce, then topped with the crispy shallots for serving.
