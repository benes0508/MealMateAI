This recipe creates a **Polenta and Vegetables with Roasted Red Pepper Sauce**. It features creamy, Parmesan-infused instant polenta triangles, broiled vegetables (asparagus, scallions, Japanese eggplant, yellow squash, and cherry tomatoes) tossed in a lemon-herb dressing, and a vibrant roasted red pepper sauce. The dish is assembled by serving the broiled polenta with the tender vegetables, topped with the smooth, flavorful sauce.
