This recipe details a Carrot Curry, featuring a creamy, spiced sauce made with toasted whole spices (star anise, cardamom, cinnamon, garam masala), pureed tomatoes, shallots, carrot juice, lime juice, saffron, and unsweetened coconut milk. Large carrot chunks are simmered in this aromatic sauce until tender. It's served with rice and garnished with coconut milk, cilantro, lime juice, and zest.
