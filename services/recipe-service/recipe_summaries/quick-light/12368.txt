This recipe is for **Spaghetti with Scallion Sauce**, a pasta dish where spaghetti is tossed with a vibrant, smooth sauce. The sauce is made by sautéing scallions and garlic in olive oil, then puréeing them with lemon zest, additional olive oil, and pasta cooking water. The dish is finished by tossing the cooked spaghetti with the scallion purée and finely grated Parmigiano Reggiano.
