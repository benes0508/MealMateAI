This recipe details how to make **Panisses with Sage Leaves and Lemon**. Panisses are a savory, crispy snack made from a cooked and chilled chickpea flour mixture, which is then sliced into strips and deep-fried until golden. The dish is finished with quickly fried sage leaves, a sprinkle of sea salt, and served with fresh lemon wedges. The chickpea flour mixture can be prepared a day in advance.
