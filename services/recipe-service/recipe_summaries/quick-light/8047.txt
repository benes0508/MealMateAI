This recipe is for Linguine with Bay Scallops, Fennel, and Tomatoes. It involves cooking linguine, then sautéing thinly sliced fennel and onion, followed by bay scallops, and finally cherry tomatoes in a skillet. The sautéed ingredients and pasta are combined, tossed with Pernod (or anise liqueur), parsley, and fennel fronds, and served with lemon wedges.
