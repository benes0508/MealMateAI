This recipe details how to prepare Linguine with Scallion Sauce and Sauteed Shrimp. It involves making a vibrant green sauce by blending blanched scallion greens with sautéed white scallions and garlic. Shrimp are quickly sautéed until golden. Linguine is cooked al dente, then tossed with the scallion sauce, and served topped with the sautéed shrimp.
