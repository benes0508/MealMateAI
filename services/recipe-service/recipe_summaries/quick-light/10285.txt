This recipe details how to prepare Vegetarian Pad Thai, a flavorful and tangy dish featuring flat rice noodles, firm tofu, and eggs. It involves making a sauce from tamarind, soy sauce, brown sugar, and sriracha. Key steps include preparing crispy fried shallots (and their infused oil), pan-frying tofu, scrambling eggs, and then stir-frying scallions and garlic with the noodles, tofu, and bean sprouts, all brought together with the tamarind sauce and finished with the cooked eggs. It's served garnished with roasted peanuts, crispy shallots, lime, and cilantro.
