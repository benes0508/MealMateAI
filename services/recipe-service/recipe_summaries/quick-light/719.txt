This recipe for "Potatoes with Roasted Poblano Chiles and Mexican Sour Cream" (Papas con Rajas y Crema Ácida) creates a creamy, savory Mexican-inspired side dish. It involves roasting, peeling, and stripping poblano chiles, boiling diced potatoes, and then sautéing onions. Finally, these components are combined with Mexican crema or crème fraîche and gently heated until the flavors meld.
