This recipe details how to prepare a **Cast-Iron Roast Chicken with Caramelized Leeks**. A whole chicken is seasoned with salt, tied, and left to sit. Leeks are tossed with olive oil, then both are roasted together in a preheated cast-iron skillet at 425°F for 50-60 minutes until the chicken reaches 155°F and the leeks are browned. The chicken is rested before carving and serving with the caramelized leeks.
