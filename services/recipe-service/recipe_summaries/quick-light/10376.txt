This recipe features roasted racks of lamb with a savory crust made from ground lamb, Kalamata olives, rosemary, and garlic, topped with seasoned breadcrumbs. It is served with a rich pan sauce combining red grapes, tawny port, chicken broth, and rosemary, enhanced by the lamb's pan drippings. The lamb is seared, crusted, roasted, and then sliced into chops.
