This recipe details how to prepare **Stir Fried Baby Bok Choy**. Key ingredients include baby bok choy, peanut oil, fresh ginger, scallions, garlic, salt, sugar, and white pepper. The dish is prepared by stir-frying aromatics, then adding sliced bok choy and seasonings. A small amount of water is added, and the bok choy is quickly steamed until tender.
