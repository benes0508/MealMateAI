This recipe, "Stuffed Eggplants and Zucchini in a Rich Tomato Sauce (Baatingan w Kusaa Bil Banadoura)", features eggplants and zucchini hollowed and filled with a mixture of ground lamb, Egyptian/Arborio rice, fresh herbs (mint, parsley, dill), and spices (cinnamon, allspice, cumin). The stuffed vegetables are gently simmered for 90 minutes in a savory tomato sauce made with caramelized onions and fresh tomatoes. The dish is finished with a fragrant 'adha' topping of fried garlic, coriander seeds, and chile infused oil, garnished with fresh herbs, and traditionally served with a side of Greek yogurt.
