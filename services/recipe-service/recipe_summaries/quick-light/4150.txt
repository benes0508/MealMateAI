This recipe for Spanish Tortilla Bites features Yukon Gold potatoes, eggs, sautéed winter greens (like collards or kale), garlic, and onion. The potatoes and onions are slowly cooked until tender, then mixed with sautéed greens and eggs, seasoned, and gently cooked in a skillet until set and golden. It's served as 36 bite-sized pieces with a homemade pimentón aioli (mayonnaise, smoked paprika, Tabasco, and garlic). Both the tortilla and aioli can be prepared up to 3 days in advance.
