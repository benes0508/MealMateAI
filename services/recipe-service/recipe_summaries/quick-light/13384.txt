This recipe is for **Castellane with Mascarpone and Roasted Grape Tomatoes**, a baked pasta dish. It features roasted grape or cherry tomatoes, Castellane pasta (or shells), mascarpone cheese for creaminess, and Parmigiano Reggiano. Fresh chives are added for flavor. The dish involves roasting tomatoes, cooking pasta, combining them with the cheeses and chives, and then baking until golden and bubbly.
