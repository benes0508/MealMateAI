This is a one-skillet stir-fry featuring hot Italian sausage, shiitake mushrooms, and napa cabbage, flavored with ginger, garlic, soy sauce, and rice vinegar. The dish is prepared by browning the sausage, then stir-frying the mushrooms and cabbage, before combining all ingredients with fresh chives and sesame oil. It's served with tortillas or wraps, hoisin sauce, and sriracha.
