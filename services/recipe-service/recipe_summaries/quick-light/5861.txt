This recipe for Udon With Chicken and Scallions involves cooking chicken, then broccoli, and finally udon noodles in boiling water. The cooked chicken is torn and tossed with oyster and hoisin sauces, sesame oil, chile garlic paste, and scallions. The noodles, broccoli, and sauced chicken are then combined, garnished with more scallions and optional sesame seeds, and served immediately.
