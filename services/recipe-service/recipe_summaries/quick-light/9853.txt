A **Curried Pea Frittata** featuring **eggs, peas, curry powder,** and **Parmesan cheese** cooked on the **stovetop** and finished under the **broiler**. It's served with a vibrant **Fresh Tomato Chutney** made with **grape tomatoes, ginger, garlic, cumin,** and **brown sugar.** This **vegetarian** dish is relatively quick to prepare and can be enjoyed warm or at room temperature.
