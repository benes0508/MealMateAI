This recipe details how to make **Chicken Tostadas** using crispy fried corn tortillas. A flavorful topping is prepared by puréeing broiled tomatoes, onions, garlic, and serrano chiles, then mixing with shredded rotisserie chicken and cilantro. The tostadas are assembled by spreading heated refried beans on the fried tortillas, topping with the chicken mixture, sliced avocado, Mexican crema, a fresh lettuce and radish salad, and crumbled queso añejo.
