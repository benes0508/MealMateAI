This recipe for "Crispy Brown Rice Kabbouleh" is a unique twist on the traditional tabbouleh salad. It features deep-fried, crispy brown rice mixed with finely chopped cauliflower, kale, cucumber, and scallions, along with rehydrated currants. The dish is seasoned with olive oil, Aleppo pepper, sumac, and vinegar, offering a fresh, textural salad with a do-ahead option for the crispy rice.
