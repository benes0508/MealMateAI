This recipe details how to make a **Mushroom Soup with Hazelnut Gremolata**. The soup base is made from rehydrated porcini and crimini mushrooms, sautéed with onion and carrot, then simmered in vegetable broth and pureed until smooth. It's topped with additional sautéed fresh wild mushrooms and a vibrant **hazelnut gremolata** made from parsley, olive oil, toasted hazelnuts, orange peel, and garlic.
