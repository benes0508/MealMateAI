A versatile, vegetable-rich Cabbage Soup known for its use in the Cabbage Soup Diet. It features a blend of cabbage, onions, tomatoes, bell peppers, celery, carrots, and green beans. This simple soup is prepared by boiling and simmering vegetables until tender, and can be enjoyed hot or cold, pureed, and customized with various herbs, balsamic vinegar, or citrus for flavor.
