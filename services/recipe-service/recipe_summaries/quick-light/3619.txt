This is a tangy and savory vinegar-based sauce, combining cider vinegar, hard cider, dark brown sugar, Worcestershire sauce, and a blend of spices including mustard powder, cayenne, garlic, and onion. It's easily prepared by bringing all ingredients to a boil, then cooling and refrigerating. The sauce can be made up to a week in advance and is ideal as a finishing sauce, marinade, or condiment for various dishes.
