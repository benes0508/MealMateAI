This recipe is for a **Chipotle Onion Dip with Garlic Pita Chips**. It involves sautéing sliced onions in butter and olive oil until deeply caramelized, then cooling them. The cooled onions are mixed with sour cream, crème fraîche, fromage blanc (or cream cheese), and chipotle chile powder. The dip is seasoned, chilled for at least an hour (or up to a day), and served with garlic pita chips.
