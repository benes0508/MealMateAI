This recipe details how to make **Chocolate Marshmallow Eggs**. It involves creating a fluffy marshmallow mixture from corn syrup, sugar, water, and gelatin, flavored with essential oils. This mixture is then spooned into egg molds, allowed to set for at least 8 hours, unmolded, and finally decorated or fully dipped in tempered bittersweet chocolate. The finished eggs should be consumed within 24 hours.
