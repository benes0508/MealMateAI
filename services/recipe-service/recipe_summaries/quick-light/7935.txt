This recipe details how to make **Chicken and Celery Pot Stickers**. It involves finely chopping celery and garlic, then combining with ground dark meat chicken, soy sauce, and sesame oil for the filling. The filling is enclosed in wonton/gyoza wrappers, sealed, and then pan-fried until golden, steamed with water, and finally made crispy on the bottom. A simple dipping sauce is prepared from chopped celery leaves, soy sauce, and rice vinegar.
