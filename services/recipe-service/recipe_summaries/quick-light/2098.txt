This recipe for **Sheet-Pan Pasta Bake with Chicken and Kale** creates a quick, family-friendly meal. Cooked pasta is tossed with sour cream, mustard, Parmesan, shredded rotisserie chicken, and mozzarella. This mixture is then combined on a baking sheet with kale and cherry tomatoes, topped with a cheesy panko breadcrumb mixture, and roasted until golden brown.
