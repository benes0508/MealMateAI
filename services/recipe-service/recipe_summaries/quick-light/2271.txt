This recipe for "Shrimp with Herby White Beans and Tomatoes" is a quick skillet dish. It involves cooking shallots, then simmering diced tomatoes with water, salt, and pepper to create a sauce. White beans and roasted garlic herb sauce (or pesto) are then stirred in to warm through. Separately, seasoned shrimp are quickly cooked until done. The shrimp are then combined with the herby bean and tomato mixture and served with crusty toasted bread.
