This recipe features **Summer Crudités** served with a warm, savory **Bagna Cauda** dipping sauce. The Bagna Cauda is prepared by slow-cooking garlic in olive oil, then adding anchovies and walnuts until dissolved and golden, seasoned with salt and pepper. The crudités include blanched baby artichokes, hard-boiled eggs, and an assortment of fresh raw vegetables such as carrots, radicchio, fennel, cucumbers, celery, and radishes, all arranged and lightly seasoned for dipping.
