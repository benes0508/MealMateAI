This recipe details how to make Ricotta-Pumpkin Gnocchi with Brown Butter, featuring homemade gnocchi, caramelized delicata squash, and sautéed broccolini. The gnocchi dough is made from pumpkin purée, ricotta, egg yolks, Parmesan, and spices, then piped into boiling water. A rich brown butter sauce is created with garlic, used to caramelize delicata squash, and later tossed with blanched broccolini. The dish is served with the gnocchi, vegetables, sauce, toasted pine nuts, and shaved Parmesan.
