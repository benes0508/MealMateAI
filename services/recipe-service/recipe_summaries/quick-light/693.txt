This recipe details how to prepare "Crispy-Skin Salmon with Miso-Honey Sauce." It involves making a flavorful sauce from ginger, white miso, honey, rice vinegar, and oil. Salmon fillets are cooked skin-side down in a cast-iron skillet until crispy, then flipped to finish cooking with residual heat. The dish is served over rice, topped with the sauce, sliced scallions, and toasted sesame seeds.
