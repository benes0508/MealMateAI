Spicy Kimchi Slaw is a vibrant and crunchy Asian-inspired salad featuring a unique, emulsified dressing made from blended kimchi, scallions, Sriracha, rice vinegar, fish sauce, and sesame oil. The base combines crisp napa cabbage, daikon radish matchsticks, and shredded carrots. Simple to prepare, it offers a bold, spicy, and savory flavor, garnished with toasted sesame seeds and cilantro.
