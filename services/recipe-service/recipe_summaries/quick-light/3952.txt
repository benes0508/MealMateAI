Ramp Pesto Spaghetti features al dente spaghetti coated in a vibrant, freshly made pesto. This unique pesto blends blanched ramp greens with chopped ramp bulbs/stalks, toasted walnuts, olive oil, and Pecorino, then tossed with the pasta and a touch of cooking liquid for a silky texture. Served with extra Pecorino and fresh lemon wedges.
