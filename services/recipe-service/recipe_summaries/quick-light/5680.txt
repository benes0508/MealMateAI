This recipe for **Pork Chops and Squash with Pumpkin Seed Vinaigrette** involves roasting winter squash until tender, cooking bone-in pork chops until browned and cooked through, and preparing a bright vinaigrette from toasted pumpkin seeds, garlic, cilantro, and lime juice. The dish is served with the vinaigrette spooned over the pork and squash, garnished with fresh cilantro.
