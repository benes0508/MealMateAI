This recipe details how to make Potato Gratin with Gruyère and Crème Fraîche. Thinly sliced russet potatoes are layered in a buttered baking dish with crème fraîche, Gruyère cheese, salt, and pepper. It's baked uncovered at 400°F for 30 minutes, then at 350°F for about 45 minutes until tender and golden. After resting, it's garnished with fresh parsley. The recipe also includes instructions for making crème fraîche if unavailable commercially.
