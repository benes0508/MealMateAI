This recipe details how to make a creamy Rice Pudding with Raisins and Cinnamon. It involves pre-cooking long-grain white rice, then combining it with a rich, reduced mixture of whole, sweetened condensed, and evaporated milks. The pudding is flavored with cinnamon sticks, vanilla beans, and lemon peel, with raisins and sugar stirred in at the end. It's typically garnished with ground cinnamon and grated lemon peel.
