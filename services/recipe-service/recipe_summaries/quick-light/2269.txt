This recipe for Creamy Wild Salmon With Kale involves seasoning wild salmon with turmeric and searing it. A spice paste of cider vinegar, dried chiles, salt, and nutmeg is prepared, and thinly sliced kale is cleaned. Garlic is saut√©ed, followed by the spice paste, then the kale is stewed until tender. Finally, unsweetened coconut milk is added, and the seared salmon is poached in the creamy sauce until cooked through. The sauce is then thickened and poured over the salmon for serving.
