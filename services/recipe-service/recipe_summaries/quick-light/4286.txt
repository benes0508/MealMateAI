This recipe is for Spiced Lamb Patties with Nutty Garlic Sauce. Lamb patties are seasoned with onion, parsley, coriander, and cumin, then pan-fried. A creamy garlic sauce is made with almond butter, lemon juice, and olive oil. The patties are served with pita bread, a fresh salad of cabbage, cucumber, and radish, and tender herbs.
