This recipe details how to make a **Roasted Red Pepper Frittata**. It involves roasting red bell peppers, then slow-cooking sliced onions and potatoes in olive oil until tender. These cooked vegetables are then combined with seasoned whisked eggs. The frittata is started on the stovetop in a cast-iron skillet, allowing the edges to set, then finished by baking in the oven until browned and fully set. It's served inverted and cut into wedges.
