This recipe details how to make Chilaquiles, a traditional Mexican dish. It involves frying corn tortilla triangles until golden, then topping them with a vibrant sauce made from pureed tomato salsa and cilantro, cooked with garlic. The dish is finished with a drizzle of sour and heavy cream mixture, and garnished with chopped red onion, crumbled queso fresco or ricotta salata, and fresh cilantro.
