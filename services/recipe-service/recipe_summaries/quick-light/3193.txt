A vibrant Miso-Turmeric Dressing, combining the savory depth of miso with the earthy notes of fresh turmeric and ginger. Brightened by rice vinegar and mirin, and finished with aromatic toasted sesame oil, it also includes finely grated carrot for subtle sweetness. This no-cook dressing is quick and easy to whisk together and can be made up to 5 days ahead, ideal for meal prep.
