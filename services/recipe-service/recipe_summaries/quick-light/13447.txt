This recipe details how to make shelf-stable pickled hot chiles using a boiling water canning method. It involves preparing a pickling brine from vinegar, water, sugar, and salt, then packing hot chiles, garlic, and pickling spices into sterilized jars. The jars are filled with the hot brine, sealed, and processed in a boiling water canner for 10 minutes, ensuring a proper seal as they cool.
