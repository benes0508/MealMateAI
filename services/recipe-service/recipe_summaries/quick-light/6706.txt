This recipe details how to prepare Spiced Glazed Carrots with Sherry & Citrus. Thin carrots are first simmered until tender, then coated in a flavorful glaze made from clementine/orange juice, sherry, and ground cloves. The dish is finished with fresh tarragon and citrus zest, offering a bright, spiced, and herbaceous side that can be prepared in about 15-20 minutes and made up to 4 hours ahead.
