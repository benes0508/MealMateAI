This recipe details a Harissa Shrimp and Summer Vegetable Sauté. Shrimp are tossed in a homemade harissa spice blend (cayenne, paprika, chili powder, garlic powder, coriander, cumin, olive oil) and sautéed with an assortment of vegetables including fennel, wax beans, green beans, snap peas, and peas, plus pea shoots, and finished with lemon. It's served hot over rice.
