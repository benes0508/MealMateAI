This recipe is for **Crab Linguine with Basil, Lemon & Chile**. It involves infusing olive oil with lemon zest and chopped bird's eye chiles, cooking linguine, then combining the pasta, white crab meat, lemon juice, and the infused oil in the same pan. The dish is finished by folding in fresh basil and seasoning, offering a quick, light, and flavorful seafood pasta.
