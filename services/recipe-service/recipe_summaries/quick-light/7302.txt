This recipe details how to prepare **Braised Lamb Shoulder Chops with Root Vegetables**. Lamb chops are seared until golden, then slow-braised in the oven for about 2 hours with a flavorful stock base, garlic, onions, celery, rosemary, thyme, and bay leaves until tender. Separately, diced carrots, turnips, and parsnips are blanched, then saut√©ed in butter. The braising liquid is strained, reduced, and skimmed. The dish is served with the tender lamb and root vegetables drizzled with the rich, reduced braising liquid.
