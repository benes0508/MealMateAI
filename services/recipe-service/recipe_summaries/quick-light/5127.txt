A dry brine designed for turkey, featuring a blend of black, pink, and white peppercorns, coriander seeds, bay leaves, kosher salt, light brown sugar, and lemon and orange zests. Preparation involves toasting and crushing the peppercorns and bay leaves, then combining them with the remaining ingredients. Intended for use on turkey, requiring rinsing and patting dry after curing and before roasting. Can be prepared up to 3 days in advance.
