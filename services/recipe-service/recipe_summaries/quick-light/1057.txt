This recipe for **Spicy Sizzling Squid (Sisig na Pusit)** features blanched squid quickly cooked in a hot cast-iron skillet with a flavorful sauce of calamansi juice, Filipino vinegar, oyster sauce, and sesame oil, along with shallots, garlic, and Thai chili peppers. It's seasoned with salt and pepper, then garnished with green onions, optional salmon roe, and crushed prawn crackers. Served sizzling hot, it can be enjoyed with rice or whole prawn crackers.
