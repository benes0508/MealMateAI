A recipe for **Thai Green Curry Paste**, it features classic Thai aromatics and spices such as green chiles, galangal, lemongrass, kaffir lime leaves, cilantro, toasted cumin and coriander, along with garlic, green onions, shrimp paste, and palm sugar. The process involves toasting and grinding the seeds, then blending them with all other ingredients in a food processor to create a smooth paste.
