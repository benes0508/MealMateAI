This recipe for "Brussels Sprout Tacos with Spicy Peanut Butter" features crispy shallow-fried and fresh thinly sliced brussels sprouts tossed in a savory fish sauce-ginger vinaigrette. The warm corn tortillas are spread with a homemade spicy peanut butter, then filled with the brussels sprouts and topped with fresh ingredients like avocado, white onion, serrano chiles, cilantro, and crushed peanuts, served with lime wedges.
