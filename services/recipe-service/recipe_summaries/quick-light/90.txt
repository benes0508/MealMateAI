This recipe for "Burst Cherry Tomato Pasta" features a simple sauce made by cooking cherry tomatoes, garlic, basil, and red pepper flakes in olive oil until they burst and thicken. Casarecce pasta is cooked al dente, then combined with the chunky tomato sauce and grated Parmesan. It's served with a drizzle of olive oil, fresh basil, and more Parmesan.
