This recipe details how to prepare **Ricotta-Stuffed Squash Blossoms** served with a simple tomato sauce. Squash blossoms are carefully filled with a mixture of ricotta, egg yolk, fresh mint, and Parmesan cheese. These stuffed blossoms are then lightly battered with flour, Parmesan, and seltzer, and deep-fried until golden and crispy. The dish is served alongside a quick tomato sauce made with garlic, red pepper flakes, and plum tomatoes.
