This recipe details how to make **Buttermilk Ice Cream**, a rich, custard-based (French-style) frozen dessert. It features the tangy notes of buttermilk, crème fraîche, and fresh lemon juice. The preparation involves cooking an egg yolk and sugar custard with cream, cooling it, then incorporating the buttermilk, crème fraîche, lemon juice, and salt before chilling and churning in an ice cream maker. The ice cream requires at least 4 hours of freezing to firm up and can be prepared up to 3 days ahead.
