This recipe details how to prepare **Roasted Squash with Mint and Toasted Pumpkin Seeds**. Acorn or butternut squash is cut into wedges, seasoned, and roasted until golden brown (approx. 30 mins at 425Â°F). Separately, shelled pumpkin seeds (pepitas) are toasted in olive oil until puffed. The roasted squash is then drizzled with balsamic vinegar and garnished with fresh torn mint leaves and the toasted pumpkin seeds.
