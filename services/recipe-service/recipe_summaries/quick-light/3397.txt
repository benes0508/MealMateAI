This recipe for **Balsamic Short Ribs** features beef short ribs browned and then slow-simmered for 2.5-3 hours with red onions, garlic, oregano, balsamic vinegar, chopped tomatoes, and beef stock until very tender. The pan juices are reduced to create a rich sauce, and the dish is served topped with a fresh basil gremolata (basil, lemon zest, garlic, olive oil).
