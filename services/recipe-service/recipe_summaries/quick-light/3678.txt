This recipe details how to make "The Boy Scouts Campfire Quesadilla" using shredded rotisserie chicken, Monterey Jack cheese, salt, pepper, and flour tortillas. The ingredients are assembled onto tortillas, folded, and then sealed in heavy-duty foil packets. These packets are cooked over medium, indirect heat on a campfire or grill for approximately 5-15 minutes until the cheese is melted, and served optionally with hot sauce.
