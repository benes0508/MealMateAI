This recipe is for **Squid, Lemon, and Zucchini Blossom Fritto Misto**, a crispy fried dish. It involves preparing a light batter with flour, club soda, and egg whites, and a homemade garlic-lemon aioli. Cleaned squid, thin lemon rounds, and zucchini blossoms are dipped in the chilled batter and deep-fried until golden and crisp. The fritto misto is then seasoned with salt and served hot with the prepared aioli and lemon wedges.
