A recipe for Pickled Pears, featuring a vibrant sweet, sour, and spicy pickling liquid made with rice vinegar, sugar, ginger, chiles, and peppercorns. Cubed pears (Asian, Anjou, or Granny Smith apples also work) are steeped in the warm brine and then chilled. This versatile condiment or side dish can be made up to a week in advance.
