This recipe for Bucatini with Walnut-Parsley Pesto involves toasting walnuts, then blending them with pickled Calabrian chiles, garlic, Parmesan, olive oil, and fresh parsley to create a vibrant pesto. Cooked bucatini or spaghetti is then tossed with the pesto and reserved pasta water until glossy. The dish is garnished with crushed walnuts and more parsley. The pesto can be made up to 2 days ahead.
