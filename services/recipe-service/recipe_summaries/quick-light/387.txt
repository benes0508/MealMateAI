This recipe details how to make **Roasted, Mashed Butternut Squash**. A medium butternut squash is halved, rubbed with olive oil, and roasted cut-side down at 400Â°F for 25 minutes, then flipped and cooked until tender (10-30 more minutes). After cooling, the seeds are discarded, the flesh is scooped out, coarsely mashed, and seasoned with salt. It can be refrigerated for 3-4 days.
