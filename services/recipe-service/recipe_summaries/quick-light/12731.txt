This recipe is for **Moroccan Slow-Cooked Lamb**. Lamb shoulder is seasoned with spices (cumin, coriander, fennel, cayenne, pepper), browned, then simmered with onion, tomato paste, chicken broth, garbanzo beans, apricots, plum tomatoes, cinnamon sticks, ginger, and lemon peel until the lamb is tender and the sauce thickens. It's garnished with fresh cilantro before serving.
