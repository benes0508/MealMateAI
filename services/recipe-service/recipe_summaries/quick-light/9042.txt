This recipe details how to prepare Mini Rack of Lamb with Nutty Beluga Lentils and Sautéed Garlic Spinach. The beluga lentils are cooked risotto-style with sautéed onion, carrot, celery, red wine, and chicken stock until tender. The frenched rack of lamb is seasoned with rosemary, salt, and pepper, roasted in the oven until medium-rare. Finally, spinach is quickly sautéed with garlic. The dish is assembled by serving the sliced lamb over a bed of lentils with a side of garlic spinach.
