This recipe describes a Spinach + Shiitake Grits Harvest Bowl. It features a creamy, grits-like base made from millet and cauliflower, flavored with spinach, shiitake mushrooms, and nutritional yeast for an umami profile. This quick-prep, plant-based meal can be cooked on the stovetop in 4-6 minutes or microwaved in 4 minutes, and is optionally served with sliced avocado.
