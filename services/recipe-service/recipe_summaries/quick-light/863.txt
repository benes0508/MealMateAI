This recipe details how to prepare "Trout Toast with Soft Scrambled Eggs." It involves butter-toasting sourdough bread, making creamy soft-scrambled eggs with crème fraîche, and then layering them on the toast. The dish is topped with flaked smoked trout, lemon zest and juice, black pepper, sliced scallions, and fresh dill. A simple side salad of arugula tossed with lemon juice and olive oil completes the meal.
