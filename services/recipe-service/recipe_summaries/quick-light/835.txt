This recipe details how to cook a large batch of rice (2 lbs) for future use. It involves boiling 18 cups of water with salt and vinegar, adding rinsed rice, then simmering for 10-20 minutes until al dente before straining. For storage, the cooked rice can be cooled and frozen in sealed bags for up to 4 months. Reheating options include microwaving, simmering in a saucepan with a splash of water, or adding directly to soup.
