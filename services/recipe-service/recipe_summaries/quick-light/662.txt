This recipe is for a refreshing **Cantaloupe with Sugar Snap Peas and Ricotta Salata** salad. It features thinly sliced melon (cantaloupe or honey kiss), sugar snap peas, and ricotta salata strips, tossed with fresh tarragon. The dish is finished with a drizzle of olive oil, lemon juice, flaky sea salt, and Aleppo-style pepper.
