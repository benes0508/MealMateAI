This recipe describes a fresh, panzanella-inspired tomato toast. It features a vibrant mix of chopped heirloom tomatoes, cucumber, and red onion, tossed in a flavorful oil infused with capers and basil. This mixture is served on garlic-rubbed toasted Italian bread and topped with crispy fried capers and basil leaves for added texture and flavor. It's a light, savory, and largely no-cook recipe perfect for a quick, fresh meal.
