This recipe describes how to make **Horseradish Cheese Grits with Confetti of Roasted Poblano Peppers and Red Onions**. It involves cooking stone-ground grits with milk, salt, and pepper until creamy. Simultaneously, poblano peppers are roasted and peeled, and red onions are sautéed until softened. Finally, cheese and horseradish are stirred into the grits, followed by gently folding in the roasted peppers and sautéed onions.
