Cold Sesame Noodles with Cucumber is a refreshing dish featuring chilled Chinese egg, cappellini, or pad thai style rice noodles. The noodles are tossed in a creamy, savory, and spicy sauce made from peanut butter, toasted sesame oil, rice vinegar, soy sauce, honey, ginger, garlic, red pepper flakes, and sriracha. It incorporates grated cucumber, scallions, and chopped peanuts, and is garnished with thinly sliced cucumber, fresh cilantro, and additional scallions and peanuts.
