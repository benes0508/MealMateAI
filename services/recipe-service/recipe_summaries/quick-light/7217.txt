This recipe for **Barbecued Chicken and Chickpea Quesadillas** features a filling of shredded cooked chicken tossed in barbecue sauce, and a chunky mashed chickpea mixture with garlic and scallions. These fillings are spread onto flour tortillas, topped with cilantro, smoked gouda, and Monterey Jack cheese, then folded and grilled until golden brown and the cheese is melted. Serve warm, cut into wedges.
