This recipe details how to make baked pasta shells with a savory sauce. It involves browning Italian sausage with garlic, sage, and red pepper flakes, then thickening it with half-and-half and Fontina/cheddar cheese. Al dente pasta shells and broccoli rabe are combined with this sausage mixture, baked until bubbling, then topped with more cheese and fresh sage before being broiled to perfection.
