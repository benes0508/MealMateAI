This recipe features quickly seared scallops served with a fragrant and flavorful spice oil. The aromatic oil is made by grinding and simmering cumin, paprika, red pepper flakes, and black peppercorns in olive oil, then stirring in garlic and lemon. This component can be prepared up to a week in advance. The seared scallops are garnished with a simple fresh herb salad, creating a vibrant and quick dish.
