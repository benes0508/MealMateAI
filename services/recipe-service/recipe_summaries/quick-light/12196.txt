This recipe is for a **Milk Chocolate Mousse with Cranberry and Candied-Orange Chutney**. It involves making a chocolate custard base with egg yolks, milk, and milk chocolate, flavored with dark rum. Whipped cream is folded into the cooled base before chilling for at least 4 hours. The dessert is assembled by layering the mousse with cranberry and candied-orange chutney, topped with whipped cream and chocolate shavings, then chilled again.
