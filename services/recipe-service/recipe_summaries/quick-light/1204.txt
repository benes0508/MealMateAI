"Spicy Tahini and Avocado Soba" is a quick and refreshing noodle dish. It features soba noodles tossed in a dressing made from tahini, red miso paste, rice vinegar, and sriracha. Fresh ingredients like jicama matchsticks, baby spinach, and sliced avocado are added, then topped with gomashio. The noodles are cooked, rinsed cold, and then combined with the dressing and vegetables.
