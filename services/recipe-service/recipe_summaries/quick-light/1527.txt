This recipe details how to make Ube (Purple Yam) Candies, a sweet treat utilizing roasted and riced sweet potatoes (purple or orange-fleshed) combined with sweetened condensed milk, nonfat dry milk, lime zest, and a pinch of salt. The mixture is cooked until jammy, then cooled, rolled into logs, coated in sugar, and cut into bite-sized pieces. These candies can be prepared up to two weeks in advance.
