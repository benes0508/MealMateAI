This recipe is for a simple and flavorful **Ginger-Scallion Ramen Noodle** dish. It features a unique sauce made by pouring hot oil over a mixture of fresh ginger, garlic, and scallions, which is then combined with soy sauce, rice vinegar, sesame oil, and a touch of sugar. Cooked ramen noodles are tossed with this savory and aromatic dressing, and it's typically served with toasted sesame seeds and chili oil. It's a quick, easy, and vegetarian-friendly option.
