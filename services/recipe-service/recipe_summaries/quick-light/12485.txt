This recipe is for **Grilled Zucchini Pizza**. It involves grilling pizza dough and zucchini separately, then assembling them on the grill. The dough is brushed with a garlic-infused olive oil, topped with grilled zucchini and mozzarella, then grilled again until the cheese is melted and the crust is golden. It's finished with fresh oregano and a drizzle of olive oil.
