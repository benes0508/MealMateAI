This recipe details how to make **Slow-Cooked Collard Greens in Olive Oil**. Fresh collard greens are simmered with garlic, red pepper flakes, olive oil, and water for 1 to 1.5 hours until very tender and dark green. A touch of apple cider vinegar and sugar are stirred in at the end for flavor balance, then served with an optional drizzle of more olive oil and vinegar.
