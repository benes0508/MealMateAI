This recipe prepares **Flattened Pork Chops with Greens and Mustard Pan Sauce**. Pork chops are pounded thin and pan-fried until browned. A quick pan sauce is made from Dijon mustard, honey, and vinegar, which is then used to coat mustard greens. The dish is garnished with quick-pickled shallots and mustard seeds for a tangy finish.
