This recipe is for **Salted Almond and Pistachio Bark**, a simple sweet treat. It involves melting **semisweet chocolate**, stirring in **vanilla** and half of the **roasted almonds and pistachios**. This mixture is then spread onto parchment paper, topped with the remaining nuts and **coarse sea salt**, and refrigerated until set (2-4 hours). Finally, the bark is broken into pieces for storage.
