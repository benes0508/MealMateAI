This recipe details how to prepare Fennel-Crusted Pork Chops with Potatoes and Shallots. Bone-in pork chops are coated in a rub of toasted fennel seeds, garlic, and smoked paprika, then seared and roasted in an ovenproof skillet alongside quartered Yukon Gold potatoes and shallots. The dish is finished with fresh parsley and red wine vinegar, served with the roasted vegetables and pan juices.
