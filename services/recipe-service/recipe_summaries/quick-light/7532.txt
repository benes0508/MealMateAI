This recipe is for "Eggplants in a North-South Sauce," featuring eggplant simmered in a flavorful tomato-based sauce. The dish is aromatic, incorporating a unique blend of whole spices including mustard, cumin, nigella, fennel, and asafetida, along with onion, garlic, and cayenne pepper, suggesting an Indian-inspired flavor profile. The eggplant is stir-fried initially then cooked down until tender in the sauce.
