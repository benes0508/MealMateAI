This recipe details how to make **Olive Oil-Roasted Leeks**. Leeks are halved, rinsed, and dried, then tossed with olive oil and salt. They are roasted cut-side down on a hot baking sheet at 300°F for about 1.5 hours until tender, then uncovered, flipped, and roasted at 400°F for 15-20 minutes until golden brown.
