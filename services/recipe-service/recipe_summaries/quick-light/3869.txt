This recipe describes how to make **Sautéed Greens with Toasted Walnuts**. It involves toasting walnuts, then sautéing thinly sliced onion, Swiss chard stems, garlic, and red pepper flakes in olive oil and butter. Swiss chard greens, beet greens, and vinegar are then added and cooked until tender. The dish is served topped with the toasted walnuts.
