This recipe for **Dark-Chocolate-Dipped Cherry Ice Cream Cones** involves filling sugar cones with cherry ice cream and freezing them. Separately, bittersweet chocolate (with oil) and white chocolate are melted. The frozen ice cream cones are then dipped into the bittersweet chocolate, rolled in toasted almonds, and drizzled with white chocolate, before being refrozen. They can be prepared a day in advance.
