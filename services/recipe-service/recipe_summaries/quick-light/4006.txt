This recipe details how to make Squid Ink Pasta with Shrimp, Nduja, and Tomato. It involves making a flavorful shrimp stock from heads/shells, then preparing a tomato sauce with nduja and sliced garlic. Chopped shrimp are added to the sauce, and squid ink linguini is cooked al dente and finished directly in the sauce with pasta water, lemon juice, and parsley for a rich, cohesive dish.
