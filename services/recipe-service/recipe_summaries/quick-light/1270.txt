This recipe is for a **One-Pot Spring Pasta with Smoked Salmon**. It features **linguine**, **asparagus**, **peas**, and **smoked salmon** in a creamy, lemony sauce made with **butter**, **lemon zest**, **lemon juice**, and **sour cream**. The pasta and vegetables are cooked together in one pot, then tossed with the sauce ingredients. It's finished with more smoked salmon, basil, and a dollop of sour cream.
