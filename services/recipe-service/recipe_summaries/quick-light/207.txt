This recipe details how to make **Green-Garlic-Rubbed Buttery Roast Chicken**. A whole chicken is seasoned and rubbed generously with a compound butter made from **green garlic, lemon zest, and unsalted butter**. The remaining green garlic stalks are tossed with olive oil and roasted alongside the chicken in a skillet or baking dish. The chicken is roasted at **325°F for 80-90 minutes** until the internal temperature reaches 155°F, then rested for 20-45 minutes before carving and serving with the roasted green garlic.
