This recipe details how to make Swiss Chard Lasagna with Ricotta and Mushroom. It involves preparing a homemade béchamel sauce, blanching and sautéing Swiss chard with onion and garlic, and sautéing crimini mushrooms. Lasagna noodles are cooked until al dente, then layered in a baking dish with béchamel, the chard mixture, mushrooms, ricotta, Fontina, and Parmesan cheeses. The lasagna is baked at 400°F (204°C) for 30 minutes covered, then 20-30 minutes uncovered until golden, followed by a 15-minute rest before serving.
