This recipe details how to prepare "3-Ingredient Charred Green Beans with Ricotta and Lemon." Green beans are charred on a grill or grill pan until crisp-tender. Simultaneously, whole milk ricotta is whipped with olive oil and salt until smooth and fluffy. The whipped ricotta is spread on a platter, topped with the charred green beans, then drizzled with olive oil, and seasoned with lemon zest, salt, and pepper, served with lemon wedges.
