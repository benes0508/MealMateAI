This recipe details how to make **Baked Apple, Raisin, and Brown Sugar Dumplings**. Golden Delicious apples are cored, pre-cooked slightly in the microwave, then filled with a mixture of raisins and brown sugar. Each apple is then encased in a flattened refrigerated biscuit, sealed, brushed with egg and cinnamon sugar, and baked until golden. The dish is served with a side of cinnamon whipped cream.
