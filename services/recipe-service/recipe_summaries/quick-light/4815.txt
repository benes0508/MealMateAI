This recipe details a Lamb Stir-Fry with Pomegranate and Yogurt. Thinly sliced lamb is marinated with cumin, coriander, paprika, garlic, and red wine vinegar. It's then stir-fried with red onion until browned and tender. The dish is served over rice, topped with a seasoned yogurt sauce, fresh pomegranate seeds, chopped pistachios, and a mix of fresh herbs.
