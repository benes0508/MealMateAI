This recipe for Crispy Eggplant Fritters with Smoked Mozzarella starts by baking eggplant until tender and dry. The cooled eggplant is then mixed with Parmesan, herbs, and breadcrumbs. Smoked mozzarella cubes are pressed into the center of each ball, which are then coated in egg and breadcrumbs, and pan-fried until golden and crispy.
