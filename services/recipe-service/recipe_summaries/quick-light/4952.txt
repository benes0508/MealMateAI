This recipe for **Pan-Seared Pork Blade Chop** involves marinating a thick pork chop with lemon slices, chopped garlic, olive oil, and rosemary for at least 8 hours. The chop is then pan-seared in a skillet, preferably cast iron, until browned and cooked to 135Â°F, with a halved head of garlic added during the last 10 minutes. After resting, it's served with lemon wedges, cooked garlic, and fresh rosemary.
