This recipe details how to prepare Pomegranate and Fennel Glazed Rack of Lamb. It involves searing lamb racks, then roasting them with sliced fennel and onion, all coated in a flavorful glaze of pomegranate molasses, fennel seeds, and fresh oregano. The dish is cooked until the lamb is medium-rare, rested, and then served over the roasted vegetables, garnished with fresh pomegranate seeds.
