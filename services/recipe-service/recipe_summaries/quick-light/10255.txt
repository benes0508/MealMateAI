This recipe for Scallops with Cauliflower, Dried Cherries, and Capers involves cooking cauliflower until tender, then wilting spinach. These vegetables are combined with shallot and cinnamon. Separately, sea scallops are seared until opaque. Finally, a brown butter sauce is made with shallot, dried tart cherries, and capers, then spooned over the scallops and vegetables. The vegetable mix can be prepared up to 2 hours ahead.
