This recipe details **Pan-Fried Sea Bass with Miso, Lemon, and Thyme-Glazed Roasties**. It involves roasting a mix of carrots, sweet potatoes, and parsnips coated in a miso, lemon, garlic, and coconut oil glaze with fresh thyme for 45 minutes. Concurrently, sea bass fillets are pan-fried until the skin is crisp, then served atop the roasted vegetables.
