This recipe details a Black Beans and Rice with Chicken and Apple Salsa dish. It features a rich black bean mixture, thickened by mashing some beans, cooked with bell pepper, onion, garlic, and spices (chili powder, coriander, cumin). This is served over rice, topped with shredded rotisserie chicken, and a refreshing salsa made from Granny Smith apple, cilantro, red onion, and lime juice.
