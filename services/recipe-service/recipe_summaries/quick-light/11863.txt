A quick and flavorful dish featuring mussels steamed in a savory sauce of tomato, dry white wine, and a distinctive anise-flavored liqueur (such as Pernod, Pastis, or Ouzo). Aromatics like onion, garlic, and parsley, along with anchovy fillets, enhance the rich flavor. It's finished with a drizzle of olive oil and fresh parsley, serving 4.
