This recipe features **spice-crusted carrots** that are first boiled and peeled, then pan-fried until deep brown with a flavorful spice mixture (sugar, mustard powder, paprika, cumin, coriander). These are served atop a **creamy harissa yogurt sauce** made with Greek yogurt, harissa paste, fresh thyme, and lemon zest. It's garnished with more herbs and lemon zest, and served with lemon wedges.
