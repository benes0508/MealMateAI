This recipe details how to make a **3-Ingredient Caramel Apple Tart** using frozen puff pastry, store-bought caramel sauce, and apples. The process involves thawing and preparing the puff pastry, tossing sliced apples with caramel and salt, arranging them on the pastry, and baking until golden brown. A final touch of thinned caramel glaze is brushed over the warm tart for serving.
