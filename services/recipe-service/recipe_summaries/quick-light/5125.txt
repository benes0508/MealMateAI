This recipe is for Black Bean Soup with Roasted Poblano Chiles. It involves roasting poblano chiles and toasting pumpkin seeds and a dried pasilla/ancho chile. An aromatic base of saut√©ed onion and garlic, blended with tomatoes and the toasted pasilla chile, is simmered with chicken broth to create the soup base. Black beans and roasted poblanos are then added. The soup is served topped with crumbled queso fresco or feta and toasted pumpkin seeds, with lime wedges on the side. It can be made up to two days in advance.
