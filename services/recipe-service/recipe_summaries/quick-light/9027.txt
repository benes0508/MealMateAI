This recipe is for a **Cheesy Chicken and Mushroom Lasagne** featuring shredded roast chicken, cremini mushrooms, and a rich white sauce (béchamel) made with milk, flour, and butter. It uses **no-boil lasagne noodles** and two types of cheese: **Parmigiano-Reggiano** mixed into the filling and **Gruyère** layered and sprinkled on top for a golden finish. The dish is assembled in an 8-inch square pan and baked covered then uncovered until golden.
