This recipe for **Celery Slaw with Seeds and Dates** creates a refreshing salad featuring thinly sliced celery, celery leaves, Persian cucumber ribbons, arugula, and chopped Medjool dates. It's dressed in a unique vinaigrette made with homemade pickled mustard seeds, shallot-infused oil, rice vinegar, soy sauce, sugar, and salt. The dish is topped with toasted sesame seeds and can have its pickled mustard seeds and vinaigrette prepared ahead of time.
