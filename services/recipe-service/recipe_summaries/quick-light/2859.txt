This recipe details "Spice-Rubbed Sustainable Fish Sliders." It involves pan-frying a white fish fillet seasoned with a homemade spice rub (coriander, cardamom, peppercorns, paprika, salt, garlic powder, oregano, chile). The sliders are assembled with the spiced fish, a slaw made from red cabbage, fennel, green apple, red onion, mint, and pepitas dressed with apple cider vinegar and olive oil, along with yogurt tartar sauce, tomato slices, and fresh slider rolls.
