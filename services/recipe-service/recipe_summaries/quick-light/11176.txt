This recipe details how to prepare **Roast Chicken with Spanish Paprika and Herb-Roasted Smashed Potatoes**. It involves butterflying and seasoning whole chickens with a paprika-salt mix and fresh thyme, then chilling them overnight. Baby potatoes are tossed with olive oil and paprika. Both are roasted simultaneously at 400°F until the chicken reaches 175°F and the potatoes are tender, then the potatoes are lightly smashed and tossed with fresh thyme. The chickens are carved and served with pan drippings.
