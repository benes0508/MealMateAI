This recipe details preparing a savory pot roast using boneless beef short ribs, seared then braised until fork-tender with a medley of vegetables (carrots, onions, celery, garlic), sherry, and chicken stock. Separately, carrots are roasted until tender, and Spanish onions are caramelized. The dish is served with the tender beef, roasted carrots, caramelized onions, and a rich, reduced pan sauce.
