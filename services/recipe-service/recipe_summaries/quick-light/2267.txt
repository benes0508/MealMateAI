This recipe is for **Paneer and Broccoli Masala**, an Indian-inspired curry. It features pan-fried paneer and broccoli florets simmered in a rich, creamy sauce made with coconut milk, tomato paste, and tamarind. Aromatic spices like turmeric, garam masala, cumin, coriander, ginger, garlic, and chiles provide depth, garnished with fresh cilantro and crispy curry leaves.
