This recipe for "Fettuccine with Walnuts, Zucchini Ribbons, and Pecorino Romano" features fettuccine tossed with thinly shaved zucchini ribbons, toasted walnuts, fresh basil, mint, and Pecorino Romano cheese. The sauce base is a crushed paste of garlic, anchovy fillets, and red pepper. The zucchini is pre-salted to remove excess moisture, and the cooked pasta is combined with the aromatic paste, olive oil, and some pasta water before mixing in the remaining ingredients.
