This recipe for Tomato and Cabbage Tabbouleh is a fresh, vibrant salad. It combines soaked bulgur with thinly sliced green cabbage, sweet onion, assorted tomatoes, and fresh mint. The salad is dressed with olive oil, lemon juice, and Aleppo pepper, then seasoned with salt. It can be prepared ahead by combining the dry ingredients, with the dressing added just before serving.
