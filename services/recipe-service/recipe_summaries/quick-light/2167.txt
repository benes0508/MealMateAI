This recipe for **Spiced Marinated Beets** involves roasting red beets until tender, then peeling and tearing them into bite-sized pieces. A flavorful oil infusion of various spices (coriander, mustard, fennel, caraway, cumin) and bay leaves is prepared, then poured over the beets along with red wine vinegar and salt. The dish is tossed to combine and allowed to cool, suitable for making ahead.
