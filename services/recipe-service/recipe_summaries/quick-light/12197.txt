This recipe is for a **Quince and Currant Chutney**, a sweet and tangy condiment. It combines quinces, currants, red onion, apple cider, apple cider vinegar, and brown sugar, spiced with fresh ginger, orange peel, star anise, black mustard seeds, red pepper, and cardamom. All ingredients are simmered together for about 45 minutes until thickened, and it can be prepared up to 4 days in advance.
