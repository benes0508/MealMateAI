This recipe for Roasted Radicchio with Anchovy Vinaigrette, Preserved Lemon, and Breadcrumbs involves broiling radicchio until tender and slightly charred. It's then tossed with a robust vinaigrette made from anchovies, grated cheese, lemon juice, and olive oil. The dish is finished with homemade toasted breadcrumbs, delicate strips of preserved lemon peel, and cheese shavings, offering a blend of bitter, savory, and tangy flavors.
