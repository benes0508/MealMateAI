This recipe details how to make "Pasta with Arugula Purée and Cherry-Tomato Sauce." It involves preparing a vibrant arugula and pine nut purée, simmering cherry tomatoes with garlic for a simple sauce, and then tossing cooked fettuccine with the arugula purée before topping it with the tomato sauce. Grated Pecorino Romano is used in the purée and as an accompaniment.
