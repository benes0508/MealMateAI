This recipe for Pasta with Rock Shrimp, Chile, and Lemon features pasta tossed with delicate rock shrimp, fresh red chile, and lemon zest. A unique bonito-infused butter forms the base of the savory, glossy sauce, which is brightened with fresh lemon juice and tarragon. The dish is finished with thinly sliced scallions and a sprinkle of finely ground dried shrimp for an added layer of umami.
