This recipe is for Sesame-Crusted Salmon with stir-fried vegetables (carrots, bell peppers, shiitake mushrooms, bok choy, scallions) and a soy sauce drizzle, served with rice. Salmon fillets are coated in sesame seeds and red pepper flakes, then pan-fried until cooked. The vegetables are stir-fried separately and served alongside the salmon and rice.
