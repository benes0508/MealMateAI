This recipe is for **Lobster Pasta with Herbed Cream Sauce**. It involves boiling live lobsters, extracting the meat, and using the shells and bodies to create a rich, herbed cream sauce with tomato paste, white wine, garlic, tarragon, thyme, and parsley. The cooked lobster meat and roe are then added to the strained sauce, which is served tossed with fettuccine or linguine.
