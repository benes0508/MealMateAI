This recipe for **Skillet Bruschetta** features **crusty bread pan-toasted** in olive oil. The topping is a **savory mix of cannellini beans** (partially mashed for creaminess), **garlic, and red pepper flakes**, cooked until slightly blistered. Fresh **kale or mustard greens are wilted** into the bean mixture with **broth and lemon juice** for brightness. Everything is **prepared in one skillet** and served **open-faced** with an extra drizzle of oil.
