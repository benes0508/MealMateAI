This recipe for **Eggplant Dip** involves roasting halved eggplants until tender, then draining and puréeing the pulp. Separately, spring onion, garlic, heirloom tomatoes, and hot paprika are sautéed. The puréed eggplant is then combined with plain lowfat yogurt and the sautéed vegetables, seasoned with salt and pepper, chilled, and garnished with fresh basil before serving with raw vegetables.
