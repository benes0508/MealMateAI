This grilled pizza features a unique blend of flavors, topped with savory **sausage**, sweet **fresh figs**, melting **Fontina cheese**, peppery **arugula**, and sharp **red onion**. It's dressed with a distinctive **pomegranate cumin dressing** and finished with tangy **crumbled goat cheese**. The dough is grilled until golden, then topped and grilled again until cooked through.
