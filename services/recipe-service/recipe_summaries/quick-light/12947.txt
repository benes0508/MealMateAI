This recipe for "Baked Figs in Lemon Syrup" involves baking fresh purple figs with butter and a sprinkle of sugar until tender. A separate lemon syrup is prepared using water, sugar, lemon juice, and blanched lemon zest. After the figs are baked, the syrup is reduced in the same pan, then fresh lemon segments are added before pouring the warm, reduced syrup over the figs. It's served warm or at room temperature.
