This recipe for Zucchini "Noodles" with Eggplant and Tomatoes creates a light, fresh, and savory main dish. It features spiralized zucchini and yellow squash (salted to remove excess moisture) tossed in a lemon-honey-basil dressing with pan-fried eggplant, cherry tomatoes, and garlic. The dish is then garnished with fresh mozzarella, olives, and basil.
