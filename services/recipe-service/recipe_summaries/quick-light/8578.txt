This recipe details **Grilled Cheese and Tomato Stacks**, featuring grilled halloumi cheese marinated in olive oil, fresh mint, and cumin. It involves grilling mini pitas and the marinated halloumi, then stacking them with fresh tomato slices. The dish is finished with a drizzle of olive oil and a sprinkle of sea salt. The halloumi can be marinated up to 2 days in advance.
