This recipe is for a **Creamy Potato and Leek Gratin**, a rich baked dish. It involves gently cooking **leeks in butter**, while thinly sliced **potatoes are simmered in a mixture of heavy cream and milk with thyme** until the liquid thickens. The cooked leeks are then combined with the potatoes and baked in a gratin dish until tender. It can be prepared up to two days in advance.
