This recipe for a Black Sesame Noodle Bowl features soba, udon, or somen noodles tossed in a rich sauce made from toasted black sesame seeds, soy sauce, rice vinegar, brown sugar, wasabi, and oil. It's served cold with toppings like avocado, grated 12-minute eggs, radishes, fresh greens, and scallions, drizzled with kecap manis. The dish can be prepared ahead and is suitable for a quick and flavorful meal.
