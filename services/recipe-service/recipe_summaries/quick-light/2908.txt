This recipe features **Quick Poached Salmon with Speedy Rat-Atat-A-Touille**. It involves first stir-frying diced red onion, zucchini, and eggplant with thyme in coconut oil, then simmering them with tomato paste, balsamic vinegar, and water to create the ratatouille. Simultaneously, skinless salmon fillets are poached in boiling water until cooked through. The dish is served by topping the ratatouille with the poached salmon.
