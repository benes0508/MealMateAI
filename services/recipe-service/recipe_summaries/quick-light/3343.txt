Sautéed Pears With Bacon and Mustard Dressing is a unique side dish or appetizer featuring tender-crisp Bosc pears sautéed in bacon fat, combined with crispy bacon bits. It's finished with a tangy white wine vinegar and whole grain mustard dressing, crunchy roasted walnuts, and fresh chives. This dish offers a delightful balance of sweet, savory, and tangy flavors with varied textures, and can be prepared up to 3 hours in advance.
