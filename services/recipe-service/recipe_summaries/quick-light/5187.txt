This recipe prepares **Cider-Brined Pork Roast with Potatoes and Onions**. A pork loin is brined for at least 8 hours (up to 2 days) in a mixture of unfiltered apple cider, brown sugar, salt, bay leaves, coriander seeds, and black peppercorns. After brining and searing, the pork is roasted with Yukon Gold potatoes and red/yellow onions until done, then rested and sliced into chops.
