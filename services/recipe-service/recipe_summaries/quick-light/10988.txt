This recipe is for **Baked Smoked Ham with Mango**, featuring a bone-in smoked ham baked with quartered onions and mango cubes, moistened with mango or peach nectar. It's cooked covered at 350°F, then uncovered at 400°F until browned, basted with pan juices. The ham is served sliced, garnished with the cooked fruit and onions, and accompanied by a reduced pan sauce.
