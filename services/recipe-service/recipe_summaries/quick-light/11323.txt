This recipe for **Pork Pot Stickers** details how to make savory dumplings. It features a filling of ground pork, napa cabbage, ginger, carrots, scallions, and garlic, seasoned with soy sauce, sesame oil, and egg. The mixture is enclosed in gyoza wrappers, pleated to seal, then cooked in a skillet by browning the bottoms, steaming with water until tender, and crisping the bottoms before serving.
